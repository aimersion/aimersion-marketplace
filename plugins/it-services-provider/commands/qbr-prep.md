Prepare a comprehensive Quarterly Business Review (QBR) for an MSP client.

Gather:
- Client company name
- Quarter and year being reviewed
- Ticket volume and resolution metrics
- SLA performance data
- Projects completed and in progress
- Budget information
- Strategic initiatives
- Known pain points or upcoming changes

Produce a QBR package:

## 1. Executive Summary
- Quarter highlights and achievements
- Overall service health score
- Key metrics vs. SLA targets
- Strategic recommendations

## 2. Service Desk Performance
- Ticket volume by category (trend over 4 quarters)
- Average response time vs. SLA
- Average resolution time vs. SLA
- First-call resolution rate
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Top 5 recurring issue types

## 3. Infrastructure Health
- System uptime (target: 99.9%)
- Backup success rate
- Patch compliance percentage
- Security incident summary
- Endpoint health status

## 4. Projects and Initiatives
For each project:
- Status (Complete / In Progress / Planned)
- Budget vs. actual
- Key outcomes delivered
- Timeline adherence

## 5. Technology Roadmap
- Recommended upgrades and refresh cycles
- End-of-life hardware and software timeline
- Strategic technology recommendations
- Cloud migration opportunities
- Estimated investment for each initiative

## 6. Budget Review
- Actual spend vs. budget
- Cost per user/device trends
- Cost optimization opportunities
- Proposed budget for next quarter

## 7. Recommendations
- Top 3-5 strategic recommendations ranked by business impact
- Each with: description, business case, estimated investment, timeline

Save as a presentation-ready document.
