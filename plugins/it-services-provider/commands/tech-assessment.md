Conduct a technology assessment for a prospective MSP client or new engagement.

Gather:
- Company name, industry, and size
- Current IT infrastructure overview
- Number of users, locations, and remote workers
- Current IT support arrangement (in-house, other MSP, break-fix)
- Known pain points and priorities
- Budget expectations
- Compliance requirements

Produce a technology assessment report:

## 1. Current State Assessment
- Infrastructure inventory summary
- Software and licensing review
- Network architecture evaluation
- Security posture overview
- Backup and disaster recovery status
- IT staffing and support structure

## 2. Gap Analysis
For each area, compare current state vs. best practices:
- What's working well
- What needs improvement
- Critical gaps that pose immediate risk

## 3. Risk Register
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Priority | Recommended Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|----------|----------------------|
| [Risk description] | High/Med/Low | High/Med/Low | P1-P4 | [Mitigation] |

## 4. Managed Services Proposal
Recommend a managed services package:
- Tier recommendation (Basic / Standard / Premium)
- Included services per tier
- Per-user or per-device pricing model
- Onboarding timeline and process
- SLA commitments

## 5. Quick Wins
Identify 3-5 improvements implementable in the first 30 days that demonstrate immediate value.

## 6. Technology Roadmap (12-month)
- Month 1-3: Stabilization and critical fixes
- Month 4-6: Optimization and modernization
- Month 7-12: Strategic improvements
