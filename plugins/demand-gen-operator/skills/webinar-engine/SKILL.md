# Webinar Engine

Execute webinars as a full demand generation motion — from concept to closed pipeline.

## Why Webinars Convert

Webinars are the highest-converting demand gen format because:
1. Registration requires active commitment (self-qualification)
2. They create a real-time relationship with your brand
3. They generate content assets across pre, during, and post phases
4. Attendance and engagement patterns identify your most interested prospects

## Pre-Webinar: 2-4 Weeks Before

### Topic Selection

Score potential topics on:
- ICP pain alignment (does this solve a real, immediate pain?)
- Timeliness (is there a news hook, trend, or seasonal angle?)
- Uniqueness (can you say something people haven't already heard?)
- Sales utility (will this content help close deals in progress?)

**Best-performing webinar formats:**
- "The [X] Framework for [Outcome]" — teach a proprietary methodology
- "Why [Conventional Approach] Is Failing" — challenge the status quo
- "[Customer] Did X and Got [Specific Result]" — customer story format
- "State of [Category] — Live Research" — original data or survey
- "Live Panel: [Debatable Question]" — 3-4 practitioners with different views

### Landing Page Copy

```
Headline: [Specific outcome or insight — not just the webinar title]
Subheadline: [Who this is for + the 3 things they'll learn]

"In this 45-minute session, you'll leave with:"
- [Specific takeaway 1 — actionable]
- [Specific takeaway 2 — actionable]
- [Specific takeaway 3 — actionable]

Host: [Name, Title] — [1-sentence credibility statement, not a bio]
Guest (if applicable): [Name, Title, Company] — [why they matter]

[Social proof: past attendee quote OR registration count OR company logos]

Date | Time | Duration
[Registration CTA button]
```

### 3-Email Promotion Sequence

**Email 1 — Invite (3 weeks out):**
Subject: [Topic] — [Date]
Body: Pain-first opener → what they'll learn → who's presenting → register CTA

**Email 2 — Social proof + urgency (1 week out):**
Subject: [X] people registered for [topic] — join them
Body: Who else is attending (company types, not names) → preview of content → CTA

**Email 3 — Day-before reminder:**
Subject: Tomorrow at [time]: [Topic] — your link inside
Body: One-sentence value reminder → logistics (time, link, duration) → add to calendar

## During the Webinar

### Run-of-Show

```
:00-:05  Welcome, housekeeping, agenda preview
:05-:10  Speaker intro (brief — credibility, not biography)
:10-:35  Core content (framework, story, data — their real value)
:35-:45  Case study or live example with specific numbers
:45-:55  Q&A (prepare 3 backup questions in case of silence)
:55:1:00 Wrap up, CTA, what's next
```

### Engagement Prompts (use these live)

Prepare these before the webinar:
- **Opening poll:** "Quick poll before we dive in — are you currently experiencing [pain]?"
- **Mid-session check-in:** "Drop in the chat: what's your #1 challenge with [topic]?"
- **Decision-reveal moment:** "Hands up (or type yes) if this describes your current situation"
- **Conversion poll:** "Would it be useful to see how this applies to your specific company?"

### Sales Signal Tracking

Flag during the session:
- Attendees who answer "yes" to conversion poll
- Anyone asking questions that signal active evaluation ("Do you integrate with X?")
- Attendees still present at minute 50+ (high intent signal)
- Anyone who drops company-specific context in chat

## Post-Webinar: 48 Hours to 2 Weeks

### Follow-Up Sequence

**Email 1 — Same day or next morning:**
Subject: Recording + top 3 takeaways from [topic]
Body: Thank them → 3 bullet takeaways → recording link → what's the obvious next step

**Email 2 — 3 days later:**
Subject: The part of [webinar] that most people miss
Body: Go deeper on one key insight → related content → soft CTA

**Email 3 — 1 week later (non-attendees only):**
Subject: You missed [topic] — here's what happened
Body: Quick summary of best moments → recording link → CTA

### Sales Handoff by Engagement Tier

**High-intent (attended full session, asked questions, answered conversion poll):**
"[Name], your question about [specific thing] showed you're dealing with [specific situation]. Based on that, [relevant capability] would be directly applicable. Would 20 minutes be useful?"

**Medium-intent (attended, engaged minimally):**
"[Name], glad you joined [topic]. The part about [most relevant section] seems particularly applicable given [company context]. Happy to share more detail if useful."

**Low-intent (registered, didn't attend):**
Send recording only. No direct sales outreach unless they engage with follow-up emails.

## Post-Webinar Content Assets

Every webinar should produce:
1. Full recording (gated or public — decide based on content sensitivity)
2. 3-5 short clip scripts (for LinkedIn video posts)
3. Blog post from the session content
4. 3-email follow-up sequence (above)
5. LinkedIn posts (5-7 pieces from the content)
6. Sales one-pager (objections raised and answered)
