# Quality Improvement

Track, analyze, and improve clinical outcomes, patient satisfaction, and operational performance for home health agencies using CMS quality frameworks.

## Key Quality Metrics

### Clinical Outcomes (OASIS-based)
- Improvement in ambulation/locomotion
- Improvement in bathing
- Improvement in bed transferring
- Improvement in management of oral medications
- Improvement in pain interfering with activity
- Improvement in dyspnea
- Acute care hospitalization rate
- Emergency department use without hospitalization

### Process Measures
- Timely initiation of care (within 48 hours of referral/SOC)
- Drug education on all medications provided to patient/caregiver
- Influenza and pneumococcal immunization assessment
- Depression screening conducted
- Falls risk assessment performed

### Patient Satisfaction (HHCAHPS)
- Overall rating of care
- Willingness to recommend agency
- Communication quality (how well caregivers communicated)
- Specific care domains (professional care, communication, scheduling)

## Quality Analysis Process

1. **Data gathering** — Help the user organize their quality data (OASIS outcomes, HHCAHPS scores, hospitalization data, complaint logs)
2. **Benchmarking** — Compare against national averages, state averages, and agency historical trends
3. **Root cause analysis** — For underperforming metrics, guide a structured root cause analysis (5 Whys, fishbone diagram)
4. **Action planning** — Generate specific, measurable improvement actions with owners and deadlines
5. **Monitoring** — Create a dashboard template to track progress monthly

## QAPI Program Support

Help build and maintain a CMS-compliant QAPI program:
- QAPI plan template with all required elements
- Performance Improvement Project (PIP) documentation
- Governing body quality report templates
- Staff education plans tied to quality findings
- Corrective action plan templates for identified deficiencies

## Star Rating Optimization

Help understand and improve CMS Home Health Star Ratings:
- Explain which metrics drive star ratings
- Identify the 2–3 highest-impact metrics to target for rating improvement
- Create focused improvement plans for those metrics
- Track progress toward rating improvement goals
