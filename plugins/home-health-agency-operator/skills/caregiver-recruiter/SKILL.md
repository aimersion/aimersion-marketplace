# Caregiver Recruiter

Attract, screen, interview, and onboard home health caregivers in a competitive labor market.

## Job Post Creation

Generate compelling job listings tailored by role:

### Role Templates
- **Registered Nurse (RN)** — Home health visit nurse, case manager, clinical supervisor
- **Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN/LVN)** — Skilled nursing visits under RN supervision
- **Home Health Aide (HHA/CNA)** — Personal care, ADL assistance, light housekeeping
- **Physical/Occupational/Speech Therapist** — Rehabilitation visit staff
- **Per Diem/PRN** — Flexible coverage staff

### Job Post Structure
1. **Attention-grabbing headline** — Lead with what makes this agency different (flexibility, culture, pay transparency)
2. **Day-in-the-life description** — Show what the job actually looks like (not just a list of duties)
3. **Requirements** — Separate must-haves from nice-to-haves
4. **Compensation transparency** — Pay range, benefits, mileage, bonus structure
5. **Growth opportunities** — Career advancement, training, certification support
6. **Application process** — Simple, mobile-friendly, quick turnaround commitment

### Platform Optimization
- Indeed, ZipRecruiter, myCNAjobs, NurseFly — platform-specific formatting
- Social media recruiting posts (Facebook groups, LinkedIn, Instagram stories)
- Employee referral program announcement templates

## Interview Guides

Create structured interview scorecards by role:
- Behavioral questions tailored to home health scenarios
- Clinical competency assessment questions (role-specific)
- Situational judgment scenarios (patient emergencies, family conflicts, boundary issues)
- Cultural fit and reliability indicators
- Red flag identification guide

## Onboarding Checklists

Generate comprehensive onboarding workflows:
- Pre-employment: background check, drug screen, license verification, TB test, physical, CPR certification
- Orientation: agency policies, HIPAA training, infection control, emergency procedures, documentation systems
- Field training: ride-along requirements, competency checkoffs, supervisory visit schedule
- 30-60-90 day check-in templates

## Retention Strategies

Help reduce turnover with:
- Competitive compensation benchmarking
- Recognition program ideas
- Flexible scheduling frameworks
- Career ladder development
- Exit interview templates to identify root causes
