Use the caregiver-recruiter skill to create recruitment materials for a home health caregiver position.

1. Ask the user for:
   - Role type (or use $ARGUMENTS if provided): RN, LPN, HHA/CNA, PT, OT, SLP
   - Employment type: full-time, part-time, per diem/PRN
   - Service area and typical caseload
   - What makes their agency a great place to work (differentiators)
   - Compensation range (if they want to include it)

2. Generate a complete recruitment package:
   - Job posting optimized for Indeed/ZipRecruiter (with a social media short version)
   - Structured interview scorecard with 8–10 questions (behavioral + clinical + situational)
   - Pre-employment checklist (background check, credentials, health screenings)
   - First-week onboarding schedule template

3. Save all materials to organized, downloadable documents.

Keep job posts authentic and compelling — lead with culture and flexibility, not just requirements.
