# /interview-kit

Generate everything your interview team needs to evaluate candidates consistently. See the **structured-interviewing** and **interview-prep** skills for the full frameworks.

## Usage

```
/interview-kit $ARGUMENTS
```

## What I Need From You

- **Role**: What position are you hiring for?
- **Level**: Junior, Mid, Senior, etc.
- **Key competencies**: What matters most? (I'll suggest defaults if you're not sure)
- **Interview team**: Who will be interviewing? (optional — I'll assign by competency)
- **Format**: In-person, video, or mixed?

## Output

A complete interview kit including:
- Competency map with weights
- Interview loop design (who interviews for what, how long each round)
- Question bank by round (behavioral + situational + technical)
- Scoring rubrics with behavioral anchors at each level (1-4 scale)
- Printable scorecard for each interviewer
- Debrief template with decision framework
- Candidate communication templates (scheduling, rejection, next steps)

## Tips

1. **Send the kit to interviewers 24+ hours before the interview** — they need time to prepare
2. **Hold a 15-minute calibration** before the first candidate — align on the bar
3. **Score immediately after each interview** — memory fades fast
