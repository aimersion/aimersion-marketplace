# /convert-contractor

Convert a contractor to a full-time employee with proper compensation conversion, compliance paperwork, and a transition plan. See the **contractor-conversion** skill for the full framework.

## Usage

```
/convert-contractor $ARGUMENTS
```

## What I Need From You

- **Contractor name**: Who are we converting?
- **Current rate**: Hourly/daily/monthly rate and how long they've been with you
- **Role and team**: What role will they fill as an employee?
- **Location**: Where are they based? (affects benefits and compliance)
- **Staffing agency involved?**: Is there a conversion fee to account for?

## Output

A complete conversion package including:
- Compensation conversion calculator (contractor rate → FTE salary with math explained)
- Side-by-side comparison showing total value (salary + benefits vs. contractor rate)
- Legal/compliance checklist (end contractor agreement, new W-4, I-9, benefits enrollment)
- Transition onboarding plan (they know the company but need the employee experience)
- Offer letter draft with conversion-specific language
- Talking points for the conversion conversation
