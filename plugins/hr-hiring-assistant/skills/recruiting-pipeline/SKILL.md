# Recruiting Pipeline

Manage your recruiting pipeline with the rigor of a sales funnel. At a 50-person company without a dedicated recruiter, the hiring manager IS the pipeline owner — this skill gives you the visibility and frameworks to not let candidates fall through the cracks.

## Pipeline Stages

| Stage | Description | Key Actions | Healthy Timeline |
|-------|-------------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Sourced** | Identified and reached out | Personalized outreach, job board posts | — |
| **Applied** | Submitted application | Resume review, initial screen | Review within 48 hours |
| **Phone Screen** | Recruiter/HM phone screen | Evaluate basic fit, logistics, motivation | Within 1 week of application |
| **Interview** | Full interview loop | Structured evaluation across competencies | Within 2 weeks of screen |
| **Debrief** | Team decision meeting | Calibrate feedback, make hire/no-hire call | Within 2 days of final interview |
| **Offer** | Extending the offer | Comp package, verbal offer, written letter | Within 2 days of decision |
| **Negotiation** | Candidate negotiating | Handle counter-offers, competing offers | 3-5 business days |
| **Accepted** | Offer accepted | Transition to onboarding | Immediate |
| **Declined** | Offer declined | Feedback collection, pipeline backup | Log reason |

## Metrics Dashboard

Track these metrics for every open role:

### Velocity Metrics
| Metric | Definition | Healthy Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Time to fill** | Days from req opening to offer accepted | 30-45 days (IC), 45-60 days (senior/exec) |
| **Time per stage** | Average days in each pipeline stage | Screen <5 days, Interview <10 days, Offer <3 days |
| **Pipeline age** | Average age of active candidates | Candidates shouldn't sit >2 weeks without action |

### Conversion Metrics
| Stage Transition | Healthy Rate | Red Flag |
|-----------------|-------------|----------|
| Applied → Screen | 20-30% | <10% (JD attracting wrong candidates) |
| Screen → Interview | 40-60% | <25% (screen criteria too loose or too tight) |
| Interview → Offer | 20-40% | <15% (interview bar unclear) |
| Offer → Accepted | 70-90% | <60% (comp not competitive or slow process) |

## Pipeline Health Diagnosis

When something is wrong with your pipeline, the metrics tell you where:

**Problem: Not enough candidates**
→ Check: Source mix, JD quality, salary competitiveness, employer brand
→ Fix: Expand sourcing channels, improve JD, add salary range, post on niche boards

**Problem: Candidates dropping off at screen**
→ Check: Time from application to screen (>5 days = they've moved on), screen experience
→ Fix: Speed up response time, automate scheduling, improve candidate communication

**Problem: Low interview-to-offer rate**
→ Check: Interview calibration, are interviewers aligned on the bar?
→ Fix: Hold calibration session, review scorecards for consistency, clarify must-haves vs. nice-to-haves

**Problem: Offers being declined**
→ Check: Comp competitiveness, process speed, candidate experience
→ Fix: Benchmark comp, accelerate timeline, improve communication throughout

**Problem: Ghosting (candidates disappear)**
→ Check: Communication frequency, process length, candidate experience
→ Fix: Touch candidates at least weekly, shorten the process, send clear next-step emails after every interaction

## Candidate Communication Cadence

Don't ghost candidates — it kills your employer brand and referral pipeline.

| Touchpoint | Timing | Channel | Template |
|-----------|--------|---------|----------|
| Application received | Same day (auto) | Email | "Thanks for applying — here's our timeline" |
| Moving to screen | Within 48 hours | Email | "We'd like to set up a call" |
| Post-screen update | Same day | Email | Decision + next steps or kind rejection |
| Interview scheduling | Within 2 days of screen | Email | Logistics, who they'll meet, how to prepare |
| Post-interview update | Within 3 days | Email/call | Decision or timeline for decision |
| Offer | Within 2 days of decision | Call + email | Verbal first, written to follow |
| Rejection | Within 3 days of decision | Email | Specific, kind, encourages future applications |
