# Interview Prep

Prepare interviewers to run effective, structured interviews for a specific role. This generates the actual materials interviewers will use — question sheets, scoring rubrics, and debrief templates.

## Interview Kit Components

### 1. Role Competency Map
Define 4-6 competencies, weighted by importance:

| Competency | Weight | Assessed In | Interviewer |
|-----------|--------|-------------|-------------|
| [Core technical skill] | High | Technical round | [Name] |
| [Problem-solving] | High | Behavioral round | [Name] |
| [Communication] | Medium | All rounds | All |
| [Collaboration] | Medium | Behavioral round | [Name] |
| [Leadership/Ownership] | Medium | HM deep-dive | [Name] |
| [Culture-add] | Low | Culture round | [Name] |

### 2. Question Bank by Round

For each interview round, provide:
- 2-3 primary questions per competency
- Follow-up probes for each
- What good, mediocre, and poor answers look like

**Behavioral Question Format (STAR):**
- Situation: "Tell me about a time when..."
- Follow-up: "What specifically did you do?" (Action)
- Follow-up: "What was the result?" (Result)
- Follow-up: "What would you do differently?" (Reflection)

**Situational Question Format:**
- "Imagine you're in [realistic scenario]. Walk me through your approach."
- Follow-up: "What if [complication]?"
- Follow-up: "How would you prioritize?"

### 3. Scoring Rubric (1-4 per competency)

| Score | Label | Criteria |
|-------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Does Not Meet | [Specific to this competency and role] |
| 2 | Partially Meets | [Specific to this competency and role] |
| 3 | Meets | [Specific to this competency and role] |
| 4 | Exceeds | [Specific to this competency and role] |

### 4. Interviewer Prep Sheet
For each interviewer, provide:
- Their assigned competencies
- Their specific questions (with probes)
- The rubric for their section
- Time allocation guidance
- Reminder: take notes on specific observations, not just scores

### 5. Debrief Template
```markdown
## Interview Debrief: [Candidate Name] — [Role]
**Date:** [Date] | **Participants:** [Names]

### Round-Robin (2 min each, no interruptions)
| Interviewer | Round | Overall Score | Top Observation |
|-------------|-------|---------------|-----------------|
| [Name] | [Round] | [1-4] | [Key insight] |

### Competency Summary
| Competency | [Interviewer 1] | [Interviewer 2] | [Interviewer 3] | Avg |
|-----------|----------------|----------------|----------------|-----|

### Discussion Points
- [Score discrepancies to resolve]
- [Concerns to address]

### Decision
[ ] Strong Hire [ ] Hire [ ] No Hire [ ] Strong No Hire

### Next Steps
- [ ] [Action item]
```

## Output

Produce a complete, printable interview kit with all sections above, customized for the specific role and your company context. If you know the interviewers' names, assign them to rounds. If not, leave as "[Interviewer Name]" for the hiring manager to fill in.
