# Marketing Helper

Create effective marketing campaigns for independent auto repair shops to attract new customers and increase revenue from existing ones.

## Seasonal Service Specials

### Spring (March-May)
- **A/C Service Special**: "Get your A/C checked before summer — A/C performance inspection $49.95 (reg $89)"
- **Alignment Check**: "Post-pothole season alignment check — $39.95"
- **Spring Multi-Point Inspection**: "Free 25-point vehicle inspection with any paid service"

### Summer (June-August)
- **Road Trip Ready Package**: "Oil change + tire rotation + multi-point inspection — $XX.XX"
- **Cooling System Special**: "Coolant flush and fill — $XX.XX (prevent overheating)"
- **Battery Test**: "Free battery test — heat kills batteries faster than cold!"

### Fall (September-November)
- **Winter Prep Package**: "Battery + tires + brakes + fluids check — $XX.XX"
- **Brake Special**: "Brake inspection — free with any service. Pad replacement from $XX/axle"
- **Heater Check**: "Heater core and thermostat inspection — $XX.XX"

### Winter (December-February)
- **New Year Special**: "15% off any repair in January — start the year right"
- **Emergency Battery Deal**: "Battery replacement — installed same day from $XX.XX"
- **Referral Bonus**: "Refer a friend this winter — you both get $25 off"

## New Customer Acquisition

### Offers That Convert
- **First visit discount**: 10-15% off first repair (capped at $50-75)
- **Free inspection**: Comprehensive vehicle inspection — creates the opportunity to find needed work
- **Oil change loss leader**: Below-cost oil change to get first-time customers in the door
- **Diagnostic fee waiver**: Free diagnostic with approved repair

### Local Marketing Channels
1. **Google Business Profile**: Optimize listing, post weekly, respond to every review
2. **Google Local Service Ads**: Pay-per-lead for "auto repair near me" searches
3. **Facebook local ads**: Target by zip code, vehicle ownership, and interests
4. **Nextdoor**: Business page and recommendations — hyperlocal trust
5. **Partnerships**: Insurance agents, tow companies, real estate welcome packages, local businesses
6. **Direct mail**: New movers list, radius targeting around the shop
7. **Signage**: Street-visible signage and A-frame boards with current specials

### Customer Referral Program
Structure: "Refer a friend who completes a repair — you both receive $25 off your next service."
- Track referrals in your POS/shop management system
- Send thank-you note to referrer
- Follow up with referred customer for review request

## Content Marketing

### Social Media Post Ideas
- Before/after repair photos (with customer permission)
- "Mechanic tip of the week" — simple maintenance advice
- Meet the team / technician spotlight
- Customer testimonials and delivery photos
- Quick educational videos: "What does your check engine light mean?"
- Shop milestones and community involvement

### Email Newsletter Topics (Monthly)
- Seasonal maintenance tips relevant to current weather
- Special offers for the month
- Customer spotlight or success story
- One educational article (tire care, fluid explanation, etc.)
- Shop news or team updates

## Pricing Your Specials

When creating promotional pricing:
- Never discount below cost — loss leaders are strategic, not habit
- Bundle services for perceived value rather than deep discounts
- Set expiration dates to create urgency
- Track redemption rates to measure effectiveness
- Limit to one major promotion at a time to avoid brand dilution
