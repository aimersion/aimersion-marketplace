# Review Manager

Manage online reputation through professional review responses and proactive review generation strategies.

## Responding to Reviews

### Positive Reviews (4-5 Stars)

Response framework:
1. **Thank by name** — personalize every response
2. **Reference specifics** — mention the service they received or detail they praised
3. **Reinforce value** — subtly highlight what makes the shop special
4. **Invite return** — welcome them back for future needs

Keep it genuine and brief (2-4 sentences). Avoid sounding robotic or template-driven.

### Negative Reviews (1-2 Stars)

Response framework:
1. **Acknowledge and empathize** — never be defensive, even if the complaint is unfair
2. **Apologize for the experience** — "I'm sorry your experience didn't meet expectations"
3. **Take it offline** — provide a direct phone number or email
4. **Show commitment** — "We take this seriously and want to make it right"

Critical rules:
- Never argue publicly — move the conversation offline
- Never reveal private details about the repair or customer
- Never blame the customer, even indirectly
- Respond within 24 hours — speed shows you care
- Keep responses professional even if the review is hostile

### Mixed Reviews (3 Stars)

Response framework:
1. Thank them for the feedback
2. Acknowledge what went well
3. Address the concern directly
4. Offer to discuss further offline

### Fake or Unfair Reviews

If you suspect a fake review:
- Respond professionally as if it's real (public audience is watching)
- Note factually if you have no record of their visit: "We couldn't find your visit in our records — please contact us directly so we can investigate"
- Flag/report through the platform's process
- Never accuse the reviewer of lying publicly

## Review Generation Strategy

### When to Ask

Best timing for review requests:
- 3-7 days after service completion (enough time to evaluate, fresh enough to remember)
- After customer expresses satisfaction in person or via text
- After resolving a concern successfully
- After completing a significant or complex repair

### How to Ask

**In-person** (highest conversion): "We really appreciate your business. If you had a good experience, a Google review would mean a lot to a small shop like ours."

**Text message** (next best): Send a direct link to your Google review page. One tap should open the review form.

**Email**: Include review link in follow-up/thank-you email. Keep the ask simple and non-pushy.

**QR code**: Display in waiting area and on invoices linking directly to Google review page.

### Review Volume Targets

- **Minimum**: 2-3 new reviews per week to maintain momentum
- **Good**: 5-8 new reviews per week
- **Excellent**: 10+ new reviews per week (creates a strong competitive moat)
- **Minimum overall rating target**: 4.5+ stars on Google

## Platform Priority

1. **Google Business Profile** — highest impact on local search visibility
2. **Yelp** — important for auto repair discovery
3. **Facebook** — social proof and community
4. **BBB** — trust signal for older demographics
5. **Nextdoor** — hyperlocal community trust
