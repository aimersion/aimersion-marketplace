description: Create monthly service specials and promotions
argument-hint: [month or season]
---

Create a complete set of service specials and promotional materials for the specified month or season.

Ask the user for:
- Target month or season
- Any specific services they want to promote
- Budget range for advertising (if known)
- Their shop's current specials (to avoid repetition)

Generate a complete promotional package using the marketing-helper skill:

1. **Service specials** (3-4 offers): Seasonal and evergreen specials with pricing guidelines, expiration dates, and terms
2. **Social media posts**: 4-6 ready-to-post social media messages promoting the specials across Facebook and Instagram
3. **Email/text blast**: A customer-facing email or text message announcing the monthly specials
4. **In-shop signage text**: Counter card or poster copy for the waiting area
5. **Google Business Profile post**: A Google-optimized post to improve local search visibility

Make each piece ready to use — the shop owner should be able to copy, customize pricing, and publish. Include suggested pricing ranges but note that the owner should adjust to their local market and cost structure.
