Conduct a comprehensive pricing strategy review for the user's SaaS product.

Gather context:
- Current pricing page or plan structure
- Target customer segments
- Key value metrics
- Competitor pricing (if known)
- Current conversion rates by plan
- Revenue distribution across plans

Deliver a pricing strategy report:

## 1. Current Pricing Assessment
Evaluate the existing pricing against best practices:
- Value metric alignment (does pricing scale with value delivered?)
- Plan differentiation (clear upgrade triggers?)
- Pricing psychology (anchoring, decoy effects)
- Packaging clarity

## 2. Competitive Positioning
Map pricing relative to competitors on a value-price matrix. Identify positioning as: premium, competitive, penetration, or freemium.

## 3. Plan Architecture Recommendations
Design optimized plan tiers:
- Feature allocation per tier using the "good-better-best" framework
- Usage limits and overage strategies
- Add-on opportunities
- Enterprise/custom tier triggers

## 4. Pricing Model Analysis
Evaluate fit for:
- Per-seat pricing: predictable but limits adoption
- Usage-based: aligns with value but unpredictable revenue
- Hybrid: combines predictability with growth alignment
- Flat-rate: simple but leaves money on table

## 5. Pricing Page Optimization
Recommend specific pricing page improvements:
- Plan naming and positioning
- Feature comparison table design
- CTA optimization
- Social proof placement
- FAQ strategy

## 6. Migration Strategy
If changing pricing, provide a rollout plan:
- Grandfathering policy
- Communication templates
- Phased rollout approach
- Risk mitigation

Save the analysis as a downloadable document.
