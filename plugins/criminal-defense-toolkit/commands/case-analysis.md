# Case Analysis Command

Perform a comprehensive criminal case analysis to identify defense opportunities and evaluate trial versus plea considerations.

## Process

1. **Gather Case Information**
   - Ask the attorney for the specific criminal charges and their statutory citations
   - Request a factual summary of the allegations and police reports
   - Ask what evidence the prosecution has disclosed or indicated it will present
   - Determine what discovery has been received and what remains outstanding
   - Identify the defendant's prior record, if any
   - Ask about any physical evidence, witness statements, or recorded communications

2. **Statutory Element Analysis**
   - Break down each charge into its required statutory elements
   - For each element, assess what proof the prosecution must provide
   - Identify which elements are contested vs. likely stipulated
   - Analyze the mens rea (mental state) requirement and how it's proven
   - Flag elements that are factually or legally vulnerable

3. **Identify Prosecution Weaknesses**
   - Examine witness credibility issues (inconsistent statements, bias, criminal history)
   - Assess evidence quality (circumstantial vs. direct, scientific reliability, chain of custody)
   - Identify procedural violations or constitutional defects
   - Flag missing evidence or gaps in the narrative
   - Evaluate witness availability and reliability

4. **Map Potential Defenses**
   - Affirmative defenses (self-defense, duress, necessity, entrapment)
   - Constitutional challenges (4th Amendment search/seizure, 5th Amendment Miranda/statements, 6th Amendment confrontation)
   - Procedural defenses (jurisdictional issues, speedy trial, prosecutorial misconduct)
   - Identity and misidentification defenses
   - Factual challenges to elements

5. **Assess Trial vs. Plea Risk**
   - Estimate trial strength and conviction probability
   - Evaluate any plea offers received or anticipated
   - Calculate sentencing exposure if convicted at trial
   - Compare to sentencing exposure under any plea offer
   - Flag collateral consequences (immigration, employment, licensing)

## Output Format

Deliver a structured case analysis memo with:
- Case caption and charges
- Element-by-element breakdown of each charge
- Prosecution weaknesses and vulnerabilities
- Viable defense strategies ranked by viability
- Recommended motions and discovery requests
- Trial risk assessment (percentage likelihood of conviction)
- Plea comparison if applicable
- Next investigative steps
