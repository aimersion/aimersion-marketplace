# Discovery Tracker Command

Systematically organize, categorize, and track all discovery in a criminal case to ensure no evidence is overlooked and Brady/Giglio obligations are identified.

## Process

1. **Collect Case and Discovery Information**
   - Ask for the case name, docket number, and charges
   - Request a list of all discovery received to date
   - Ask what discovery has been requested but not yet received
   - Determine if discovery is ongoing or complete
   - Identify the prosecuting agency and discovery counsel

2. **Categorize Evidence**
   - **Physical Evidence**: weapons, drugs, documents, contraband, trace evidence
   - **Documentary Evidence**: police reports, witness statements, lab reports, communications
   - **Digital Evidence**: surveillance footage, body camera recordings, cell phone records, GPS data
   - **Testimonial Evidence**: witness lists, expert disclosures, statement summaries
   - **Exculpatory Material**: Brady material, Giglio material, impeachment evidence

3. **Create Discovery Log**
   - Assign unique identifier to each discovery item
   - Record date received and source
   - Note category and brief description
   - Flag legal significance (Brady, Giglio, impeachment)
   - Track physical location/custody
   - Mark authentication and expert review status

4. **Identify Missing Discovery**
   - List items requested but not received
   - Prioritize by legal importance
   - Draft follow-up request letters if needed
   - Note statutory deadlines for prosecution disclosure

5. **Flag Brady and Giglio Material**
   - Identify evidence of prosecutor misconduct or dishonesty
   - Flag evidence of police bias, dishonesty, or disciplinary history
   - Note evidence of informant credibility issues
   - Mark anything that could reasonably tend to exonerate or impeach

6. **Chain of Custody Review**
   - Verify proper handling and custody for physical evidence
   - Identify any gaps or irregularities
   - Flag evidence that may be contaminated or lost
   - Note expert testing status

## Output Format

Deliver a discovery organization package with:
- Master discovery log (spreadsheet format) with all categorized items
- Missing discovery checklist with follow-up requests
- Brady/Giglio material summary
- Chain of custody flags and concerns
- Evidence organization recommendations
- Authentication status for key items
- Timeline of discovery requests and responses
