Use the review-generator skill to help the practice systematically collect more patient reviews.

1. Ask the user about:
   - Their current review situation (how many Google reviews, current rating)
   - Which platforms matter most to them (Google, Facebook, Yelp, Healthgrades)
   - Their preferred ask method (in-office, text, email)
   - Their practice name and doctor name(s) for personalization

2. Generate a complete review generation kit:
   - 3 text message templates (for different "golden moments")
   - 2 email templates (post-visit and milestone celebration)
   - 1 front desk verbal script
   - 1 printed card design text (with QR code placeholder)
   - 3 review response templates (5-star, 4-star, negative)

3. Create a recommended timing strategy — when to ask which patients.

4. Include HIPAA compliance reminders for responding to reviews publicly.

5. Save all templates to a single organized document the user can share with their team.

All templates must be ethical — no incentivized reviews, no pressure tactics, no fake review strategies.
