# Referral Program Builder

Design, launch, and manage a patient referral program that turns satisfied chiropractic patients into a reliable growth engine.

## Program Design

### Structure Options

Help the user choose the right model:

1. **Thank-You Model** — Referring patient receives a small gift (gift card, branded wellness item, free add-on service like massage or posture scan) after the referred patient completes their first visit. Simple and HIPAA-compliant.

2. **Mutual Benefit** — Both the referrer and the new patient receive something (e.g., referrer gets a wellness credit, new patient gets a complimentary posture assessment). Creates a win-win the referrer feels good sharing.

3. **Tiered Rewards** — Escalating rewards for multiple referrals (1 referral = small gift, 3 = larger gift, 5 = VIP wellness package). Gamifies the process and rewards top advocates.

4. **Community Model** — Each referral triggers a donation to a local charity chosen by the practice. Appeals to altruistic patients and builds community goodwill.

### Reward Ideas (Compliant)

- Branded merchandise (water bottles, foam rollers, resistance bands)
- Gift cards to local businesses (restaurant, coffee shop — supports community ties)
- Complimentary add-on services (posture scan, ergonomic assessment, massage)
- Wellness product bundles (supplements, pillows, ice/heat packs)
- Priority booking or VIP scheduling perks

Important: Never offer discounts on chiropractic services as referral rewards in states where fee-splitting or patient inducement laws apply. Always recommend the user verify compliance with their state board.

## Launch Materials

Generate these deliverables:

1. **Referral cards** — Physical or digital cards patients can share (include practice name, offer details, unique tracking code or QR)
2. **In-office signage** — Poster or counter display text explaining the program
3. **Email announcement** — Launch email to existing patients introducing the program
4. **Social media posts** — Announcement posts for each platform
5. **Front desk script** — What staff says when handing out referral cards or mentioning the program
6. **Text message template** — Quick reminder to active patients about the referral program

## Tracking System

Recommend a simple tracking approach:

- Spreadsheet template with columns: Referrer Name, Referred Patient, Date Referred, First Visit Date, Reward Sent, Notes
- Suggest practice management software integrations if the user has one
- Monthly review cadence: who referred, conversion rate, reward fulfillment status

## Optimization

After launch, help analyze and improve:
- Which patients refer the most? (Identify and nurture "super-referrers")
- What messaging drives the most referrals?
- Is the reward compelling enough? Too generous?
- Where in the patient journey is the best time to introduce the program?
