# Marketing Generator Skill

Create seasonal campaigns, life event targeted messaging, and multi-channel marketing materials to drive new business and cross-sell opportunities.

## Campaign Planning Framework

Each marketing campaign should address:
- **Audience**: Target demographic and psychographics
- **Trigger**: Seasonal timing or life event trigger
- **Message**: Core value proposition and emotional hook
- **Channels**: Direct mail, email, social media, community, digital
- **Timeline**: Campaign launch and duration
- **Call-to-Action**: Specific next step requested (call, quote, review, etc.)
- **Success Metrics**: Expected response rate and conversion targets

## Life Event Marketing Strategies

### Marriage/Engagement Targeting

**Message Framework:**
"Congratulations on your engagement! As you build your life together, let's make sure you're protected."

**Email Sequence:**
1. Congratulatory welcome message with coverage checklist
2. 3 days later: Spousal coverage and joint liability discussion
3. 7 days later: Estate planning and beneficiary review offer
4. 14 days later: Bundling/discount opportunities for newlywed couples

**Direct Mail:**
- Oversized postcard to new marriage license recipients (public records)
- "Newly Married? Let's Talk Coverage" headline
- Offer free joint coverage review call
- Include response card and QR code

**Social Media (Instagram/Facebook):**
- Engagement photo posts congratulating new couples
- Carousel posts on coverage considerations for married couples
- Video testimonial from recently married couple
- User-generated content: customers sharing their engagement story

### New Baby Targeting

**Message Framework:**
"Your family just grew—your protection should too. Let's talk coverage for your new addition."

**Email Sequence:**
1. Congratulatory message with life insurance fact sheet
2. 5 days later: Life insurance needs calculator for new parents
3. 10 days later: Disability coverage importance for childcare costs
4. 15 days later: 529 education savings coordination offer

**Direct Mail:**
- Greeting card style mailer to new birth records
- "New Baby on the Way? Build a Strong Protection Foundation"
- Attach baby safety checklist with insurance planning integrated
- Follow-up postcard 2 weeks later if no response

**Social Media:**
- Video content: "Your Life Insurance Needs Just Changed" explainer
- Instagram Stories: New parent protection tips
- Testimonial video from young families
- Educational carousel: Life insurance needs by family size

### Home Purchase Targeting

**Message Framework:**
"Your new home is the biggest investment you'll make. Let's make sure it's fully protected."

**Direct Mail:**
- Yellow Door branded mailer to new home purchasers (public records)
- "Welcome to Your New Home" headline with neighborhood map
- Immediate offer for bundled quote (auto + home)
- Include new homeowner coverage checklist

**Email Sequence:**
1. Welcome message with new homeowner coverage guide
2. 3 days later: Home insurance limits and replacement cost discussion
3. 7 days later: Auto bundling opportunity and savings
4. 14 days later: Umbrella coverage and liability exposure

**Digital Campaign:**
- Targeted ads to recent home buyers (real estate platform data)
- Landing page: "New Home? New Coverage Needs"
- Video: Tour of home with insurance coverage discussion integrated
- Chatbot: New homeowner coverage assistant

### Business Launch Targeting

**Message Framework:**
"Starting a business? Don't overlook the insurance foundation that protects your dream."

**Email to Database of Business Owners:**
- Subject: "New Business Started? Your Insurance Checklist"
- Content: General liability, property, workers' comp, professional liability overview
- Call-to-action: Free business insurance review

**LinkedIn Campaign:**
- Sponsored content: "Business Launch Checklist: Insurance Essentials"
- Target: Business owners under 5 years in operation
- Video: Types of business insurance explained in 2 minutes
- Article: Common insurance gaps for new businesses

**Local Business Network:**
- Speaking opportunity: "Insurance Fundamentals for New Business Owners"
- Table at chamber of commerce event
- Sponsorship of small business accelerator program

### Retirement Planning Targeting

**Message Framework:**
"You've worked hard to build your nest egg. Let's review how to protect it."

**Email to Customers 55+:**
- Subject: "Pre-Retirement Coverage Review: Protect Your Legacy"
- Content: Life insurance, disability, liability, estate planning considerations
- Offer: Complimentary retirement-focused coverage review call

**Direct Mail:**
- Oversized mailer to age 55+ demographic
- "Retirement Planning Includes Insurance Planning"

**Digital Campaign:**
