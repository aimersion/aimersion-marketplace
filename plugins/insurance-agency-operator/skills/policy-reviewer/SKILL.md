# Policy Reviewer Skill

Analyze client insurance coverage across all lines of business and identify gaps, overlaps, and renewal opportunities.

## Coverage Analysis Framework

Start by gathering the client's current coverage details across all lines:
- **Auto Insurance**: liability limits, collision/comprehensive deductibles, uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage
- **Home Insurance**: dwelling limit, personal property coverage, liability limit, deductible, replacement cost vs. actual cash value
- **Umbrella/Excess Liability**: limits, underlying coverage requirements, exclusions
- **Life Insurance**: type (term/whole/universal), face amount, beneficiaries, riders
- **Disability Insurance**: individual and group, elimination period, benefit period, definition of disability
- **Business Insurance**: general liability, professional liability, property, workers' compensation, business interruption

## Gap Identification Process

1. **Exposure Assessment**: Review client's assets, income, liabilities, and lifestyle factors
2. **Limit Comparison**: Compare current limits against industry benchmarks and client's specific exposure
3. **Coverage Type Review**: Verify appropriate coverage types for client's situation (replacement cost vs. ACV, etc.)
4. **Exclusion Analysis**: Identify critical exclusions and endorsements needed
5. **Multi-line Coordination**: Check for gaps where coverage from one policy should feed another

## Gap Categories and Recommendations

**Common Coverage Gaps:**
- Underinsured liability on auto or home (falling short of asset protection need)
- No umbrella policy when net worth exceeds underlying limit exposure
- Insufficient replacement cost coverage for home value
- Missing valuable endorsements (scheduled property, home business, etc.)
- Life insurance insufficient for family income replacement
- No disability insurance for income replacement
- Business exposures unprotected or inadequately insured

## Renewal Recommendation Process

1. **Competitive Analysis**: Compare current rates and terms against 2-3 comparable quotes
2. **Loss History Review**: Examine claims frequency and severity
3. **Coverage Updates**: Recommend adjustments based on life changes, new assets, or exposure changes
4. **Bundling Opportunities**: Identify multi-policy discount potential
5. **Rider Review**: Assess all riders/endorsements for continued appropriateness

## Documentation Template

For each client analysis, provide:
- Current coverage summary by line
- Gap identification with risk assessment
- Recommended coverage improvements with rationale
- Renewal strategy with competitive positioning
- Implementation action items and timeline

## Key Metrics to Track

- Premium-to-limit ratios by coverage line
- Bundling discount capture rate
- Gap identification accuracy
- Client adoption rate of recommendations
- Renewal retention success
