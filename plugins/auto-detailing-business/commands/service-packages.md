Help the user create, update, or optimize their detailing service packages and pricing.

Ask the user for:
- Current service offerings (if updating existing packages)
- Target market (budget, mid-range, premium, or luxury)
- Business model (mobile, shop-based, or both)
- Local market conditions (city, competition level)

Generate a complete service menu using the package-builder skill:

1. **Tiered packages** (3-4 tiers from basic to ultimate): Name, description, included services, estimated duration, and pricing by vehicle size category
2. **Add-on services menu**: 8-10 upsell options with pricing and selling triggers
3. **Vehicle size pricing matrix**: Base multipliers by vehicle category
4. **Upsell strategy**: Suggested upsell scripts for each package tier
5. **Competitive positioning analysis**: How the pricing compares to typical market ranges

Format the output as a professional service menu that could be used on a website, printed, or shared with customers. Also provide the internal cost analysis showing target margins per service.
