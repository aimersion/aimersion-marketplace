Optimize the daily appointment schedule for maximum revenue and efficiency.

Ask the user for:
- Date being planned
- Current appointments booked (with service types and vehicle info)
- Available resources (number of detailers, bays, or mobile units)
- Any constraints (travel time for mobile, equipment limitations)

Generate an optimized daily schedule using the booking-optimizer skill:

1. **Optimized timeline**: Appointments ordered with appropriate buffer times between services
2. **Revenue projection**: Expected revenue for the day based on booked services
3. **Gap analysis**: Identify any unfilled time slots and suggest how to fill them (walk-ins, express services, maintenance tasks)
4. **Resource allocation**: If multiple detailers, assign based on skill level and service complexity
5. **Preparation checklist**: Supplies and chemicals needed for the day's specific services

If the user has open slots, suggest outreach strategies (text recent customers, post social media flash deals, contact membership holders).

For mobile detailing, optimize the route order to minimize drive time between locations.
