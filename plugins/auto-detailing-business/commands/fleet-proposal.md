Create a professional fleet detailing proposal for the target company.

Ask the user for:
- Target company name and type of fleet
- Approximate number of vehicles
- Vehicle types (sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, etc.)
- Desired service frequency
- On-site or off-site service preference
- Any special requirements or concerns

Generate a complete fleet proposal using the fleet-account-manager skill:

1. **Cover letter**: Professional introduction tailored to the prospect's business
2. **Service description**: Detailed scope of work by vehicle type and service tier
3. **Pricing table**: Per-vehicle pricing with volume discounts, monthly and annual totals
4. **Scheduling plan**: Proposed service schedule that minimizes disruption to the client's operations
5. **Quality assurance**: Inspection process, photo documentation, satisfaction guarantee
6. **Terms and conditions**: Payment terms, contract options, insurance coverage
7. **Complimentary demo offer**: Offer to detail 1-2 vehicles free as a quality demonstration

Format as a professional business proposal that could be emailed or printed. Include all pricing so the prospect can make a decision without additional back-and-forth.
