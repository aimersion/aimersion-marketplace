# Package Builder

Design profitable, compelling service packages that make it easy for customers to choose and easy to upsell.

## Package Tier Strategy

Structure offerings into 3-4 tiers following "Good / Better / Best / Ultimate" naming:

### Tier 1: Basic / Maintenance Wash
- Exterior hand wash and dry
- Wheel and tire cleaning
- Window cleaning (exterior)
- Tire dressing
- **Duration**: 30-45 minutes
- **Price range**: $35-75 (sedan), $45-95 (SUV/truck)

### Tier 2: Standard / Full Detail
- Everything in Basic, plus:
- Interior vacuum and wipe-down
- Dashboard and console dressing
- Interior window cleaning
- Door jamb cleaning
- Air freshener
- **Duration**: 1.5-2.5 hours
- **Price range**: $125-200 (sedan), $150-250 (SUV/truck)

### Tier 3: Premium / Complete Detail
- Everything in Standard, plus:
- Clay bar treatment
- One-step polish
- Sealant or spray wax
- Leather conditioning
- Deep carpet and upholstery cleaning
- Engine bay cleaning
- **Duration**: 4-6 hours
- **Price range**: $250-400 (sedan), $300-500 (SUV/truck)

### Tier 4: Ultimate / Showroom
- Everything in Premium, plus:
- Multi-step paint correction
- Ceramic coating or premium sealant
- Headlight restoration
- Trim restoration
- Full interior steam cleaning
- **Duration**: 8-12+ hours (may be multi-day)
- **Price range**: $500-1500+ (varies greatly by condition and coating choice)

## Pricing Strategy

### Cost-Based Pricing
Calculate your cost floor:
- **Labor**: Your hourly cost (wage + taxes + benefits) × estimated hours
- **Products**: Chemical and supply cost per service (typically $5-25 per vehicle)
- **Overhead**: Rent, insurance, equipment amortization, vehicle costs (mobile)
- **Profit margin target**: Aim for 40-60% gross margin on services

### Market-Based Pricing
- Research 5-10 local competitors' published prices
- Position yourself: budget, mid-market, or premium
- Premium positioning requires: better results, better communication, better experience
- Never compete on price alone — compete on value and quality

### Vehicle Size Pricing
Use vehicle size categories rather than quoting every model:

| Category | Examples | Price Multiplier |
|----------|---------|-----------------|
| Small/Compact | Civic, Corolla, Mazda3 | 1.0x (base) |
| Midsize Sedan | Camry, Accord, Model 3 | 1.0-1.1x |
| Full-size Sedan/Wagon | Avalon, E-Class | 1.1-1.2x |
| Compact SUV | RAV4, CR-V, Tucson | 1.15-1.25x |
| Mid-size SUV | Highlander, Pilot, Explorer | 1.2-1.35x |
| Full-size SUV | Suburban, Expedition | 1.35-1.5x |
