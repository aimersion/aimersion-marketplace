# Fleet Account Manager

Win and manage commercial fleet detailing accounts with professional proposals, competitive volume pricing, and reliable recurring service schedules.

## Fleet Account Types

### Dealerships
- New car prep (PDI detailing)
- Used car reconditioning
- Service loaner maintenance
- Showroom display vehicles
- Volume: 10-100+ vehicles/month
- Pricing: Typically lower margin, high volume

### Corporate Fleets
- Employee vehicles, company cars
- Executive vehicles (higher standards)
- Sales team vehicles
- Volume: 5-50 vehicles/month
- Pricing: Mid-range, consistent revenue

### Rental Car Companies
- Turn cleaning between rentals (express)
- Deep clean on rotation
- Volume: High (20-100+/week)
- Pricing: Low per-unit, very high volume

### Ride-Share / Taxi Fleets
- Interior-focused cleaning
- Odor elimination
- Quick turnaround required
- Volume: 10-50/week
- Pricing: Budget, must be fast

### Commercial Vehicles
- Trucks, vans, work vehicles
- Branding/wrap maintenance
- Monthly or quarterly deep clean
- Volume: 5-30/month
- Pricing: Premium due to size

## Proposal Structure

### 1. Executive Summary
- Who you are and what you offer
- Understanding of their fleet needs
- Proposed solution overview

### 2. Service Description
- Detail each service tier offered to the fleet
- Specific scope of work per vehicle type
- Quality standards and inspection process

### 3. Pricing
- Per-vehicle pricing by service level
- Volume discount tiers (10+, 25+, 50+ vehicles/month)
- Contract term options (month-to-month, 6-month, 12-month)
- Payment terms (Net 15 or Net 30 for fleet accounts)

### 4. Schedule & Logistics
- Service frequency (daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)
- On-site vs. pickup/delivery
- Turnaround time guarantees
- Emergency/rush service availability

### 5. Quality Assurance
- Multi-point inspection checklist
- Before/after photo documentation
- Customer satisfaction follow-up process
- Issue resolution policy

## Volume Pricing Guidelines

| Monthly Volume | Discount from Retail |
|---------------|---------------------|
| 1-9 vehicles | 0% (retail pricing) |
| 10-24 vehicles | 15-20% off retail |
| 25-49 vehicles | 20-30% off retail |
| 50-99 vehicles | 30-40% off retail |
| 100+ vehicles | Custom negotiated |

**Minimum margin rule**: Never go below 25% gross margin, even on high-volume accounts. Volume doesn't help if you lose money.
