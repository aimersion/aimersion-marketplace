# Social Content

Create engaging social media content that showcases detailing expertise, builds trust, and drives bookings.

## Content Categories

### 1. Before/After Showcases (Highest Engagement)

Structure:
- **Before photo/video**: Show the condition honestly — dirt, swirls, stains
- **Process shots**: Key transformation moments (polishing, steaming, coating)
- **After reveal**: Dramatic final result with good lighting
- **Caption**: Brief description of services performed, time invested, customer reaction

Caption template:
"🔄 TRANSFORMATION TUESDAY: This {Year} {Make} {Model} came to us with [condition]. After [X] hours of [services performed], the results speak for themselves. Swipe to see the before → after! 👉
📞 Book your transformation: [Contact info]
#{BusinessName} #DetailingTransformation #BeforeAndAfter #{City}Detailing"

### 2. Educational Tips (Builds Authority)
- "3 things you're doing wrong when washing your car"
- "Why automatic car washes damage your paint"
- "How to maintain your ceramic coating between details"
- "The truth about waterless wash products"
- "How often should you really wax your car?"

### 3. Product Spotlights
- Highlight products used during details
- Explain why professional products differ from consumer brands
- Quick demo videos showing product performance
- Honest reviews build credibility — don't oversell

### 4. Behind-the-Scenes
- Setup process for a detail
- Satisfying cleaning videos (carpet extraction, steam cleaning)
- Workshop/bay tour
- Product organization and storage
- Equipment maintenance

### 5. Customer Testimonials
- Screenshot positive reviews (with permission) and add visual branding
- Customer delivery moments
- "What our customers say" compilation posts
- Video testimonials (most powerful)

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Instagram (Primary Platform for Detailing)
- **Feed**: Polished before/after, final results with consistent editing style
- **Stories**: Real-time work in progress, polls, Q&As, daily life
- **Reels**: Satisfying process videos (30-60 seconds), tips, reveals
- **Posting frequency**: 4-5 feed posts/week, daily Stories, 2-3 Reels/week
- **Best times**: 11 AM-1 PM and 7-9 PM local time

### TikTok (Growth Opportunity)
- **Satisfying videos**: Paint correction reveals, carpet cleaning, steam cleaning
- **Trending sounds**: Use popular audio with detail content
- **Raw and authentic**: Less polished than Instagram — people want real
- **Posting frequency**: 1-2 per day for growth phase

### Facebook
- **Community focused**: Local car groups, community events
- **Reviews and recommendations**: Encourage check-ins and reviews
- **Event promotion**: Detail specials, grand openings, seasonal events
- **Posting frequency**: 3-4 per week

### Google Business Profile
- **Weekly photo updates**: Recent work, shop/setup photos
- **Posts about specials**: Time-limited offers with CTA
- **Respond to every review**: Positive and negative

## Content Production Tips

### Photography
- Clean background or consistent backdrop
- Natural light or consistent studio lighting
- Shoot at the same angles for before/after consistency
- Include close-up detail shots (swirl marks, water beading, interior textures)
- Use a tripod for consistent framing

### Video
- Horizontal for YouTube, vertical for Reels/TikTok/Stories
- Capture the process: clay bar, polishing, coating application
- Time-lapse full details for impact
- Include audio: polisher sounds, extraction sounds — they're satisfying
- Keep final edits under 60 seconds for social (longer versions for YouTube)
