# Customer Retention

Build recurring revenue and customer loyalty through membership programs, maintenance plans, and retention strategies.

## Membership Program Design

### Tier Structure

**Tier 1: Maintenance Member** ($49-79/month)
- 1 express wash per month
- 10% off all additional services
- Priority booking
- Member-only seasonal specials

**Tier 2: Premium Member** ($99-149/month)
- 1 standard detail per month (or 2 express washes)
- 15% off all additional services
- Priority booking + same-day availability
- Free minor touch-ups between visits
- Annual ceramic spray sealant included

**Tier 3: VIP / Unlimited** ($199-299/month)
- Unlimited express washes
- 1 premium detail per quarter
- 20% off all add-on services
- First access to new services
- Complimentary loaner car freshener refills
- Annual paint correction touch-up

### Membership Economics

Pricing formula for profitability:
- **Monthly price** should exceed: (Service cost × expected visits) + overhead allocation
- **Target margin**: 30-50% gross margin on membership services
- **Break-even point**: Member becomes profitable by month 2-3
- **Retention target**: 70%+ of members retained at 12 months

### Membership Selling Points
- "Save up to 30% compared to individual bookings"
- "Never worry about scheduling — priority access guaranteed"
- "Your car always looks its best without thinking about it"
- "Lock in today's prices — no rate increases for active members"

## Maintenance Plan Recommendations

### Post-Ceramic Coating Maintenance
- **Monthly**: Express maintenance wash (pH-neutral, coating-safe)
- **Quarterly**: Decontamination wash + ceramic boost spray
- **Annually**: Full inspection + coating top-up or reapplication assessment

### Regular Customer Maintenance
- **Monthly**: Express wash — maintain appearance
- **Quarterly**: Standard detail — deeper interior + exterior care
- **Bi-annually**: Premium detail — full restoration and protection
- **Annually**: Ultimate detail — complete paint correction and protection reset

## Loyalty Rewards

### Point System
- 1 point per $1 spent
- 100 points = $10 reward
- Bonus points for referrals (50 points per referred customer who books)
- Birthday bonus (double points during birthday month)
- Anniversary bonus (extra reward on membership anniversary)

### Punch Card Alternative (Simpler)
- Buy 5 express washes, get 1 free
- Buy 3 details, get an express wash free
- Easy to implement without technology

## Retention Communication Cadence

| Timing | Message |
|--------|---------|
| Post-service (Day 1) | Thank you + before/after photos |
| Day 7 | Review request |
| Day 21 | Maintenance tip related to their service |
| Day 45 | "Time for a refresh?" reminder |
| Day 60 | Special offer if no rebooking |
| Day 90 | Win-back offer (15-20% off) |
