Skills

8 specialized skills

Each skill knows exactly when to activate and what to do. Describe what you need in plain language.

🔍
Job Finder
Searches LinkedIn, Indeed, Greenhouse, Lever, Wellfound, and niche boards. Scores every role across fit, company quality, comp, and winability. Returns prioritized A-list, Strong, and Reach tiers.
find me jobs search for roles job hunt
📄
Resume Tailor
Keyword gap analysis against the JD, ATS scoring, complete rewrite of bullets using Action + Result + Impact formula. Quantifies everything. Shows exactly what changed and why.
tailor my resume optimize for this job
✍
Cover Letter Writer
Writes specific, compelling cover letters that use company research and real career stories. Never generic. Includes email subject line and a 30-word LinkedIn InMail version.
write cover letter draft my application
🏢
Company Researcher
Builds a full intelligence brief: funding, product, culture, interview process, recent news. Surfaces talking points, questions to ask, and red flags to probe. Used before applications and before every interview.
research this company interview prep
📤
Application Submitter
Manages the full 4-track strategy: ATS application, LinkedIn recruiter outreach, hiring manager signal, and referral activation. Generates every message, ready to send.
apply to this job submit application
💬
Outreach Engine
Generates personalized outreach for recruiters, hiring managers, warm contacts, and cold networking. 6 message types, 3 tone variations each. Includes follow-up cadences and response handling scripts.
message recruiter reach out to network
🎯
Interview Coach
Builds a 10-story STAR+ bank, generates tailored question sets by role and stage, runs mock interview sessions with feedback. Covers behavioral, technical, case, and salary negotiation.
prep for interview practice questions mock interview
📊
Pipeline Tracker
Manages job search like a sales pipeline with 8 stages. Tracks every application, triggers follow-ups, shows conversion metrics, identifies stuck opportunities, and runs weekly reviews.
show my pipeline track applications follow up
Slash Commands

6 power commands

One-shot commands that orchestrate multiple skills into complete workflows.

/find-jobs
Search 10+ job boards, score every role, return prioritized results by tier with direct apply links.
e.g. /find-jobs Senior PM, remote, SaaS, $180k+
/apply
Full application package in one command: research, tailored resume, cover letter, recruiter message, pipeline entry.
e.g. /apply [job URL or paste JD]
/daily-sprint
Run your complete job search day: pipeline review, new role search, priority application, outreach queue.
e.g. /daily-sprint focus on fintech today
/interview-prep
Full interview prep: company brief, likely questions, story bank, mock interview, questions to ask them.
e.g. /interview-prep Acme Corp, Head of Product, final round
/pipeline
Show dashboard, follow-up queue, conversion metrics, and what to do today.
e.g. /pipeline update Acme to offer stage
/outreach
Generate personalized messages for any target — recruiter, HM, warm contact, or cold network.
e.g. /outreach recruiter at Stripe
