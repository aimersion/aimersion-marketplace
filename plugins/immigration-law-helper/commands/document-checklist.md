# Document Checklist Command

Ask the client for:

1. **Visa category or benefit type** (e.g., family-based I-130/I-485, employment-based H-1B, asylum)
2. **Beneficiary information** (name, birth date, passport number, current visa status)
3. **Petitioner/sponsor information** (if applicable)
4. **Any special circumstances** (prior visa overstay, criminal history, medical issues, etc.)
5. **Documents already collected** (to avoid duplication)

## Generate a comprehensive checklist that covers:

### Required Documents (organized by category):

- **Identity documents** (passport, birth certificate, national ID)
- **Civil documents** (marriage certificate, divorce decree, adoption papers)
- **Medical examination** (Form I-693, vaccination records)
- **Financial documents** (tax returns, W-2s, employment letters, bank statements)
- **Employment/education records** (diplomas, degrees, professional licenses)
- **Police clearances and background checks** (form FD-258 if applicable)
- **Photos** (USCIS specifications for digital photos)
- **Travel documents** (prior visas, entry/exit records, passport pages)

### Category-Specific Requirements:

- For **family-based** cases: relationship evidence, affidavit of support (Form I-864), civil documents
- For **employment-based** cases: labor certification evidence, employer authorization, specialty occupation proof
- For **asylum** cases: country conditions research, persecution documentation, expert declarations

### Translation and Certification Requirements:

- Which documents require certified English translations
- Translator qualifications and certification format
- Estimated timeline for obtaining translations

### Document Organization Notes:

- File naming conventions for USCIS
- Recommended order of presentation
- Page limits and formatting requirements
- When to include originals vs. certified copies

Link to the document-checklist skill and reference materials (family-based-documents.md, employment-based-documents.md) for complete category-specific requirements.

Generate in a clear checklist format with checkboxes, priority levels (required, recommended, supporting), and estimated collection timeline.
