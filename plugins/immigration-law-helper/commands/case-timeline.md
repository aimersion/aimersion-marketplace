# Case Timeline Command

Ask the client for the following information:

1. **Visa category or immigration benefit** (e.g., I-485 adjustment of status, I-130 family petition, N-400 naturalization, asylum application)
2. **Filing date** (when the petition or application was submitted)
3. **USCIS service center** or office that will adjudicate the case (if known)
4. **Current case status** (e.g., petition received, pending review, interview scheduled)
5. **Any receipt notices, priority dates, or case numbers** they have

Once you have this information:

## Generate a detailed case timeline that includes:

- **Filing date and receipt notice timeline** (expected date to receive I-797)
- **Initial review period** (document verification and initial processing)
- **Biometric services appointment** (if applicable)
- **Interview scheduling** (estimated date based on service center processing times)
- **Decision timeline** (expected approval/denial date)
- **Key milestones** specific to the visa category
- **Critical deadlines** for responding to USCIS notices
- **Recommended status check dates** (when to contact USCIS if no updates)

## Include service center-specific information:

- Processing time averages for the specific service center
- Whether premium processing or expedite requests are available for this category
- Any known delays or backlogs affecting the service center

Format the timeline as a clear, calendar-style document with dates, events, and recommended actions. Flag any critical deadlines in red and note aging-out concerns (especially for priority dates nearing visa availability dates or applicants approaching aging-out from visa status).
