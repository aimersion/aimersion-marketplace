## Instructions

Build a comprehensive daily operations plan for today's warehouse activities. Gather the following inputs and structure the daily operations brief:

### Step 1: Gather Operational Inputs

**Inbound Operations**:
- Scheduled inbound appointments (carrier, arrival time window, expected carton/pallet volume, product type)
- Any expedited or hot items due in
- Current dock capacity and availability
- Receiving team staffing (normal, reduced, or extra shift)

**Outbound Operations**:
- Orders in queue (order count, total line count, total unit count)
- Carrier cut-off times (identify all carriers picking today and their cut-off windows)
- Priority orders (expedites, SLA-critical, high-value)
- Current inventory availability (any stock-outs, holds, or constraints)

**Staffing & Resources**:
- Planned headcount by shift (receiving, put-away, picking, packing, shipping)
- Any absences or staffing constraints
- Cross-training availability for flexibility
- Shift schedules (start/end times)

**Equipment & Constraints**:
- Equipment status (forklifts, pallet jacks, conveyors operational?)
- Any equipment down or maintenance planned
- Space constraints (storage full, receiving full, dock congestion likely?)
- Systems issues (WMS healthy, network connectivity?)

### Step 2: Build Inbound Plan

**Dock Assignments**:
- For each appointment: assign dock door, unload sequence, expected duration
- Note any precedence (expedites first, cold-chain items, hazmat handling)

**Receiving Productivity Target**:
- Total expected inbound volume
- Labor standard for receiving (cartons/hour or pallets/hour)
- Required labor hours; compare to scheduled staff
- Flag if understaffed or overstaffed

**Risk Flags**:
- Any appointments at risk (late arrival, oversized loads)?
- Cold-chain items requiring temperature monitoring?
- Hazmat or special handling items?

### Step 3: Build Outbound Plan

**Wave Schedule**:
- Create 2–4 waves based on carrier cut-off times
- Work backward from cut-off to determine pick start time for each wave
- List orders per wave, line count, priority tier
- Assign zones to be picked per wave

**Picking/Packing/Shipping Targets**:
- Estimate labor hours needed for each wave (based on lines per hour benchmark)
- Identify if current staffing sufficient
- Identify any at-risk waves (tight timing, high volume)

**Carrier Schedule**:
- Confirm carrier pickup times for each wave
- Note any consolidation opportunities (combine orders for same carrier)
- Flag late-arriving orders (orders that arrive after wave cutoff)

### Step 4: Staffing Allocation

**By Area**:
- Receiving: __ staff
- Put-away: __ staff
- Picking: __ staff
- Packing: __ staff
- Shipping: __ staff
- Support/Quality/Supervision: __ staff
- **Total**: __ FTE

**By Shift** (if staggered):
- Early shift (time): roles, headcount
- Day shift (time): roles, headcount
- Evening shift (time): roles, headcount

**Cross-Training Deployment** (if needed):
- Identify flexibility opportunities (if picking wave at risk, pull cross-trained packer)

### Step 5: Risk Flags & Contingencies

**Capacity Concerns**:
- Dock utilization %
- Are any waves at risk of missing cut-off?
- Is storage space constrained?

**Equipment Issues**:
- Any equipment down affecting workflow?
- Backup equipment available?

**Staffing Gaps**:
- Identified absences? (Have backup temp labor on standby?)
- Skill gaps? (New hires on today? Schedule experienced mentor)

**External Dependencies**:
- Waiting on customer purchase orders?
- Waiting on specific inbound receipts before fulfillment can begin?

### Step 6: Key Performance Targets for Today

Set realistic daily targets (benchmark to your facility averages):

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Receiving Productivity** | __ cases/hour | Based on expected volume |
| **Receiving Accuracy** | >99% | Perfect receipts |
| **Picking/Hour** | __ lines/hour | Benchmark productivity |
| **Dock-to-Stock Time** | <3 hours | Average receipt to available |
| **On-Time Shipment** | >98% | Orders shipped before cut-off |
| **Shipping Accuracy** | >99.5% | Perfect orders shipped |
| **Safety** | Zero incidents | Reinforce safety protocol |
