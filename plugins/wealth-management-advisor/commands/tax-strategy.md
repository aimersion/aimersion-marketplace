# Tax Strategy Command

Develop comprehensive tax optimization strategies aligned with the client's situation, income level, and long-term goals.

## Purpose

Create actionable, year-round tax strategies that minimize tax liability through harvesting, conversions, charitable giving, and strategic asset placement while maintaining investment discipline.

## Inputs

The user should provide:
- **Client Situation**: Income level, filing status, dependents
- **Tax Bracket**: Federal and state marginal rates
- **Portfolio**: Asset values and cost basis
- **Realized/Unrealized Gains**: Current position
- **Loss Carryforwards**: Prior year capital losses
- **Income Sources**: W-2, self-employment, investment income
- **Charitable Intent**: Interest and level
- **Retirement Planning**: 401k/IRA status and conversion interest
- **Time Horizon**: Planning for 1 year, multi-year, or long-term
- **Goals**: Specific tax optimization objectives

## Usage Pattern

```
Develop a tax strategy for [CLIENT NAME].
Situation: [ANNUAL INCOME, FILING STATUS, KEY DETAILS]
Concerns: [SPECIFIC TAX ISSUES]
Goals: [PRIMARY OBJECTIVES]
Assets: [PORTFOLIO COMPOSITION AND VALUES]
```

## Output Includes

1. **Tax Situation Analysis**
   - Current marginal tax rate
   - Effective tax rate calculation
   - Tax bracket analysis
   - Alternative Minimum Tax (AMT) risk assessment
   - Net Investment Income Tax (NIIT) exposure
   - State tax considerations

2. **Tax-Loss Harvesting Plan**
   - Unrealized losses identified
   - Harvest candidates prioritized by loss size
   - Tax rate benefit calculated
   - Replacement securities identified
   - Wash sale avoidance strategy
   - Multi-year harvest plan
   - Estimated annual tax savings

3. **Roth Conversion Analysis**
   - Conversion feasibility assessment
   - Target conversion amount (bracket optimization)
   - Pro-rata rule impact (if multiple IRAs)
   - Income projection for conversion year
   - After-tax analysis
   - Five-year holding period strategy
   - Estimated tax cost and long-term benefit

4. **Charitable Giving Strategy**
   - Charitable intent assessment
   - Vehicle recommendations (DAF, CRT, CLT, direct gifts)
   - Deduction limit analysis
   - Carryforward optimization
   - Bunching strategy (if high/low income years)
   - Tax benefit quantification
   - Administrative complexity and costs

5. **Asset Location Optimization**
   - Current account-by-account audit
   - Recommended reallocation
   - Implementation plan (tax-efficient transitions)
   - Expected tax impact
   - Long-term efficiency gains
   - Ongoing monitoring strategy

6. **Capital Gains Management**
   - Concentrated stock assessment
   - Holding period optimization
   - Short-term vs long-term strategy
   - Collar strategy evaluation
   - Exchange fund analysis
   - Prepaid variable forward evaluation
   - NUA strategy (if employer stock)

7. **Income Timing and Deferral**
   - Income projection for coming years
   - Timing opportunities
   - Deduction bunching opportunities
   - Retirement distribution strategies
   - Business income timing (if applicable)
   - Estimated tax impacts

8. **Annual Tax Planning Calendar**
   - January-March actions
   - April-May considerations
   - June-August planning
   - September-October preparations
   - November-December execution
   - Deadline tracking

9. **Implementation Plan**
   - Prioritized actions (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
   - Responsible parties (advisor, CPA, client)
   - Estimated tax impact per action
   - Execution timeline
   - Required coordination

## Customization Options

- **Aggressive Tax Planning**: Maximum legal strategies for high-income clients
- **Conservative Approach**: Simpler strategies with clear regulatory support
- **Business Owner Focus**: Self-employment income and entity optimization
- **Retiree Focus**: RMD, Social Security, and Medicare Premium planning
- **High-Net-Worth Focus**: Alternative investments, concentrated stock, complex structures
- **Charitable Focus**: Maximize both tax benefit and giving impact

## Regulatory Compliance

- All strategies comply with current tax law
- Wash sale rules applied consistently
- Pro-rata rule calculations verified
- Gift tax considerations noted
- State tax implications included
- Documentation requirements identified

## Integration with Other Skills

- **Estate Planner**: Coordinate lifetime gifting with estate plan
- **Portfolio Reporter**: Align tax strategy with investment performance
- **Compliance Tracker**: Document tax decisions and rationales
- **Client Communicator**: Explain tax strategy to client clearly

## Notes

- Tax laws change; review and update strategy annually
- Coordinate with client's CPA for implementation
- Document tax decisions and rationales
- Some strategies require multiple-year perspective
- Consider client's risk tolerance and complexity preference
- State tax implications vary significantly
- Implement strategies in tax-efficient manner (sequencing matters)
