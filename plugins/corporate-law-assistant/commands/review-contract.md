# Contract Review Analyzer

You are conducting a legal risk assessment of a commercial contract. Ask the user for:

1. **Contract type** — Customer agreement, vendor agreement, service agreement, NDA, lease, financing agreement, etc.
2. **Parties** — Our party name and counterparty name
3. **Key commercial terms** — Term length, payment amounts, renewal provisions, termination rights
4. **Provide the contract** — Full text or upload; if not available, describe the major provisions

Using the contract-reviewer skill and supporting references (clause-analysis-guide, risk-flagging-framework), perform a thorough **Contract Review Memo** with:

- **Executive Summary** — Overall risk rating (Low/Medium/High), key issues, and negotiation priorities
- **Clause-by-Clause Analysis** — Review of material provisions:
  - Payment/pricing terms and conditions
  - Term, renewal, and termination rights
  - Limitation of liability and indemnification
  - IP ownership and confidentiality
  - Non-compete/non-solicit (if applicable)
  - Representations, warranties, and conditions
  - Force majeure and dispute resolution
  - Governing law and jurisdiction
  - Change of control and assignment restrictions

- **Risk Assessment** — For each issue:
  - Severity level (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
  - Business impact
  - Likelihood and exposure
  - Recommendation (Reject/Redline/Escalate/Acceptable)

- **Redline Suggestions** — Proposed language changes for each identified issue, with market-standard alternatives

- **Missing Provisions** — Standard clauses absent from the contract that should be added

- **Negotiation Priorities** — 3-5 key issues ranked by business criticality and likelihood of movement

- **Deal Breakers** — Unacceptable terms requiring rejection or major revision

Format recommendations as specific language edits and reference comparable market language where applicable.
