# Wellness Plan Builder

Create tiered wellness packages that improve client compliance, increase revenue predictability, and deliver better preventive care.

## Why Wellness Plans Work

- **For the pet:** Pets on wellness plans receive 2-3x more preventive care visits, leading to earlier disease detection
- **For the client:** Predictable monthly payments make vet care budgetable and reduce sticker shock
- **For the practice:** Increased visit frequency, improved client retention (90%+ renewal vs. 60-70% annual return rate), predictable monthly revenue

## Plan Design by Life Stage

### Puppy/Kitten Plans (0-1 year)

High-value period — puppies/kittens need 3-4 vaccine visits plus spay/neuter. Perfect for building lifetime loyalty.

**Typical inclusions:**
- 3-4 puppy/kitten exam visits with vaccinations
- Core vaccine series (DHPP ×3 or FVRCP ×3, Rabies ×1)
- Non-core vaccines as appropriate (Bordetella, Lepto, FeLV)
- Fecal parasite screens (×2)
- Deworming treatments
- Microchip with registration
- Spay or neuter surgery
- Pre-surgical bloodwork
- 6-12 months of heartworm/flea/tick prevention
- Nail trims (unlimited or set number)
- Nutrition consultations
- Puppy/kitten behavioral guidance

**Pricing approach:** Calculate total à la carte value. Discount 20-30%. Divide by 12 months. Typical range: $55-95/month depending on what's included and geographic market.

### Adult Plans (1-7 years)

Maintenance period — keep them coming in regularly and compliant on preventives.

**Typical inclusions:**
- Annual comprehensive exam
- Core vaccinations (per due schedule)
- Heartworm/tick-borne disease screening (4Dx or equivalent)
- Fecal parasite screen
- Annual bloodwork (CBC, chemistry)
- Urinalysis
- 12 months heartworm prevention
- 12 months flea/tick prevention
- Dental assessment
- Nail trims (set number or unlimited)
- Discount on additional services (10-15% off dental cleaning, surgery, etc.)

**Pricing approach:** Discount 15-20% from à la carte. Typical range: $35-65/month.

### Senior Plans (7+ years)

Higher medical need — biannual exams and expanded diagnostics catch age-related conditions early.

**Typical inclusions:**
- 2 comprehensive exams per year
- Core vaccinations (titer testing option)
- Heartworm/tick-borne disease screening
- Fecal parasite screen
- Comprehensive bloodwork ×2 per year (CBC, chemistry, T4)
- Urinalysis ×2 per year
- Blood pressure screening
- 12 months heartworm prevention
- 12 months flea/tick prevention
- Dental assessment ×2
- Nail trims
- Pain/mobility assessment
- Discount on dental cleanings, diagnostics, and medications

**Pricing approach:** Discount 15-20% from à la carte. Typical range: $55-85/month.

## Tiered Plan Structure

Most successful programs offer 2-3 tiers:

| Tier | Name Suggestion | What's Different |
|------|----------------|-----------------|
| Essential | "Healthy Paws" | Core vaccines, exam, basic diagnostics, preventives |
| Plus | "Healthy Paws+" | Essential + comprehensive bloodwork, dental discount, more nail trims |
| Premium | "VIP Paws" | Plus + dental cleaning, unlimited sick visit exams, highest discounts |

## Financial Modeling

### Revenue Impact Calculation
- Average clients enrolled × monthly plan price = predictable monthly revenue
- Target: 20-30% of active clients enrolled within 2 years
- A 2-DVM practice with 3,000 active clients enrolling 600 at $50/month = $30,000/month in predictable revenue

### Breakeven Analysis
Calculate: If a client uses every single service in the plan, does the practice lose money? If yes, adjust pricing up or service inclusions down. Healthy target: 5-10% margin on full utilization. In practice, not every client uses every service, so actual margins are typically 15-25%.

## Terms and Conditions Essentials

- 12-month commitment (auto-renewing)
- Early cancellation: remaining plan balance due
- No refunds for services already rendered
- Plan is for the enrolled pet only (not transferable between pets)
- If pet passes away: remaining balance forgiven or prorated based on services used
- Services do not roll over to next plan year
- Client responsible for services not included in plan at regular pricing
- Practice reserves right to adjust pricing at renewal
