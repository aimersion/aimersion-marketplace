Generate an equipment maintenance checklist or track maintenance status for the car wash.

Ask the user for:
- Checklist type needed (daily opening, weekly, monthly, or annual)
- Any current equipment issues or concerns
- Equipment age and type (tunnel, touchless, self-serve bays)
- Chemical inventory status (if tracking chemicals)

Generate a comprehensive report using the equipment-monitor skill:

1. **Maintenance checklist**: Complete checklist for the requested interval with every inspection point
2. **Chemical inventory status**: Current levels, usage rates, and reorder alerts
3. **Downtime log review**: Any recent equipment failures, patterns, and preventive measures
4. **Cost tracking**: Maintenance and chemical costs per car, trending over time
5. **Upcoming maintenance**: Scheduled major service items in the next 30/60/90 days
6. **Capital equipment assessment**: Any equipment approaching end of useful life or needing replacement

Format checklists as printable documents that staff can use on-site during their inspections.
