Analyze an existing membership program or design a new one for the car wash business.

Ask the user for:
- Whether they have an existing program (analyzing) or need to create one (designing)
- Current wash packages and retail prices
- Number of active members (if existing)
- Current monthly churn rate (if known)
- Variable cost per wash (chemical + labor)
- Competitive landscape (what nearby washes charge for memberships)

For **existing programs**, generate:
1. **Program health dashboard**: Active members, MRR, churn rate, average redemption rate
2. **Profitability analysis**: Revenue per member minus variable cost of redemptions
3. **Churn analysis**: Monthly churn trend, top cancellation reasons, cancel-save success rate
4. **Tier distribution**: Percentage of members at each tier — is the mix optimal?
5. **Recommendations**: Specific actions to grow membership, reduce churn, and increase revenue

For **new programs**, generate:
1. **Tier design**: 3-4 membership tiers with names, included services, and pricing
2. **Financial model**: Projected enrollment, MRR, redemption costs, and profit per member
3. **Launch plan**: How to introduce the program and convert existing retail customers
4. **Marketing materials**: Signup pitch scripts, signage copy, email announcement
5. **Retention strategy**: Cancel-save scripts, engagement tactics, multi-vehicle discounts

Use the membership-manager skill for pricing frameworks, economics, and retention strategies.
