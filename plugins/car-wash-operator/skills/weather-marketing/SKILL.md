# Weather Marketing

Leverage weather patterns to drive volume through smart promotions, rain guarantees, and weather-triggered campaigns.

## Rain Guarantee Program

### How It Works
"If it rains within 24/48 hours of your wash, come back for a free re-wash!"

### Implementation
- **Trigger**: Measurable rainfall (0.10"+ typical threshold)
- **Window**: 24 hours (standard) or 48 hours (generous / competitive advantage)
- **Redemption**: Show receipt or scan membership tag within {window} of rain event
- **Applicable washes**: Mid-tier and above (exclude basic wash to protect margin)
- **Membership bonus**: Members get automatic re-wash eligibility — no receipt needed

### Why It Works
- Removes the #1 objection: "Why wash it if it's going to rain?"
- Drives traffic on sunny days (customers wash knowing they're covered)
- Re-wash redemption rate is typically only 15-25% (most won't come back)
- Builds trust and removes purchase friction
- Great marketing hook for social media and signage

### Rain Guarantee Signage Copy
"Our Rain Guarantee: If it rains within 48 hours of your wash, bring your receipt back for a FREE re-wash. Because we believe a clean car shouldn't have to wait for perfect weather."

## Weather-Triggered Campaigns

### Pollen Season (Spring)
**Trigger**: Pollen count reaches "high" in local forecast
**Campaign**: "Pollen Alert! Your car is covered in yellow — we can fix that. Wash special: {offer}"
**Channels**: Text blast to customer list, social media, Google Business post
**Duration**: Active during peak pollen weeks (typically March-May)

### Post-Rain Event
**Trigger**: Rain stops after significant rainfall
**Campaign**: "The rain stopped — now let's get rid of the water spots! Post-rain wash special: {offer}"
**Timing**: Send within 2-4 hours of rain stopping
**Best for**: Sunny day after rain — people are out and see dirty cars

### Salt Season (Winter)
**Trigger**: Road salt application or freezing temperatures
**Campaign**: "Salt eats your car's finish. Protect your investment with an underbody flush. Winter wash: {offer}"
**Focus**: Underbody wash packages — emphasize protection, not aesthetics
**Duration**: November-March in salt states

### Heat Wave (Summer)
**Trigger**: 90°F+ forecast for 3+ consecutive days
**Campaign**: "Beat the heat at {Wash Name}! Cool off with a fresh wash. Stay inside your car — we do the work."
**Angle**: Convenience — A/C comfort while car gets cleaned

### First Nice Day of Spring
**Trigger**: First 60°F+ sunny day after winter
**Campaign**: "Spring is here! Your car survived winter — reward it with a detail. Spring special: {offer}"
**Expectation**: Highest-volume day of spring — be fully staffed

### Pre-Holiday Travel
**Trigger**: 3-5 days before major travel holidays (Thanksgiving, Christmas, 4th of July)
**Campaign**: "Road trip ready? Get a fresh wash before you hit the road. Holiday prep special: {offer}"

## Weather Response Playbook

| Weather Event | Response Time | Campaign Type | Channel |
|--------------|--------------|---------------|---------|
| Rain incoming | 24 hrs before | "Beat the rain" | Social, text |
| Post-rain clear | 2-4 hrs after | "Post-rain cleanup" | Text blast |
| Pollen spike | Same day | "Pollen alert" | Social, email |
| First freeze | Same day | "Salt protection" | Social, text |
| Snow/ice melt | Day after | "Road salt removal" | Text, social |
| Heat wave | First day of wave | "Stay cool" convenience | Social |
| Major storm aftermath | 12-24 hrs after | "Storm recovery" | All channels |
