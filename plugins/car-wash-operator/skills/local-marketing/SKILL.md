# Local Marketing

Drive local traffic to the car wash through targeted campaigns, community partnerships, and loyalty programs.

## Grand Opening Campaign

### Timeline (8 Weeks)

**Weeks 1-2: Pre-Launch Tease**
- Social media countdown posts
- "Coming soon" banner on site
- Email/text blast to any existing customer database
- Local press release to newspapers and online news
- Google Business Profile setup and optimization

**Weeks 3-4: Soft Opening**
- Invite-only or limited-hours opening for neighbors and friends
- Test all systems at volume
- Train staff under real conditions
- Collect feedback and fix issues
- Start Google review collection from early visitors

**Weeks 5-6: Grand Opening Event (Main Event)**
- Free or heavily discounted washes for 2-3 days
- Event elements: food truck, music, giveaways, ribbon cutting
- Local officials/chamber of commerce invited
- Membership signup special (first month free or 50% off)
- Charity component ("We'll donate $1 per wash to {local charity}")
- Live social media coverage
- Professional photos for future marketing

**Weeks 7-8: Momentum Building**
- Thank-you email to all visitors with membership offer
- Social media recap posts with event photos
- Continue introductory pricing (2-week extension)
- Direct mail to surrounding 3-5 mile radius
- Referral program launch

### Grand Opening Budget Guide
- **Signage and banners**: $500-2,000
- **Food/beverages**: $500-1,500
- **Giveaways**: $300-1,000
- **Free wash cost** (chemical/labor): $500-2,000
- **Digital advertising**: $1,000-3,000
- **Direct mail**: $1,000-3,000
- **Misc (DJ, tent, supplies)**: $500-1,500
- **Total range**: $4,300-14,000

## Loyalty and Punch Card Programs

### Simple Punch Card
"Buy 5 washes, get 1 free"
- Works for retail (non-member) customers
- Encourages repeat visits
- Low-tech — physical card or simple digital tracking
- Cost: ~17% discount (1 free out of 6)

### Digital Loyalty (App-Based)
- Points per dollar spent
- Tiered rewards (Bronze/Silver/Gold based on spend level)
- Birthday rewards
- Referral bonuses
- Push notifications for promotions
- Integrates with POS system

## Community Partnership Strategies

### Cross-Promotions
- **Restaurants nearby**: "Show your car wash receipt for 10% off" (and vice versa)
- **Gas stations**: Partner on bundled deals
- **Auto dealers**: Provide complimentary washes for new car buyers
- **Real estate agents**: New homeowner welcome packages with free washes
- **Insurance agencies**: "Protect your investment — free wash with policy review"

### Charity Partnerships
- Monthly charity wash day ("$1 from every wash goes to {Charity}")
- School fundraiser partnerships ("Show your school pass for $2 off, we donate $1")
- First responder/military discounts (builds goodwill and word-of-mouth)
- Youth sports team sponsorships (logo on team jerseys = visibility)

### Corporate Accounts
- Offer bulk wash books (20 washes at 15% discount)
- Fleet accounts for local businesses
- Employee benefit programs ("Add our car wash to your employee perks")

## Digital Marketing Essentials

### Google Business Profile (Highest Priority)
- Complete all business info (hours, services, photos, attributes)
- Post weekly (specials, updates, photos)
- Respond to every review within 24 hours
- Add photos weekly (interior, exterior, before/after, team)
- Enable messaging for customer inquiries

### Local SEO
- Target keywords: "car wash near me", "car wash {city}", "best car wash {city}"
- Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across all directories
- Get listed on: Google, Yelp, Facebook, Apple Maps, Bing Places, Waze
- Build local backlinks: chamber of commerce, local directories, sponsors

### Social Media (Focus Areas)
- **Facebook**: Community engagement, events, reviews, local targeting
- **Instagram**: Visual content — clean car photos, behind-the-scenes
- **TikTok**: Fun/satisfying wash process videos
- **Nextdoor**: Hyperlocal recommendations, new neighbor outreach
