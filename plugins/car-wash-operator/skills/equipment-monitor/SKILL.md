# Equipment Monitor

Track equipment maintenance, monitor chemical usage, and prevent costly downtime through proactive maintenance scheduling.

## Equipment Maintenance Schedules

### Daily Maintenance
- **Conveyor/tunnel system**: Visual inspection, check chain tension, clear debris
- **Spray nozzles**: Check all nozzles for clogs or misdirection
- **Brushes/wraps**: Inspect for damage, check positioning
- **Dryers**: Clean lint screens, check belt tension, listen for bearing noise
- **Water reclaim system**: Check filters, empty sediment traps
- **Payment terminals**: Test all payment methods, clean screens
- **Vacuums**: Empty canisters, check hoses, test suction
- **General site**: Drain cleanup, remove debris, check signage and lighting

### Weekly Maintenance
- **Conveyor**: Lubricate chain and bearings, check roller condition
- **Chemical system**: Check dilution ratios, inspect injectors, verify chemical levels
- **Water softener**: Check salt levels, verify regeneration cycle
- **Spot-free rinse system**: Check TDS levels, replace DI resin if needed
- **Dryers**: Inspect motors, check ductwork connections
- **Bay doors/gates**: Lubricate tracks, test sensors and safety stops
- **Signage and lighting**: Replace burned-out lights, clean signage

### Monthly Maintenance
- **Full conveyor inspection**: Chain wear measurement, roller replacement assessment
- **Brush/wrap replacement assessment**: Measure material wear
- **Complete chemical system audit**: All injectors, lines, proportioners
- **Water heating system**: Inspect heater, check temperature settings
- **Electrical panel inspection**: Check breakers, connections, corrosion
- **Sump/pit cleaning**: Pump and clean all below-grade sumps
- **Safety system test**: All e-stops, sensors, safety gates

### Quarterly/Annual Maintenance
- **Major conveyor service**: Chain replacement (if needed), bearing replacement
- **Motor service**: Check amperage, vibration, and bearing condition
- **RO/DI system**: Membrane replacement, full system service
- **Water reclaim system**: Full clean, media replacement, pump service
- **Roof and drainage**: Inspect for leaks, clean gutters and drains
- **Compliance**: Environmental permits, water discharge testing

## Chemical Usage Tracking

### Key Chemicals and Usage Rates

| Chemical | Application | Typical Usage Rate | Monitor For |
|----------|------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Pre-soak | Dirt/road film removal | 1-3 oz per car | Under-application = poor clean, over = waste |
| Foam polish/tri-color | Customer visual appeal | 1-2 oz per car | Consistent foam height and color |
| Wheel cleaner | Brake dust removal | 0.5-1.5 oz per car | Effectiveness on alloy wheels |
| Clear coat/sealant | Paint protection | 0.5-1 oz per car | Beading test — water should bead on exit |
| Drying agent | Spot-free finish | 0.5-1 oz per car | Water sheeting effectiveness |
| Tire dressing | Tire shine | 0.5-1 oz per car | Even coverage, not over-applied |
| Rain repellent | Windshield treatment | 0.2-0.5 oz per car | Bead test on windshield |

### Chemical Cost per Car
Target: $0.50-2.00 per car (varies by package level)
- Basic wash: $0.30-0.75
- Mid-tier: $0.75-1.25
- Premium: $1.25-2.00
- Ultimate: $1.75-2.50

### Monitoring Dilution Ratios
- Check weekly with titration or refractometer
- Over-diluted = poor wash quality (customer complaints)
- Under-diluted = wasted chemical cost
- Maintain dilution logs for consistency

## Downtime Tracking

For each equipment failure, document:
1. **Date and time** of failure
2. **Equipment affected**
3. **Impact** (partial operation, full shutdown, reduced capacity)
4. **Duration** of downtime
5. **Root cause** (wear, operator error, power issue, etc.)
6. **Repair performed** and cost
7. **Preventive measure** to avoid recurrence

### Target Metrics
- **Uptime**: 98%+ during operating hours
- **Mean time between failures**: Track per equipment component
- **Planned vs. unplanned downtime ratio**: Target 80% planned / 20% unplanned
- **Average repair time**: Track to identify where to stock spare parts
