Research and identify current trending topics relevant to Aimersion AI's niche across social media and the web.

## Process

1. **Search for trends** — Use web search to find:
   - Current trending topics on X/Twitter in AI and technology
   - Viral or high-engagement LinkedIn posts in the AI/tech space
   - Recent major AI news, product launches, and industry developments
   - Trending hashtags in relevant categories

2. **Filter for relevance** — Evaluate each trend against Aimersion AI's expertise areas:
   - AI and machine learning
   - Business automation
   - Technology adoption
   - Productivity and workflow tools
   - SaaS and startup ecosystem

3. **Assess each trend** — For every relevant trend, evaluate:
   - **Timeliness**: Is it rising, peaking, or fading?
   - **Audience fit**: Will Aimersion AI's audience engage with this?
   - **Brand safety**: Any risks to brand reputation?
   - **Content potential**: How many posts or angles can we get from it?

4. **Generate content angles** — For the top 5 trends, propose specific content ideas for X, LinkedIn, and Facebook.

5. **Prioritize** — Rank trends by opportunity (relevance x timeliness x audience fit) and recommend which to act on first.

## Output
Present a prioritized trend report with content opportunities. For the #1 trend, draft a ready-to-post example for each platform.
