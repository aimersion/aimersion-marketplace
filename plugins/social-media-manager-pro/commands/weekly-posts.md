Generate a full week (Monday through Friday) of social media content for Aimersion AI across X, LinkedIn, and Facebook.

## Process

1. **Research current context** — Search the web for recent AI industry news, Aimersion AI updates, and trending topics to make content timely and relevant.

2. **Plan the week** — Create a content calendar with one post per platform per day (15 posts total). Vary content types across the week:
   - Monday: Industry insight or thought leadership
   - Tuesday: Product/feature highlight or tip
   - Wednesday: Engagement post (question, poll, or community discussion)
   - Thursday: Industry news commentary or trend piece
   - Friday: Lighter content (behind-the-scenes, team highlight, or weekend inspiration)

3. **Draft all posts** — Write each post following platform-specific best practices:
   - **X**: Under 280 characters, punchy, 1-2 hashtags
   - **LinkedIn**: Professional tone, strong opening hook, 3-5 hashtags at end
   - **Facebook**: Friendly and conversational, clear CTA, 1-3 hashtags

4. **Include hashtags** — Research and add relevant hashtags for each post using a mix of broad, mid-range, and niche tags. Always include #AimersionAI where natural.

5. **Present the calendar** — Format as a clear daily schedule with all posts organized by day and platform. Include suggested posting times.

6. **Save the output** — Write the complete content calendar to a markdown file for reference.

## Brand Voice Reminders
- Knowledgeable but approachable
- Forward-thinking and empowering
- Avoid jargon-heavy language or aggressive sales tone
- Include real value in every post

## Output
Present the full weekly calendar, then ask if the user wants to:
- Adjust any posts
- Post any of them immediately
- Add Instagram captions
- Modify the themes for any day
