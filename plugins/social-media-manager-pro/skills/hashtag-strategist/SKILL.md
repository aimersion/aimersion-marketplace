# Hashtag Strategist

Research, curate, and recommend hashtag strategies tailored by platform, niche, and content goal.

## Hashtag Strategy by Platform

### X (Twitter)
- **Optimal count**: 1-2 per tweet (more reduces engagement)
- **Placement**: Inline within the tweet text or at the end
- **Strategy**: Focus on trending or conversation-specific tags; avoid generic overused tags
- **Research method**: Search X for trending topics, check what's active in the AI/tech space

### LinkedIn
- **Optimal count**: 3-5 per post
- **Placement**: At the end of the post, separated by line breaks
- **Strategy**: Mix of broad industry tags and niche-specific tags; LinkedIn's algorithm indexes hashtags for discovery
- **Research method**: Check LinkedIn's suggested hashtags, review competitor usage

### Facebook
- **Optimal count**: 1-3 (hashtags have minimal impact on Facebook)
- **Placement**: At the end or inline if natural
- **Strategy**: Use sparingly; focus on branded or campaign-specific hashtags
- **Research method**: Check Facebook search for community usage

### Instagram
- **Optimal count**: 5-15 (up to 30 allowed, but quality over quantity)
- **Placement**: End of caption or first comment
- **Strategy**: Tier hashtags by size — mix high-volume (500K+), mid-volume (10K-500K), and niche (under 10K) for best reach
- **Research method**: Search Instagram explore, analyze competitor posts, check related tags

## Hashtag Research Process

1. **Identify the content topic and goal** — What is the post about? Is the goal awareness, engagement, or conversion?
2. **Determine the platform(s)** — Apply platform-specific best practices
3. **Research relevant hashtags** — Use web search to find current, active hashtags in the niche
4. **Categorize by reach tier**:
   - **Broad**: High-volume, general tags (e.g., #AI, #Technology)
   - **Mid-range**: Industry-specific tags (e.g., #AItools, #MachineLearning)
   - **Niche**: Targeted community tags (e.g., #AIforBusiness, #SmallBusinessAI)
   - **Branded**: Company-specific tags (e.g., #AimersionAI)
5. **Compile recommendations** — Provide a curated set with rationale

## Output Format

Present hashtag recommendations as:

**Topic**: [content topic]
**Platform**: [target platform]

| Hashtag | Tier | Rationale |
|---------|------|-----------|
| #Example | Broad | High visibility in [category] |
| #Specific | Niche | Targeted [audience] community |
