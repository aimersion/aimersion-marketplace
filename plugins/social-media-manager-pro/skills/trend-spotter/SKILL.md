# Trend Spotter

Monitor current trends across social media and the web, then translate them into timely content opportunities for Aimersion AI.

## Trend Research Process

1. **Search for current trends** — Use web search and X search to find what's trending in AI, technology, and relevant business topics
2. **Filter for relevance** — Evaluate each trend against Aimersion AI's brand, audience, and expertise
3. **Assess timing** — Determine if the trend is rising, peaking, or fading; prioritize early-stage trends
4. **Generate content angles** — For each relevant trend, propose specific content ideas by platform
5. **Evaluate risk** — Flag any trends that carry brand safety risks (controversial, political, potentially offensive)

## Trend Sources

Search across these sources to find current trends:
- **X (Twitter)**: Trending topics, viral posts in AI/tech, industry conversations
- **LinkedIn**: Trending articles, industry news, professional discourse
- **Web search**: Recent news, product launches, industry reports, viral content
- **Industry-specific**: AI announcements, tech company news, SaaS developments

## Relevance Criteria for Aimersion AI

A trend is relevant if it connects to:
- Artificial intelligence and machine learning developments
- Business automation and efficiency
- Technology adoption and digital transformation
- Small business and enterprise tech tools
- AI ethics, safety, and responsible development
- Productivity and workflow innovation
- Startup and SaaS ecosystem news

## Trend Evaluation Framework

For each trend, assess:

| Factor | Rating | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Relevance** | High / Medium / Low | How closely does it align with Aimersion AI's space? |
| **Timeliness** | Rising / Peak / Fading | Where is this trend in its lifecycle? |
| **Audience fit** | Strong / Moderate / Weak | Will our audience care? |
| **Brand safety** | Safe / Caution / Avoid | Any risk to brand reputation? |
| **Content potential** | Rich / Limited | How much content can we create from this? |

## Output Format

### Trend Report

For each identified trend:

**Trend**: [Name/description]
**Why it matters**: [1-2 sentences on relevance to Aimersion AI]
**Timeliness**: [Rising / Peak / Fading]
**Content opportunities**:
- **X**: [Specific tweet idea]
- **LinkedIn**: [Specific post angle]
- **Facebook**: [Specific content idea]

### Prioritized Recommendations

Rank the top 3-5 trends by opportunity score (relevance x timeliness x audience fit) and recommend which to act on first.

## Timing Guidance

- **Breaking news / viral moments**: Act within hours if relevant
- **Industry trends**: Act within 1-2 days to stay timely
- **Emerging themes**: Plan content for the coming week
- **Evergreen trends**: Queue for content calendar gaps
