Create a seasonal marketing campaign for the independent pharmacy. Season or type: $ARGUMENTS

## Process

1. **Context** — Ask the user for:
   - Pharmacy name and community description
   - Services offered (immunizations, compounding, MTM, point-of-care testing, delivery, etc.)
   - Target demographic (elderly, families, local community, specific health conditions)
   - Budget range
   - Previous successful campaigns (what worked before)

2. **Campaign Design:**

   **Campaign Theme and Timeline**
   - Campaign name and tagline
   - Start and end dates
   - Key milestones and events

   **In-Store Marketing:**
   - Window signage copy and design direction
   - Counter displays and bag stuffers
   - Shelf talkers for featured products
   - Waiting area posters/flyers
   - Staff talking points (what to say at pickup)

   **Digital Marketing:**
   - Social media posts (3-4 posts per week during campaign)
   - Email newsletter content
   - Google Business Profile updates
   - Website banner or landing page copy
   - Facebook/Instagram ad copy (if running paid)

   **Community Outreach:**
   - Health screening events (blood pressure, blood glucose, A1C, bone density)
   - Community partnerships (senior centers, churches, schools, local businesses)
   - Educational workshops or lunch-and-learns
   - Local newspaper or community bulletin placement

   **Direct Patient Communication:**
   - Targeted messages to patients who would benefit from the campaign service
   - Prescription bag inserts
   - Medication counseling talking points during dispensing

3. **Seasonal Campaign Templates:**

   **Flu Season (Sept-Feb):**
   - Walk-in flu shot promotion
   - High-dose / adjuvanted options for 65+
   - "Protect your family" messaging
   - Workplace flu shot clinics for local businesses
   - Track immunization numbers for reporting

   **Heart Health (February):**
   - Free blood pressure screenings
   - Statin adherence check-ins
   - Heart-healthy OTC product displays
   - Partner with local cardiologists for referrals

   **Diabetes Awareness (November):**
   - Free A1C screenings or blood glucose checks
   - Diabetes supply showcase
   - MTM outreach to diabetic patients
   - CGM (continuous glucose monitor) education if stocked

   **Back-to-School (August):**
   - Required immunization clinics
   - Rescue inhaler and EpiPen refill reminders
   - ADHD medication refill outreach
   - Head lice treatment product display

   **Holiday Season (Nov-Dec):**
   - Gift ideas from the pharmacy (wellness baskets, OTC gift sets)
   - Medication sync before holiday travel
   - "Don't forget your meds" travel reminder
   - Senior delivery service promotion

4. **Measurement Plan:**
   - Services delivered (vaccinations given, screenings performed)
   - New patients acquired during campaign
   - Revenue from campaign-related services and products
   - Social media engagement metrics
   - Patient feedback/satisfaction
