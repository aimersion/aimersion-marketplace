Generate a professional software development project proposal or Statement of Work (SOW).

Gather from the user:
- Client name and industry
- Project description and objectives
- Technical requirements and constraints
- Timeline expectations
- Budget range (if known)
- Engagement model (fixed price, T&M, retainer)

Produce a comprehensive proposal document with:

## 1. Executive Summary
- One-page overview of the engagement
- Key value propositions
- Why the agency is the right fit

## 2. Understanding of Requirements
- Restate the client's needs in the agency's own words
- Demonstrate domain understanding
- Highlight any risks or ambiguities identified

## 3. Proposed Solution
- Technical architecture overview
- Technology stack recommendations with rationale
- Integration points
- Security and compliance considerations

## 4. Project Approach
- Development methodology (Agile/Scrum specifics)
- Sprint structure and cadence
- Communication plan
- Quality assurance approach
- Deployment strategy

## 5. Team Composition
- Roles needed (tech lead, developers, QA, PM, designer)
- Experience requirements per role
- Team ramp-up plan

## 6. Timeline and Milestones
- Phase breakdown with deliverables per phase
- Milestone dates
- Dependencies and assumptions
- Buffer allocation

## 7. Investment
- Pricing by phase or monthly
- Payment schedule
- What's included and excluded
- Change request process

## 8. Terms and Conditions
- IP ownership
- Warranties
- Confidentiality
- Termination clauses

Save the proposal as a formatted document the user can download.
