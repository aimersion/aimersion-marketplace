Generate a resource allocation and capacity plan for the agency.

Gather:
- Current team roster (names, roles, skill sets)
- Active projects and their resource needs
- Upcoming projects in pipeline
- Team members' current utilization
- Any planned time off or constraints

Produce a resource plan:

## 1. Current Utilization Dashboard
- Table showing each team member, their projects, and utilization %
- Highlight anyone over 90% (burnout risk) or under 60% (bench)
- Calculate overall team utilization rate

## 2. Skill Inventory Matrix
- Map team members against key skills (frontend, backend, mobile, DevOps, design, QA, PM)
- Identify skill gaps and single points of failure
- Recommend cross-training priorities

## 3. Project Staffing Plan
For each active project:
- Required roles and hours per sprint
- Current staffing vs. needs
- Risk assessment for any understaffed projects

## 4. Capacity Forecast (next 90 days)
- Week-by-week capacity by role
- Planned project ramp-ups and ramp-downs
- Identified bottlenecks
- Hiring or contractor needs

## 5. Bench Management
- Current bench time available
- Recommended internal projects or training
- Business development resource needs

## 6. Recommendations
- Immediate staffing changes needed
- Hiring priorities
- Projects at risk and mitigation plans

Output as a downloadable document or spreadsheet.
