Qualify an inbound lead for the development agency.

Gather:
- Lead source (referral, website, RFP, conference, etc.)
- Company name and industry
- Project description
- Estimated budget (if known)
- Timeline requirements
- Decision maker information
- Technical complexity indicators

Produce a lead qualification report:

## 1. Lead Score (0-100)
Score across dimensions:
- Budget fit (0-25): Does their budget match your rates?
- Technical fit (0-25): Can your team deliver this?
- Timeline fit (0-15): Is the timeline realistic?
- Strategic fit (0-15): Does this advance your agency goals?
- Engagement quality (0-20): Decision-maker access, communication responsiveness

## 2. BANT Analysis
- Budget: Estimated project value and client's budget awareness
- Authority: Who's the decision maker? Are they engaged?
- Need: How urgent and real is the need?
- Timeline: When do they need to start and deliver?

## 3. Red Flag Assessment
Check for common agency deal-breakers:
- Unrealistic timeline for scope
- Budget significantly below market rate
- No clear decision maker
- Technology stack the team can't support
- Client has history of scope creep or difficult relationships
- RFP with >5 competing agencies

## 4. Discovery Call Prep
- Key questions to ask
- Technical questions to assess complexity
- Budget probing questions
- Decision process questions
- References to request

## 5. Recommendation
- Pursue / Pursue with conditions / Pass
- If pursue: recommended engagement model and rough estimate
- If pass: reason and suggested referral if applicable

## 6. Portfolio Case Studies
Suggest 2-3 relevant case studies from the agency's portfolio to include in the response (prompt user for their portfolio details).
