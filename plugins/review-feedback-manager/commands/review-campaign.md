Create a complete review solicitation campaign to generate more authentic customer reviews.

## Steps

1. **Understand the business context** — Ask:
   - What industry/type of business?
   - What's your primary review platform (Google, Yelp, Facebook, other)?
   - Do you have a direct link to your review page?
   - How do you typically communicate with customers (email, text, in-person)?
   - Approximately how many customers have you served in the last 30 days?

2. **Design the campaign** using the review-solicitor skill:
   - Select the best channel(s) for their business
   - Create email templates and/or SMS scripts
   - Design the timing and follow-up sequence
   - Generate QR code instructions if applicable

3. **Create campaign assets**:
   - Draft the initial review request email
   - Draft the follow-up reminder (3-5 days later)
   - Create SMS scripts if applicable
   - Write in-person scripts for staff
   - Create a tracking checklist

4. **Set up the campaign**:
   - Create email drafts in connected email (Gmail/Outlook) if available
   - Provide a campaign calendar with send dates
   - Set up tracking metrics

Deliver a complete campaign package:
