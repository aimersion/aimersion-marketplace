Build a complete email flow with timing, subject lines, content, and segmentation logic.

## Steps

1. **Identify flow type** — Ask which flow to build:
   - Abandoned Cart Recovery (3 emails)
   - Welcome Series (4 emails)
   - Post-Purchase (5 emails)
   - Browse Abandonment (1-2 emails)
   - Win-Back Campaign (3-4 emails)
   - VIP/Loyalty Program
   - Or ask: "Which flow should I build first?" and recommend based on their current setup

2. **Gather context** — Ask for:
   - Brand name and voice/tone
   - Average order value
   - Product category
   - Current email platform (Klaviyo, Shopify Email, Omnisend, etc.)
   - Any existing flows running

3. **Build flow** — Following the email-flow-builder skill:
   - Complete email sequence with timing
   - Subject lines for each email
   - Content outline for each email
   - Discount escalation strategy (if applicable)
   - Segmentation logic

4. **Deliver** — Present the complete flow in the deliverable format, plus platform-specific setup tips
