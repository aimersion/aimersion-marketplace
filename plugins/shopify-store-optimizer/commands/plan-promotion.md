Plan a complete promotional campaign with timeline, discount strategy, email sequence, and landing page.

## Steps

1. **Identify the promotion** — Ask:
   - What occasion? (BFCM, Valentine's, flash sale, clearance, custom)
   - When does it run?
   - What's the goal? (Revenue target, inventory clearing, new customer acquisition)
   - What products/collections are included?
   - Budget for discounts (how deep can they go without hurting margins?)

2. **Build the plan** — Following the seasonal-promoter skill:
   - Timeline with preparation milestones
   - Discount structure (type, tiers, customer segments)
   - Email campaign sequence with subject lines and timing
   - Landing page structure
   - Social media post ideas
   - Success metrics to track

3. **Deliver** — Present the complete promotion plan in the deliverable format from the seasonal-promoter skill
