# Product Page Optimizer

Optimize Shopify product pages for maximum conversion using proven copywriting formulas, trust signal placement, pricing psychology, and mobile-first design principles.

## Why This Matters

Product pages are where buying decisions happen. Most Shopify stores write descriptions like instruction manuals instead of sales copy — leading with features ("304 stainless steel") instead of benefits ("Won't rust for years"). Products with reviews convert 270% better. A 1-second page speed delay cuts conversions 7%. This skill fixes all of that.

## Core Workflow

1. **Assess** — Understand the product, target customer, and current page performance
2. **Select Formula** — Choose the right copywriting formula for the product type
3. **Write** — Generate benefit-focused copy using the selected formula
4. **Optimize** — Add trust signals, pricing psychology, and SEO elements
5. **Audit** — Review against the conversion checklist

## Copywriting Formula Selection Guide

Choose based on product type and customer awareness level:

| Product Type | Best Formula | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Most products (default) | **PAS + FAB hybrid** | Hooks emotionally, then delivers specifics |
| High-ticket / skepticism products | **4Ps** (Promise, Picture, Proof, Push) | Overcomes objections with proof |
| Lifestyle / brand-driven products | **Star Story Solution** | Builds emotional connection |
| Technical / comparison-heavy products | **FAB** (Feature, Advantage, Benefit) | Systematic feature-to-benefit translation |
| Problem-solving products | **PAS** (Problem, Agitate, Solution) | Amplifies pain, positions product as relief |
| Products where customer is ready to buy | **AIDA** (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action) | Direct path to purchase |
| Transformation products (before/after) | **BAB** (Before, After, Bridge) | Shows the transformation |

For detailed formula templates and examples, read `references/copywriting-formulas.md`.

## Product Description Structure

Every product description should follow this high-converting structure (80-120 words for main description):

```
[HEADLINE — 7-12 words, benefit-focused, not feature-focused]

[OPENING HOOK — 1 sentence from customer's perspective]

[KEY BENEFITS — 3-4 bullet points answering "So what?" and "Why should I care?"]

[HOW IT WORKS — 2-3 sentences, simple mechanism explanation]

[PROOF — Star rating + review count, testimonial snippet, certification]

[OBJECTION HANDLER — Address #1 hesitation reason]

[GUARANTEE + CTA — Money-back promise + specific action]
```

### Writing Rules

1. **Lead with benefits, not features**
   - Bad: "Features 200gsm polyester fill"
   - Good: "Stays warm at -10°C without restricting movement"

2. **Use conversion language**
   - Bad: "Our products are the best"
   - Good: "99% of customers recommend us to friends"

3. **Answer the real question**: "Why should I buy THIS instead of the alternative?"

4. **Keep it scannable**: One benefit per line, short paragraphs, bold key phrases

5. **Remove decision friction**: State guarantee, list what's included, answer shipping/return questions

6. **Make CTAs specific and benefit-driven**
   - Bad: "Buy Now"
   - Good: "Get Yours — 60-Day Guarantee" or "Add to Cart — Ships Today"

## Trust Signal Placement Strategy

Trust signals increase conversion by up to 42%. Place them strategically:

**Near the price:**
- Star rating + review count
- "Free shipping" badge (if applicable)
- Payment method icons

**Near the Add to Cart button:**
- Money-back guarantee badge
- Security/SSL badge
- "In stock — ships today" indicator

**Below the fold:**
- Full customer reviews with photos
- "Frequently bought together" section
- Detailed specifications in expandable section

## Pricing Psychology Techniques

Apply these based on the product:

- **Charm pricing**: $29.99 instead of $30 (11.3% conversion lift in studies)
- **Anchoring**: Show original price crossed out next to sale price (+32% perceived value)
- **Bundle framing**: "Save 15% when you buy the set" increases AOV 15-30%
- **Free shipping threshold**: Set just above average order value to increase AOV
- **Subscription framing**: "Subscribe & save 20%" for consumables

## Product Page SEO Checklist

- Primary keyword in: title tag, H1, first 100 words, meta description, image alt text
- Unique meta description per product (150-160 chars with keyword and CTA)
- 5-6 product images minimum: front, back, detail, scale, lifestyle, packaging
- Image file names descriptive (blue-cotton-tshirt.jpg not IMG_4532.jpg)
- Images compressed to 70-100KB without visible quality loss
- Structured data / schema markup for products (price, availability, reviews)
- Internal links to related products and collections

## Mobile-First Product Page Checklist

Mobile drives 60-65% of Shopify traffic but converts 30-50% worse than desktop:

- [ ] Product images load in under 2 seconds on 3G
- [ ] Add to Cart button visible without scrolling
- [ ] Sticky Add to Cart bar on scroll
- [ ] Tap targets minimum 44x44px
- [ ] Product title readable without zooming
- [ ] Price and availability prominent
- [ ] Reviews accessible with one tap
- [ ] Image gallery swipeable
- [ ] Description expandable/collapsible sections
- [ ] Express checkout options visible (Shop Pay, Apple Pay)

## Urgency & Scarcity — What Works vs. What Feels Scammy

**Authentic (use these):**
- Real inventory counts: "Only 3 left in stock"
- Genuine deadlines: "Sale ends Sunday at midnight"
- Seasonal relevance: "Order by Dec 18 for Christmas delivery"
- Social proof: "147 people bought this today"

**Scammy (avoid these):**
- Fake countdown timers that reset on refresh
- "Only 1 left!" when there are 500 in stock
- Manufactured urgency with no real deadline
- Pop-ups showing fake recent purchases
