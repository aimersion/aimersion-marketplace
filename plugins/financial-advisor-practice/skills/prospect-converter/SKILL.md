# Prospect Conversion for Financial Advisors

Design and execute multi-channel campaigns to convert prospects into clients. Cover seminar invitations, educational drip sequences, referral programs, and center-of-influence (COI) outreach.

## Campaign Types

### Seminar / Event Invitations
1. **Event concept** — Define topic (retirement planning, Social Security optimization, tax-efficient investing, estate planning basics), target audience, venue, and format (dinner seminar, lunch-and-learn, webinar, workshop).
2. **Invitation copy** — Write compelling invitation with clear value proposition, speaker credentials, event details, RSVP mechanism. Create versions for mail, email, and social media.
3. **Follow-up sequence** — RSVP confirmation, day-before reminder, post-event thank-you with next-step offer (complimentary consultation).
4. **Compliance review notes** — Flag any claims requiring substantiation, ensure no promissory language, include required disclosures.

### Drip Campaigns
1. **Audience segmentation** — Pre-retirees (55-65), recent retirees, business owners, young professionals, inheritance recipients, divorcees/widows.
2. **Content calendar** — 6-12 touch sequence mixing educational content, social proof (anonymized case studies), and soft calls-to-action.
3. **Email sequence** — Subject lines optimized for open rates, plain-text personal style (not marketing blast), clear unsubscribe.
4. **Compliance** — No performance claims without disclosure, no testimonials without required disclaimers (SEC Marketing Rule compliance).

### Referral Programs
1. **Client referral request** — Scripts and email templates for asking existing clients for referrals. Time the ask after positive events (strong review, successful plan milestone).
2. **Thank-you process** — Acknowledgment for referrals (within compliance limits — no cash or material gifts for referrals at most firms).
3. **COI partnerships** — Outreach templates for CPAs, estate attorneys, insurance agents, HR directors, real estate agents.
4. **Reciprocal value** — Content and co-marketing opportunities to offer COI partners.

## Tone and Voice

- Professional but warm and approachable
- Educational, not salesy
- Emphasize fiduciary duty and client-first philosophy
- Avoid jargon with retail prospects; use appropriate terminology with professional referral partners

## Compliance Guardrails

- All materials should note: "Not a guarantee of future results"
- No specific investment recommendations in marketing materials
- Include ADV disclosure where required
- Testimonials and endorsements must comply with SEC Marketing Rule (as of Nov 2022)
- Seminar invites must not be misleading about the nature of the event
