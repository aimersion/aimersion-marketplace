# Market Commentary for Financial Advisors

Produce timely, professional market commentary suitable for client distribution. Balance educational value with compliance requirements.

## Commentary Types

### Weekly Market Recap
1. **Market summary** — Major index performance (S&P 500, Dow, Nasdaq, Russell 2000, international benchmarks) for the week with context.
2. **Key drivers** — 2-3 primary factors driving market movement (economic data, Fed policy, earnings, geopolitical events).
3. **Sector highlights** — Notable sector performance and rotation trends.
4. **Fixed income update** — Treasury yields, credit spreads, and bond market developments.
5. **Looking ahead** — Key economic releases, earnings reports, or events in the coming week.
6. **Advisor perspective** — 1-2 paragraphs of balanced, long-term-focused commentary.

### Quarterly Investment Letter
1. **Quarter in review** — Performance summary with context for all major asset classes.
2. **Economic landscape** — GDP, employment, inflation, consumer sentiment, housing.
3. **Portfolio positioning** — Explain current allocation rationale without being overly specific about holdings.
4. **Outlook** — Forward-looking themes with appropriate hedging language.
5. **Planning opportunities** — Seasonal or timely planning topics (tax-loss harvesting, RMDs, Roth conversions, rebalancing).

### Volatility / Crisis Communication
1. **Acknowledge the situation** — Name what happened without minimizing client concerns.
2. **Historical context** — Put the event in perspective with past episodes and recoveries.
3. **Portfolio impact** — General assessment of how diversified portfolios are positioned.
4. **Our approach** — Reaffirm investment philosophy and process.
5. **Action plan** — What you are doing (or not doing) and why.
6. **Availability** — Offer to meet or call to discuss individual situations.

## Writing Guidelines

- **Tone** — Confident but not arrogant. Calm but not dismissive. Knowledgeable but accessible.
- **Length** — Weekly: 400-600 words. Quarterly: 800-1,200 words. Volatility: 300-500 words.
- **Reading level** — Target 8th-10th grade reading level for retail clients.
- **Always** — Attribute data sources. Use "we believe" or "in our view" for opinions. Include date of writing.
- **Never** — Predict specific market levels or timing. Guarantee outcomes. Use fear-based language to drive action. Recommend specific securities in mass communications.

## Compliance Requirements

- Include standard disclosure: "Past performance is not indicative of future results."
- Opinions are those of the author and subject to change without notice.
- Not a recommendation to buy or sell any security.
- Consult your financial advisor before making investment decisions.
- ADV disclosure or offer thereof.
- Pre-approval by compliance/CCO if firm requires it.
