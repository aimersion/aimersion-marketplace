Write a professional market commentary for client distribution. Type: $ARGUMENTS

Follow the market-commentator skill methodology:

1. Determine commentary type: weekly recap, quarterly letter, or volatility/crisis response.
2. Search for current market data and recent economic developments.
3. Structure the commentary:
   - Weekly: major index performance, key drivers, sector highlights, fixed income update, week ahead, advisor perspective (400-600 words)
   - Quarterly: quarter review, economic landscape, portfolio positioning rationale, outlook, planning opportunities (800-1,200 words)
   - Volatility: acknowledge situation, historical context, portfolio impact, advisor's approach, availability (300-500 words)
4. Write at an 8th-10th grade reading level for retail clients.
5. Use balanced, calm, long-term-focused tone. Attribute data sources. Use "we believe" for opinions.
6. Append the standard compliance disclosure block.

Never predict specific market levels or timing. Never guarantee outcomes. Never recommend specific securities in the commentary.
