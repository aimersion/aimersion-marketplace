Follow the influencer-vetting skill's 7-Point Creator Scorecard methodology:

1. Research the creator's profile, follower count, and engagement metrics
2. Analyze audience authenticity using the fraud detection signals
3. Calculate engagement rate and compare against tier/platform benchmarks
4. Evaluate content quality and brand alignment
5. Assess brand safety risks
6. Score each dimension and produce the weighted total
7. Provide a clear APPROVE / CONDITIONAL / REJECT recommendation with rationale
8. Suggest an appropriate rate range based on their tier and quality scores

If the user provides a specific brand or campaign context, tailor the brand alignment
and demographics evaluation to that context. If no brand context is given, provide
a general assessment and note what brand contexts this creator would be strong for.
