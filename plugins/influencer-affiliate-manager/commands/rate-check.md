Evaluate whether the influencer rate is fair for "$ARGUMENTS".

Use the rate card framework from the affiliate-program and influencer-vetting skills:

1. Identify the creator's tier based on follower count
2. Look up engagement rate benchmarks for their platform and tier
3. Calculate what the rate "should be" using multiple pricing models:
   - CPM-based: (estimated reach × platform CPM rate) + content creation premium
   - Engagement-based: follower count × engagement rate × rate per engagement
   - Industry benchmark: typical rates for this tier and platform

4. Compare the quoted rate against:
   - The calculated fair range
   - Industry benchmarks for the tier
   - The brand's target CPM or CPA goals

5. Provide a verdict:
   - FAIR: Rate is within 15% of calculated range
   - HIGH: Rate is 15-50% above range (suggest negotiation points)
   - OVERPRICED: Rate is 50%+ above range (suggest alternatives or counter-offer)
   - UNDERPRICED: Rate is below range (creator may be inexperienced or desperate —
     could be a great deal, but verify quality)

6. Suggest negotiation strategies if the rate is high:
   - Bundle additional deliverables
   - Offer longer-term commitment for volume discount
   - Propose hybrid compensation (lower flat fee + performance bonus)
   - Trade usage rights inclusions for rate reduction
