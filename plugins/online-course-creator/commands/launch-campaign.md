Plan a complete course launch campaign using the marketing-launcher skill for timelines, email sequences, webinar funnels, and sales page copy.

Determine the launch type:
1. **Full launch** — Complete pre-launch through post-launch sequence
2. **Email sequence only** — Launch or evergreen email series
3. **Webinar funnel** — Registration page, webinar script, follow-up sequence
4. **Sales page copy** — Full sales page with all conversion elements
5. **Evergreen funnel** — Automated, always-on sales system

Ask the user for:
- Course name, price, and target audience
- Launch dates and cart open/close timeline
- Current email list size and audience warmth
- Available channels (email, social, webinar, ads)
- Bonuses or special offers planned
- Testimonials or social proof available

Deliver the complete campaign plan including: timeline with daily tasks, full email sequence (subject lines and body copy), social media content calendar, webinar script outline (if applicable), and sales page copy framework. Follow the marketing-launcher skill's proven structures.

Save as `launch-plan-[course-name].md`
