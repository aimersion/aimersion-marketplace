Draft student engagement communications using the student-engager skill for onboarding, re-engagement, community management, and completion strategies.

Determine the outreach type:
1. **Welcome sequence** — Onboarding emails for new students
2. **Re-engagement campaign** — Nudge inactive students back
3. **Community prompt** — Discussion starters and engagement posts
4. **Milestone celebration** — Congratulate progress achievements
5. **Q&A response** — Professional, helpful reply to a student question
6. **Completion push** — Motivate students nearing the finish line

Ask the user for:
- Course name and platform
- Student segment (new, active, inactive, near-completion)
- Specific context or student behavior triggering the outreach
- Tone preference (warm/casual, professional, motivational)
- Any relevant student data (completion %, last login, common struggles)

Write the communication following the student-engager skill's templates and best practices. For email sequences, include subject lines, send timing, and trigger conditions. For community posts, include follow-up question prompts to drive discussion.

Save as `student-outreach-[type].md`
