# Platform Optimizer

Optimize the business strategy for online courses — pricing models, upsell sequences, bundle strategies, and conversion rate improvements.

## Pricing Strategies

### Price Point Guidelines
- **Mini-course** (1-2 hours): $27-$97
- **Standard course** (4-8 hours): $197-$497
- **Premium/Flagship course** (12-30+ hours): $497-$2,000
- **Cohort/coaching program**: $1,000-$5,000+
- **Certification program**: $2,000-$10,000+

### Pricing Psychology
- Use .97 or .00 endings (avoid .99 for premium positioning)
- Show value first, price second
- Offer payment plans (3-6 installments) — expect 40-60% take rate
- Compare to alternatives: "Less than $X per day" or "Costs less than one [relevant expense]"
- Stack bonuses to increase perceived value before revealing price

### Pricing Models
- **One-time purchase**: Simple, best for evergreen courses
- **Subscription/membership**: Recurring revenue, requires ongoing content
- **Tiered pricing**: Basic/Pro/Premium with increasing access levels
- **Cohort pricing**: Premium for live group experience with deadlines
- **Pay-what-you-want**: Good for audience building, not revenue optimization

## Upsell & Cross-Sell Sequences

### Upsell Ladder
1. **Free content** (blog, YouTube, podcast) → Email list
2. **Lead magnet** (guide, mini-course, challenge) → Trust building
3. **Tripwire** ($7-$47 low-ticket offer) → Buyer conversion
4. **Core course** ($197-$997) → Main revenue driver
5. **Premium program** ($1,000-$5,000 coaching/cohort) → High-value clients
6. **Ongoing membership** ($29-$99/month) → Recurring revenue

### Post-Purchase Upsells
- **Immediate upsell** (thank you page): Related product at 30-50% discount
- **Order bump** (checkout page): Small add-on ($27-$47) — templates, worksheets, bonus module
- **Downsell** (for cart abandoners): Payment plan or stripped-down version
- **Cross-sell email** (7-14 days after): Related course or next-level program

## Conversion Rate Optimization

### Sales Page Optimization
- **Headline testing**: Test transformation-focused vs. curiosity-based headlines
- **Social proof placement**: Place testimonials near pricing and CTA buttons
- **CTA button copy**: Test action verbs ("Start Learning" vs "Enroll Now" vs "Get Instant Access")
- **Video vs. text**: Test long-form sales letter vs. video sales letter
- **Guarantee prominence**: Make the money-back guarantee highly visible
- **Mobile optimization**: 50%+ of traffic is mobile — test the mobile experience

### Email Conversion
- Segment by engagement level and tailor messaging
- Personalize subject lines and opening lines
- A/B test send times (Tuesday-Thursday, 10am or 7pm typically perform well)
- Track click-through rates on each email in a launch sequence
- Send behavioral triggers (visited sales page but didn't buy)

## Bundle Strategies

### Bundle Types
- **Course + templates**: Add done-for-you resources
- **Course + community**: Add private group access
- **Course + coaching**: Add 1:1 or group coaching calls
- **Multi-course bundle**: Package related courses at a discount
- **Lifetime access bundle**: All current + future courses

### Bundle Pricing
- Price bundles at 60-70% of individual item total
- Show the "savings" prominently
- Make the bundle the default/recommended option
- Limit bundle availability for urgency

## Key Metrics to Track

- **Enrollment rate**: Visitors to sales page → purchases
- **Email open rate**: Target 30-40% for warm list
- **Email click rate**: Target 3-5% for sales emails
- **Completion rate**: Students who finish the course
- **Refund rate**: Target under 5%
- **Student lifetime value**: Total revenue per student across all products
- **Net Promoter Score**: Would students recommend the course?
