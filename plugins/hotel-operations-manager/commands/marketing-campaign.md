Plan a comprehensive hotel marketing campaign for: $ARGUMENTS

Follow these steps:

1. **Campaign brief** — Ask for or confirm:
   - Campaign objective (fill need dates, grow a segment, launch a package, drive direct bookings)
   - Target audience and guest profile
   - Budget range
   - Booking window and stay window dates
   - Property positioning and unique selling points

2. **Offer development** — Design the package or promotion:
   - Core offer structure (what's included, pricing approach)
   - Rate strategy and value perception
   - Booking incentives and urgency elements
   - Upsell opportunities

3. **Channel plan** — Map out distribution across:
   - Direct website (landing page, booking engine setup)
   - Email marketing (segmentation, sequence, subject lines)
   - Social media (platform-specific content, paid promotion)
   - OTA (special offer listings, visibility programs)
   - PR and partnerships (media pitches, influencer outreach)
   - Paid advertising (Google Hotel Ads, metasearch, display)

4. **Content calendar** — Create a week-by-week content and activity calendar for the campaign duration.

5. **Success metrics** — Define KPIs, tracking methods, and targets.

Format as a professional campaign brief document. Save to `campaign-[name].md`.
