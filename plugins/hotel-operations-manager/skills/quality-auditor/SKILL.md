# Hotel Quality Auditing

Maintain and elevate property standards through systematic inspections, guest satisfaction tracking, and continuous improvement programs.

## Quality Inspection Framework

### Room Inspection Checklist Categories

Score each area on a 1-5 scale (1=unacceptable, 3=meets standard, 5=exceptional):

1. **Entrance & Hallway**: Door condition, lock function, peephole, hallway cleanliness, signage
2. **Bedroom**: Bed presentation, linen quality, pillow arrangement, furniture condition, lighting function, clock/alarm
3. **Bathroom**: Fixture cleanliness, grout condition, amenity placement, towel presentation, water pressure/temperature, drain function
4. **Amenities & Supplies**: Minibar stocked, stationery complete, TV/remote function, in-room technology (thermostat, lighting controls)
5. **Cleanliness Detail**: Dust (surfaces, vents, baseboards), spots (mirrors, glass, chrome), odors, carpet/floor condition
6. **Safety & Compliance**: Smoke detector, sprinkler visibility, emergency info posted, balcony safety, electrical safety

### Scoring Methodology

- **Score 90-100%**: Exceeds brand standard — recognize the team
- **Score 80-89%**: Meets standard — minor touch-ups needed
- **Score 70-79%**: Below standard — corrective action within 24 hours
- **Below 70%**: Unacceptable — room pulled from inventory until remediated

### Public Area Inspection

Inspect daily: lobby, elevators, corridors, restrooms, pool/fitness, parking, exterior.
Focus areas: cleanliness, lighting, temperature comfort, music/ambiance, signage, furniture condition, odors.

## Guest Satisfaction Tracking

### Key Metrics Dashboard

Track these metrics with trending (MTD, QTD, YTD vs. prior year):

| Metric | Source | Frequency | Target |
|--------|--------|-----------|--------|
| Overall Satisfaction | Post-stay survey | Daily | >85% top-2 box |
| Net Promoter Score | Post-stay survey | Monthly | >50 |
| TripAdvisor Rating | Public | Weekly | >4.3 / 5.0 |
| Google Rating | Public | Weekly | >4.3 / 5.0 |
| Booking.com Score | OTA | Weekly | >8.5 / 10 |
| Review Volume | All platforms | Monthly | >X reviews/month |
| Response Rate | All platforms | Weekly | >90% |
| Complaint Resolution Time | Internal | Weekly | <2 hours |

### Guest Feedback Analysis

Categorize feedback themes for actionable insights:

- **Room quality**: Cleanliness, comfort, maintenance, amenities, noise
- **Service**: Staff friendliness, responsiveness, knowledge, personalization
- **F&B**: Food quality, variety, value, service speed, ambiance
- **Facilities**: Pool, fitness, spa, parking, common areas
- **Value**: Rate vs. perceived quality, hidden fees, comparison to expectations
- **Location**: Accessibility, neighborhood, transportation, local recommendations

### Complaint Escalation Matrix

| Complaint Type | Response Owner | Escalation If Unresolved | Compensation Authority |
|---------------|---------------|------------------------|----------------------|
| Room cleanliness | Housekeeping supervisor | FOM within 30 min | Amenity/discount up to $50 |
| Maintenance issue | Engineering lead | GM if safety-related | Room move + amenity |
| Service failure | Department manager | GM within 2 hours | Up to 1 night comp |
| Billing dispute | Front office manager | Controller | Adjustment per policy |
| Safety/security | Security + GM | Ownership immediately | Full refund authority |

## Continuous Improvement Program

### Monthly Quality Meeting Agenda

1. Review inspection scores by area — identify trends
2. Analyze guest satisfaction metrics — compare to comp set
3. Review top 5 complaint themes with root cause analysis
4. Recognize top-performing team members
5. Set improvement priorities for next 30 days with owners and deadlines

### Service Recovery ROI

Track service recovery outcomes: guests who experienced a problem AND received excellent recovery are often more loyal than guests with no problems. Monitor "problem experience resolved" scores separately from overall satisfaction.
