# Hotel Marketing Planning

Drive demand and shift booking mix toward higher-margin channels through strategic marketing campaigns, OTA optimization, and direct booking initiatives.

## Campaign Planning Framework

### Seasonal Campaign Calendar

Map campaigns to demand patterns:

- **Q1 (Jan-Mar)**: New Year wellness packages, Valentine's/romance, spring break family, business travel restart
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun)**: Wedding season, graduation celebrations, early summer leisure, corporate retreats
- **Q3 (Jul-Sep)**: Peak summer leisure, back-to-school family getaways, Labor Day, shoulder season teasers
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec)**: Fall foliage, holiday parties, Thanksgiving, Christmas packages, NYE galas, gift cards

### Campaign Brief Template

For each campaign:

1. **Objective** — what you want to achieve (fill need dates, grow segment, shift channel mix, increase TRevPAR)
2. **Target audience** — who this appeals to (demographics, psychographics, past guest segments)
3. **Offer structure** — what the package/promotion includes, pricing, booking window, stay window
4. **Channels** — where it will be promoted (website, email, social, OTA, PR, paid ads)
5. **Creative needs** — photography, copy, landing page, booking engine setup
6. **Budget** — media spend, creative costs, commission/discount cost
7. **Success metrics** — bookings, revenue, ROI, channel shift

## OTA Optimization

### Listing Quality Checklist

- **Photography**: Minimum 25 high-quality images including hero exterior, lobby, each room type, bathroom, amenities, dining, pool/fitness, local area
- **Description**: Compelling property description (250-400 words) highlighting unique selling points, recent renovations, location advantages
- **Room types**: Each room type has distinct photos, accurate descriptions, and correct amenity tags
- **Policies**: Cancellation, check-in/out, pet, parking policies are current and competitive
- **Rates**: Rate parity maintained across channels; packages/value-adds differentiated on direct
- **Reviews**: Response rate >90%, response time <48 hours, professional tone

### OTA Ranking Factors

Improve visibility by optimizing:

- **Conversion rate**: Better photos and descriptions increase look-to-book ratio
- **Price competitiveness**: Rate Shopper scores within comp set
- **Content score**: Complete profile, all amenities tagged, recent photos
- **Review score**: Overall rating AND recency of reviews
- **Commission level**: Higher commission = higher visibility on some platforms (evaluate ROI carefully)
- **Availability**: Fewer restrictions = more search result appearances

## Direct Booking Strategy

Shift bookings from OTA (15-25% commission) to direct channels:

1. **Best rate guarantee** — match or beat OTA rates on your website, prominently displayed
2. **Exclusive direct perks** — offer value-adds only on direct: free parking, room upgrade, F&B credit, late checkout
3. **Retargeting** — pixel website visitors and serve ads reminding them to book direct
4. **Email marketing** — nurture past guests with personalized offers and loyalty benefits
5. **Metasearch** — bid on Google Hotel Ads, TripAdvisor, Trivago to capture shoppers at point of comparison
6. **Social proof** — showcase reviews and UGC on your website booking flow

## Social Media Content Strategy

Content pillars for hotel social media:

- **Property showcase** (30%): Room reveals, amenity highlights, seasonal decor, renovations
- **Guest experience** (25%): Guest stories (with permission), celebrations, behind-the-scenes
- **Local/destination** (25%): Area attractions, events, restaurant recommendations, seasonal activities
- **Offers & promotions** (15%): Packages, flash sales, loyalty perks, gift cards
- **Team spotlight** (5%): Staff stories, milestones, culture, community involvement
