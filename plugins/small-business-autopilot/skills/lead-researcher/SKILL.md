# Lead Researcher

Find, qualify, and organize potential customers based on the user's ideal customer profile.

## Core Workflow

1. **Define the ideal customer** — industry, location, size, pain points, budget signals
2. **Research prospects** — use web search and connected tools to find matching businesses
3. **Qualify and score** — evaluate each prospect against qualification criteria
4. **Deliver the list** — organized, scored, with contact context and outreach angles

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Framework

Before researching, establish the ICP with the user:

- **Industry/vertical**: What type of businesses are they targeting?
- **Location**: Geographic focus (city, state, region, or national)?
- **Company size**: Solo, small team (2–10), or mid-size (10–50)?
- **Pain points**: What problem does the user's product/service solve?
- **Budget signals**: Revenue range, funding stage, growth indicators?
- **Decision maker**: Who buys — owner, manager, specific role?

## Research Methodology

### Finding Prospects
Use web search to find businesses matching the ICP:
- "[Industry] + [City/Region]" — find local businesses
- "[Industry] + "looking for" + [user's service category]" — find active buyers
- "[Industry association] + members + [location]" — find organized businesses
- "Best [industry] in [city]" — find prominent local players
- Search LinkedIn for company pages matching industry and location
- Search X for business owners discussing relevant pain points

### Qualifying Prospects
Score each prospect on these dimensions (1–5 scale):

| Dimension | What to Look For |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Fit** | Matches ICP on industry, size, location |
| **Need** | Shows signs of the problem you solve |
| **Budget** | Revenue/funding suggests they can afford you |
| **Timing** | Recent changes suggest they're ready to buy |
| **Access** | Contact information is findable |

**Score thresholds**:
- 20–25: Hot lead — prioritize outreach
- 15–19: Warm lead — worth pursuing
- 10–14: Cool lead — nurture with content
- Below 10: Not qualified — skip

### Timing Signals (Indicators of Buying Readiness)
- Recently hired for a role related to your service
- Just raised funding or announced growth
- Posted about a problem you solve
- Website is outdated (if you sell web/marketing services)
- Competitor just launched — they may need to respond
- Seasonal factors in their industry

## Deliverable Format

Present leads in a structured format:

```
# Prospect Research Report
Date: [date]
ICP: [summary of ideal customer]
Prospects found: [n]

## Hot Leads (Score 20+)

### [Business Name]
- Industry: [industry]
- Location: [city, state]
- Size: [estimated]
- Score: [n/25]
- Why they're a fit: [1-2 sentences]
- Outreach angle: [suggested approach]
- Contact: [name, role if found]
- Website: [URL]

## Warm Leads (Score 15–19)
[same format]

## Cool Leads (Score 10–14)
[condensed format — name, industry, score, one-line note]
```

## Outreach Context

For each hot lead, include a suggested outreach angle:
- What specific pain point to reference
- Any mutual connections or shared context
- A relevant piece of the user's content or case study to share
- Suggested channel (email, LinkedIn, phone, in-person)
