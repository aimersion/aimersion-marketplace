# /pricing-intel [competitor or market]

Research competitive pricing using the pricing-intelligence skill.

## Instructions

1. Read the `skills/pricing-intelligence/SKILL.md` to load the pricing analysis framework
2. If a specific competitor was named, focus the research on them. If a market/category was named, do a landscape-level pricing comparison across top competitors.
3. Research pricing from multiple sources (pricing pages, reviews, community discussions)
4. Analyze pricing architecture: value metric, tier structure, billing model, entry point, expansion model
5. Build the side-by-side comparison
6. Assess total cost of ownership at multiple scales
7. Generate sales-specific pricing talk tracks
8. Deliver the full Pricing Intelligence Report
9. Flag any pricing data that's uncertain or potentially outdated
