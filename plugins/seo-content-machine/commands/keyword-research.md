Research keywords for the topic: $ARGUMENTS

Follow the keyword-researcher skill methodology. Complete these steps:

1. **Expand the seed topic** into 25-40 keyword variations including:
   - Long-tail phrases and question keywords (mine "People Also Ask" from Google)
   - Comparison keywords ("[topic] vs", "[topic] alternative")
   - Problem keywords ("[topic] not working", "fix [topic]")
   - Audience-specific ("[topic] for beginners", "[topic] for enterprise")
   - Modifier stacks ("best [topic]", "free [topic]", "[topic] 2026")

2. **SERP-validate the top 10-15 keywords:** For each, use web search to check:
   - What content format actually ranks (guide, listicle, comparison, tool)
   - What SERP features appear (AI Overview, featured snippet, PAA, video)
   - Who ranks (high-authority sites only, or are smaller sites ranking too?)
   - What the current top results miss (content gaps)

3. **Classify each keyword** by detailed intent:
   - Informational-Learn, Informational-Do, Commercial Investigation, Transactional

4. **Assess real difficulty** based on who actually ranks (not an arbitrary score):
   - Easy (thin content/forums ranking), Medium (mixed competition), Hard (authority sites dominate), Not Worth It (no click opportunity)

5. **Estimate volume** through proxy signals (autocomplete behavior, ad presence, result count) and note click opportunity separately (AI Overviews can make high-volume keywords zero-click).

6. **Build topic clusters** with:
   - A pillar keyword and recommended page format
   - 4-8 supporting keywords with specific content recommendations
   - A "money keyword" each cluster serves
   - Internal linking architecture

7. **Score and rank** using the priority matrix: Business Relevance (30%), Click Opportunity (25%), Difficulty (20%), Conversion Potential (15%), Content Gap (10%).

8. **Identify featured snippet opportunities** — keywords where a snippet exists and the current holder is beatable.

Present the results as:
- Executive summary (3-5 sentences)
- Primary keywords table (Keyword | Intent | Volume | Click Opportunity | Difficulty | SERP Features | Priority)
- Topic clusters with publishing plans
- Top 5 quick wins
- Featured snippet opportunities
- Strategic recommendations with realistic timeline expectations

Save the report as a markdown file.
