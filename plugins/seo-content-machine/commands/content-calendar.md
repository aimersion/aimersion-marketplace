Generate a strategic monthly content calendar.

Follow the content-planner skill methodology. Complete these steps:

1. **Gather context:** Ask the user about:
   - Business goals for this month (traffic growth, lead generation, brand authority, product launch)
   - Priority topics or keyword clusters to target
   - Publishing capacity (how many posts per week — be realistic)
   - Any upcoming events, launches, or seasonal factors
   - Existing content that may need refreshing

2. **Build topic cluster architecture:**
   - Define 1-2 primary clusters to build this month
   - For each cluster: pillar page + 4-6 supporting post keywords
   - Map the internal linking structure
   - Identify the "money page" each cluster drives traffic to

3. **Determine the content mix** based on goals:
   - Growth-focused: 40% informational, 20% quick wins, 20% pillar, 10% commercial, 10% refreshes
   - Conversion-focused: 25% commercial, 25% informational+CTA, 20% conversion, 15% refreshes, 15% pillar
   - Authority-focused: 30% original research, 25% pillar, 20% thought leadership, 15% supporting, 10% refreshes

4. **Build the calendar** with:
   - Target publish week, working title, primary keyword, intent, content format
   - Word count target (based on SERP analysis, not arbitrary)
   - Cluster assignment and internal linking plan
   - Schema markup type to implement
   - Featured snippet target (if applicable)
   - CTA for each piece
   - New content vs. refresh designation

5. **Sequence strategically:**
   - Pillar pages before supporting cluster posts
   - Quick wins early to build momentum
   - Conversion content aligned with campaigns
   - Refreshes between new content sprints
   - No more than 2 similar topics in the same week

6. **Add a content refresh schedule:**
   - Identify 3-5 existing posts showing decay signals or outdated information
   - Specify what type of refresh each needs (light, medium, heavy)
   - Schedule refreshes into the calendar

7. **Set KPI targets** with 30-day and 90-day benchmarks per content type.

8. **Preview next month** with 3-5 content ideas already identified.

Present as: strategy summary, topic cluster map, monthly calendar table, content refresh schedule, internal linking plan, KPI targets, and next month preview. Save as a markdown file.
