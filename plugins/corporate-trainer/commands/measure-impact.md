Measure training impact using the roi-calculator skill for ROI frameworks, data collection methods, and stakeholder reporting.

Determine the focus:
1. **ROI calculation** — Calculate return on investment for a completed training
2. **Impact measurement plan** — Design data collection for an upcoming training
3. **Business case** — Build justification for training investment
4. **Stakeholder report** — Present training results to leadership

Ask for: training program details, costs (direct and indirect), business metrics affected, baseline data, and post-training results (if available).

Deliver quantified analysis with clear methodology and visual-ready data for stakeholder presentations. Use Phillips ROI methodology and Kirkpatrick levels.

Save as `training-impact-[program].md`
