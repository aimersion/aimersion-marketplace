Create a marketing plan for a tutoring business using the marketing-generator skill for local SEO, referral programs, seasonal campaigns, and social media strategies.

Determine the marketing focus:
1. **Full marketing plan** — Comprehensive strategy across all channels
2. **Seasonal campaign** — Targeted campaign for a specific time of year
3. **Referral program** — Design a client referral system
4. **Local SEO content** — Website pages and blog posts for local search
5. **Social media plan** — Content calendar and post templates

Ask the user for:
- Business name and location
- Subjects and grade levels served
- Current client base size and target growth
- Budget for marketing (if any)
- Current marketing channels in use
- Biggest marketing challenge or goal
- Differentiators (unique approach, credentials, results)

Deliver actionable, specific marketing content — not just strategy. Include actual copy for emails, social posts, website pages, or flyer text. Follow the marketing-generator skill's seasonal calendar and content pillars.

Save as `marketing-plan-[focus].md`
