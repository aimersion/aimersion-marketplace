Generate a progress report for tutoring parents using the parent-reporter skill for structure, tone, and formatting.

Determine the report type:
1. **Session summary** — Quick post-session update (under 150 words)
2. **Monthly progress report** — Comprehensive monthly update with goal tracking
3. **Quarterly comprehensive report** — Detailed assessment with data and visuals
4. **Goal update** — Focused communication about specific goal progress

Ask the user for:
- Student name and subject(s)
- Reporting period
- Goals being tracked and current status
- Specific progress or milestones to highlight
- Challenges or areas needing continued focus
- Recommendations for home support
- Upcoming focus areas

Follow the parent-reporter skill's tone guidelines: lead with positives, be specific with examples, frame challenges as next steps, and celebrate effort. Include specific evidence for every claim about progress.

Save as `progress-report-[student]-[date].md`
