Optimize tutoring scheduling and tutor-student matching using the scheduling-optimizer skill for capacity planning, match criteria, and cancellation management.

Determine the optimization focus:
1. **Tutor-student matching** — Match a new student with the best-fit tutor
2. **Schedule optimization** — Reorganize weekly schedule for maximum efficiency
3. **Capacity planning** — Analyze utilization and plan for growth
4. **Cancellation policy** — Design or refine cancellation and no-show policies
5. **Group session planning** — Form and schedule small group sessions

Ask the user for:
- Current schedule information (tutors, students, time slots)
- Tutor availability and subject expertise
- Student needs and preferences
- Current pain points (gaps, conflicts, cancellations)
- Business goals (revenue target, student count, utilization rate)

Provide specific, actionable recommendations with implementation steps. For matching, present 2-3 tutor options with rationale. For schedule optimization, show a revised weekly grid. For capacity planning, include utilization metrics and hiring triggers.

Save as `schedule-optimization-[focus].md`
