# Scheduling Optimizer

Optimize tutoring session scheduling, tutor-student matching, and business capacity to maximize revenue and student outcomes.

## Tutor-Student Matching

### Matching Criteria (Priority Order)
1. **Subject expertise** — Tutor's strongest subjects match student needs
2. **Grade level experience** — Tutor is experienced with the student's age group
3. **Availability alignment** — Tutor and student/parent schedules overlap
4. **Teaching style fit** — Match tutor's approach to student's learning preferences
5. **Personality compatibility** — Energy levels, communication style, patience
6. **Logistics** — Location (if in-person), timezone (if online)

### Matching Process
1. Review the student's assessment results and learning profile
2. Identify 2-3 potential tutors who meet subject and availability requirements
3. Consider the student's learning style and personality
4. Make the match and brief the tutor on the student's profile
5. Check in after 2-3 sessions to confirm the match is working
6. Be willing to re-match if the fit isn't right

## Schedule Optimization

### Peak Demand Windows
- **After school** (3:00-6:00 PM weekdays): Highest demand, premium scheduling
- **Early evening** (6:00-8:00 PM weekdays): Strong demand, family-friendly
- **Saturday morning** (9:00 AM-12:00 PM): Popular for test prep and enrichment
- **Sunday afternoon** (1:00-5:00 PM): Moderate demand, homework help
- **Before school** (7:00-8:00 AM): Niche demand, motivated students

### Scheduling Best Practices
- Block sessions in consistent weekly time slots (same day/time each week)
- Build 10-15 minute buffers between sessions for notes and preparation
- Group same-subject students in adjacent time slots for tutor efficiency
- Reserve 1-2 open slots per week for makeup sessions and new students
- Schedule assessments during lower-demand times

### Capacity Planning
- Calculate maximum weekly sessions per tutor (factor in prep time)
- Target 75-85% utilization rate (leave room for cancellations and growth)
- Track session fill rates by day/time to identify expansion opportunities
- Hire or expand hours when utilization consistently exceeds 85%

## Cancellation & No-Show Management

### Prevention Strategies
- Send automated reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before sessions
- Implement a clear cancellation policy (24-48 hours notice required)
- Charge for late cancellations or no-shows (after first occurrence)
- Offer easy rescheduling options (online booking, text/app)

### Policy Template
> **Cancellation Policy**: Please provide at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) and no-shows will be charged at the full session rate. One courtesy cancellation is provided per semester. Makeup sessions are available based on tutor availability.

### Tracking Metrics
- Cancellation rate by student (flag patterns above 15%)
- No-show rate (target below 5%)
- Makeup session utilization
- Revenue impact of cancellations

## Group Session Scheduling

### Small Group Benefits
- Lower cost per student = accessible to more families
- Peer learning and social motivation
- Higher tutor revenue per hour
- Natural waiting list pipeline for 1:1 services

### Group Formation Guidelines
- Maximum 4 students per group (3 is ideal)
- Students within 1 grade level of each other
- Similar skill levels (based on assessment)
- Compatible schedules
- Same subject and learning objectives
