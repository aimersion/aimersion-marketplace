Build a comprehensive wedding day timeline for: $ARGUMENTS

Follow these steps:

1. **Gather wedding details** — Ask for: ceremony time, ceremony and reception locations (same or different?), guest count, wedding party size, photography style preferences (first look or traditional), any cultural or religious ceremony elements, reception end time.

2. **Build the master timeline** — Create three interlocking timelines following the timeline-builder skill:
   - Couple & wedding party timeline (getting ready through send-off)
   - Vendor arrival and service schedule
   - Guest experience flow

3. **Customize for their wedding** — Adjust timing based on wedding style (intimate, classic, destination, cultural), venue logistics, and any special elements (live painting, food trucks, late-night snacks, after-party).

4. **Add contingency notes** — Include buffer times, rain plan triggers, and emergency adjustments.

5. **Deliver** — Format as a clean, printable timeline document. Save to `wedding-timeline-[date].md`.
