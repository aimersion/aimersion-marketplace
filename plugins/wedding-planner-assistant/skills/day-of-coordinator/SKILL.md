# Day-of Wedding Coordination

Produce comprehensive execution documents that ensure flawless wedding day logistics for the coordinator, vendors, venue staff, and wedding party.

## Run Sheet Structure

### Ceremony Run Sheet

Create a minute-by-minute ceremony document including:

**Pre-Ceremony Setup** (2 hours before)
- Vendor arrival check-in times
- Decor and floral placement map
- Sound system test (microphones, speakers, ceremony music)
- Program and seating chart distribution
- Reserved seating placement (family, VIPs)

**Processional Order**
Number each person in walk order with their escort, music cue, and position:

| Order | Who | Escorted By | Music Cue | Position |
|-------|-----|------------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Officiant | Enters from side | N/A | Center altar |
| 2 | Groom + parents | From right | Seating music | Right of altar |
| 3 | Bridesmaids | Solo or paired | Processional | Left side |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |
| Last | Bride + escort | From rear | Bridal march | Center aisle |

**Ceremony Flow**
- Welcome and opening remarks
- Readings (who reads what, in what order)
- Vows (memorized or prompted by officiant?)
- Ring exchange
- Unity ceremony (if applicable — sand, candle, wine, etc.)
- Pronouncement and kiss
- Recessional order (reverse of processional)

**Post-Ceremony**
- Receiving line (if applicable) with line order
- Guest transition instructions (cocktail hour location, transportation)
- Family formal photo list (pre-planned combinations to save time)

### Reception Run Sheet

Detailed timeline with responsible party for each moment:

| Time | Event | Music/Song | Responsible | Notes |
|------|-------|-----------|-------------|-------|
| 6:30 PM | Doors open | Background cocktail | Venue staff | Check table settings |
| 6:45 PM | Guests seated | Transition music | DJ | Dim lights |
| 7:00 PM | Grand entrance | [Specific song] | DJ | Announce names |
| 7:05 PM | First dance | [Specific song] | DJ | Clear floor |

## Emergency Kit Checklist

Pack and bring a day-of emergency kit containing:

**Wardrobe Emergencies**: Safety pins (various sizes), sewing kit (white, black, ivory thread), fashion tape, stain remover pen, steamer, extra buttons, clear nail polish (for stocking runs), heel protectors for grass

**Health & Comfort**: Pain reliever, antacid, allergy medication, bandages, blister pads, tissues, mints/breath strips, deodorant, tampons/pads, eye drops, sunscreen, bug spray

**Beauty Touch-Ups**: Bobby pins, hair ties, hairspray, makeup blotting papers, lip color, pressed powder, nail file, tweezers, hand lotion

**Tools & Supplies**: Scissors, tape (clear + duct), zip ties, super glue, Sharpie markers, pens, phone chargers (multiple types), extension cords, batteries, flashlight, umbrella, lint roller

**Documents**: Extra copies of timeline, vendor contact list, emergency contacts, marriage license, seating chart, song list, vow copies
