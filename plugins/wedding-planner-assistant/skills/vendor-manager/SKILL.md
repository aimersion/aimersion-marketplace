# Wedding Vendor Management

Systematically evaluate, book, and manage vendor relationships to deliver the best experience within budget.

## Vendor Evaluation Framework

### Comparison Criteria by Category

Evaluate each vendor on these dimensions (weight by client priority):

| Criterion | Weight | Score 1-5 | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Portfolio quality / style match | 25% | | Does their work align with the couple's vision? |
| Reviews and references | 20% | | Online reviews + reference calls |
| Price and value | 20% | | Total cost vs. what's included |
| Availability on wedding date | Pass/Fail | | Confirmed available? |
| Personality and communication | 15% | | Easy to work with? Responsive? |
| Experience level | 10% | | Years, number of weddings, venue familiarity |
| Backup plan / insurance | 10% | | What if they can't make it? |

## Contract Review Checklist

Before signing any vendor contract, verify:

**Essential Terms**
- [ ] Wedding date, start/end times, and location clearly stated
- [ ] Detailed description of services/deliverables included
- [ ] Total price with payment schedule and accepted payment methods
- [ ] Deposit amount and refund policy
- [ ] Cancellation terms for both parties
- [ ] Force majeure / Act of God clause

**Protection Clauses**
- [ ] Liability insurance (proof of coverage)
- [ ] Backup vendor/substitute policy if primary can't attend
- [ ] Image/content usage rights (especially photographers and videographers)
- [ ] Overtime rates clearly defined
- [ ] Damage/breakage policy (rentals, venue)

**Logistics**
- [ ] Setup and breakdown times
- [ ] Vendor meal requirements
- [ ] Parking/loading requirements
- [ ] Noise restrictions or curfews
- [ ] Subcontractor approval (can they send someone else?)

### Red Flags in Vendor Contracts

- No cancellation or refund terms
- 100% payment required upfront
- No liability insurance
- Vague deliverable descriptions ("standard package")
- Automatic ownership of all images/content
- Penalty clauses that seem disproportionate
- No force majeure clause

## Payment Tracking

Track all vendor payments with:

| Vendor | Total Cost | Deposit Paid | Deposit Date | Balance Due | Due Date | Payment Method | Status |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-------------|------------|---------|---------------|--------|

Standard payment schedule: 25-50% deposit at booking, 25-50% at 30 days before, final balance 1-2 weeks before or day-of per contract.
