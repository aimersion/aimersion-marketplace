Generate a comprehensive product launch plan. This combines timeline building, audience strategy, content planning, and pricing into one complete deliverable.

## Steps

1. **Gather Context** — Ask the user about:
   - Product type (SaaS, e-commerce, DTC, Amazon, crowdfunding, digital product)
   - Target launch date (or flexible timing)
   - Current stage (idea, MVP, beta, ready to ship)
   - Target audience and market
   - Team size and budget level
   - Existing audience (email list, social, customers)

2. **Generate Launch Timeline** — Use the launch-timeline-builder skill to create a week-by-week plan customized to their product type and constraints.

3. **Build Pricing Strategy** — Use the pricing-strategist skill to recommend a pricing approach with launch-specific tactics (early bird tiers, competitive positioning).

4. **Create Audience Building Plan** — Use the audience-builder skill to design a pre-launch audience strategy with email sequences and referral programs.

5. **Plan Launch Content** — Use the launch-content-planner skill to generate a content calendar across email, social, and PR channels.

6. **Run Readiness Check** — Use the launch-readiness-auditor skill to identify gaps and create a fix-it list.

## Output

Deliver a comprehensive launch plan document that the user can use as their operating playbook. Include:

```
# Complete Launch Plan: [Product Name]
## Launch Date: [Date]

### 1. Executive Summary
[Product, audience, strategy, timeline overview]

### 2. Launch Timeline
[Week-by-week plan with milestones and dependencies]

### 3. Pricing Strategy
[Pricing tiers, launch discounts, competitive positioning]

### 4. Audience Building Plan
[Pre-launch tactics, email sequences, referral program]

### 5. Content Calendar
[Email, social, PR content plan by week]

### 6. Launch Day Playbook
[Hour-by-hour launch day execution plan]

### 7. Post-Launch Plan
[30-60-90 day action plan]

### 8. Success Metrics
[KPIs, targets, and tracking plan]

### 9. Risk Register
[Top risks and mitigation strategies]
```

If the user has calendar connected, offer to create calendar events for major milestones.
