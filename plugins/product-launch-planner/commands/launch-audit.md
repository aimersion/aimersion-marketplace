Run a launch readiness audit that scores your preparation across 8 critical dimensions and generates a prioritized fix-it list.

## Steps

1. **Product Context** — Ask what they're launching, when, and their target audience.

2. **Walk Through 8 Dimensions** — Conversationally assess each dimension:
   - Product Readiness (20%)
   - Market Validation (15%)
   - Audience & Distribution (15%)
   - Messaging & Positioning (10%)
   - Pricing & Monetization (10%)
   - Content & Marketing (10%)
   - Operations & Support (10%)
   - Analytics & Measurement (10%)

3. **Score Each Dimension** (1-5 scale) based on the user's responses.

4. **Calculate Weighted Score** and determine Go/No-Go recommendation.

5. **Generate Fix-It List** — Prioritized by impact, with specific actions and time estimates.

## Output

Deliver the full audit scorecard with dimension-by-dimension scoring, critical gaps, and a prioritized action plan. Include the recommendation: Go, Delay (with new timeline), or Restructure.

If social or email platforms are connected, check actual content/list status to make scoring more accurate.
