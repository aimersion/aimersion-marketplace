Run a structured post-launch retrospective that analyzes performance, identifies root causes, and generates a 30-60-90 day improvement plan.

## Steps

1. **Gather Launch Data** — Ask the user for:
   - Launch goals vs. actual results
   - Traffic, signup, and revenue numbers
   - Customer feedback themes
   - What they think went well and what didn't

2. **Pull Connected Data** — If platforms are connected:
   - Email campaign metrics (open rates, CTR, conversions)
   - Social media engagement data
   - Page insights and reach

3. **Build Scorecard** — Compare goals to actuals with Red/Amber/Green status for each metric.

4. **Run Retrospective** — Walk through:
   - What worked (continue doing)
   - What didn't work (stop doing)
   - What was missing (start doing)
   - What surprised you

5. **Root Cause Analysis** — For each Red metric, identify contributing factors and root causes.

6. **Customer Feedback Synthesis** — Categorize feedback into: feature requests, bugs, confusion points, praise signals.

7. **Generate Action Plan** — Create prioritized 30-60-90 day improvement plan.

## Output

Deliver a comprehensive post-launch analysis report with scorecard, retrospective findings, root cause analysis, and a time-bound action plan.
