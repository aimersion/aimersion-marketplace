# Audience Builder

Create pre-launch audience building plans that convert strangers into eager buyers before you even launch. The #1 predictor of launch success is the size and quality of your pre-launch audience.

## Why Pre-Launch Audience Building Matters

Campaigns that hit 30% of their funding goal in the first 48 hours have >90% success rate. Amazon products need sales velocity in the first 8 days to capture algorithmic ranking. SaaS products with 1,000+ waitlist signups convert at 25%+ on launch day. The math is clear: build the audience first, then launch.

## Gathering Context

Before creating an audience building plan, understand:

1. **Product type and launch date** — what are we building audience for, and how much time do we have?
2. **Target customer** — who specifically are we trying to reach? (demographics, psychographics, where they hang out)
3. **Existing audience** — email list size, social following, customer base, personal network size
4. **Budget** — can they run paid ads, or is this organic-only?
5. **Unique angle** — what's the compelling story/hook that would make someone want to join early?

## Audience Building Strategies

### Strategy 1: Waitlist / Landing Page Funnel

The foundation of any pre-launch audience. Build this first, then drive traffic to it.

**Landing Page Elements (target: 15-40% conversion rate):**
- Clear headline communicating the transformation/benefit
- Subheadline with specificity ("Join 2,847 founders getting early access")
- Visual element: product screenshot, demo video, or compelling imagery
- Minimal signup form (email only — every extra field drops conversion 10-15%)
- Social proof: testimonial quotes, logo bar, signup count
- Progress indicator ("Your spot: #2,847 in line" — increases urgency)
- Mobile-responsive design (50%+ of traffic will be mobile)

**Lead Magnet Options (by conversion rate):**
- Interactive quiz related to the product problem: 20-40% conversion
- Calculator or assessment tool: 30-70% higher conversion than static content
- Free mini-course or video training: 15-25% conversion
- PDF guide or checklist: 10-20% conversion
- Webinar registration: up to 70% conversion (but lower attendance)

### Strategy 2: Email Nurture Sequence

Once someone joins the waitlist, nurture them from "curious" to "ready to buy."

**5-Stage Pre-Launch Email Sequence:**

| Email | Timing | Subject | Content Focus |
|-------|--------|---------|---------------|
| 1. Welcome | Immediate | "You're in! Here's what's coming..." | Thank them, set expectations, share the vision |
| 2. Story | Day 3-5 | "Why we're building [product]..." | Origin story, founder journey, the problem that drove you to build this |
| 3. Education | Weekly (wks 2-5) | Educational content on problem space | Valuable content that positions you as the expert — no selling |
| 4. Sneak Peek | Week 4-5 | "First look: here's what [product] does..." | Feature previews, beta screenshots, early testimonial |
| 5. Launch Prep | Week 7-8 | "[Product] launches in [X] days — your exclusive offer" | Early bird pricing, exclusive bonuses, FAQ, clear CTA |

**Email Benchmarks to Track:**
- Open rate: target 30-40% (pre-launch lists should be highly engaged)
- Click-through rate: target 5-10%
- Waitlist-to-customer conversion: target 25%+

### Strategy 3: Referral / Ambassador Program

Turn your early signups into a growth engine. Well-designed referral programs generate 10-30% of total revenue.

**Referral Program Structure:**
- **Dual-sided rewards** (86% of successful programs use this — reward both referrer AND referred friend)
- **Minimum reward expectation:** customers expect at least $21 or 11% discount as baseline
- **Tiered escalation:** First 5 referrals = reward A, 6-10 = reward B, 11+ = reward C (27% more referrals than flat-rate)

**Reward Types by Product:**
- **SaaS:** Free months, extended trials, feature upgrades
- **E-commerce:** Store credit, exclusive products, first-access
- **Digital products:** Higher tier access, bonus content, 1-on-1 time with creator
- **Crowdfunding:** Move up in queue, unlock exclusive reward tier

**Referral Conversion Benchmarks:**
- Pet products: 8.58% median
- Food & beverage: 7.90%
- Software/SaaS: 7.86%
- Apparel/Fashion: 5.40%
- Target: 8%+ to join top performers

### Strategy 4: Community Building

Build a community around the problem your product solves, not the product itself.

**Community Platforms by Audience:**
- Slack/Discord: developers, tech-savvy users, B2B
- Facebook Groups: consumers, local businesses, niche interests
- Reddit (subreddit): niche enthusiasts, research-oriented audiences
- Circle/Mighty Networks: course creators, premium communities

**Community Growth Tactics:**
- Share genuinely valuable content 80% of the time, mention product 20%
- Run weekly AMAs or discussion threads
- Feature community members and their stories
- Create exclusive "founding member" benefits for early community members
- Use community as beta testing pool

### Strategy 5: Influencer Seeding

For DTC brands and consumer products, micro-influencers (10k-50k followers) deliver the best ROI.

**Outreach Framework:**
- Identify 50+ micro-influencers in your niche
- Send personalized outreach (not template spam)
- Offer free product + affiliate commission (not just free product)
- Ask for honest review, not scripted promotion
- Time influencer posts to coincide with launch day for maximum impact

## 8-Week Audience Building Timeline

| Week | Focus | Target |
|------|-------|--------|
| 1-2 | Build landing page, set up email automation, define lead magnet | Page live, automation tested |
| 3-4 | Launch waitlist, begin organic content, start personal outreach | 200-500 signups |
| 5-6 | Activate referral program, increase content cadence, begin influencer outreach | 1,000-2,000 signups |
| 7-8 | Launch prep emails, final push, activate all partnerships | 3,000-5,000 signups |

**If budget allows paid ads:** Start Meta/Google ads in week 3, optimize for email signups (not purchases). Target $1-5 per email signup depending on niche.
