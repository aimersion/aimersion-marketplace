# Marketing Seasonal

Help tax professionals develop and execute seasonal marketing campaigns and year-round advisory service promotions. Use this skill to build client relationships and grow tax practice revenue.

## Core Functions

### Pre-Season Campaigns (July - December)

Plan campaigns to capture early-bird clients and promote year-end tax planning:

**Early Bird Campaign (August - September)**
- Announce tax preparation availability and booking
- Offer incentive pricing for early bookings
- Create sense of urgency before peak season
- Target previous clients with seasonal email
- Highlight streamlined process and faster turnaround

**Year-End Tax Planning Campaign (October - December)**
- Promote proactive tax planning opportunities
- Offer planning consultations (paid or complimentary)
- Highlight savings opportunities (estimated payments, retirement contributions, charitable giving)
- Target businesses for Q4 planning
- Create content on recent tax law changes

**Black Friday/Cyber Monday (November)**
- Discount packages for bundled services
- Early-bird discounts for 2026 preparation
- Bundle tax prep with planning or bookkeeping
- Email promotions and social media blitz
- Emphasize limited-time nature

**Year-End Content Push (November - December)**
- Tax deduction maximization guides
- Last-minute tax planning tips
- Retirement contribution deadline reminders
- Entity structure planning for upcoming year
- Capital gains harvesting strategies

### Peak Season Campaigns (January - April)

Execute campaigns to maximize new client acquisition and service volume:

**New Year Resolution Campaign (January)**
- "Tax Health Check" or "Tax Audit" service
- New year = new client acquisition target
- Resolution marketing (financial organization, tax planning)
- Email series targeting prospects
- Website and social media focus on tax season readiness

**Early Filer Campaign (January - February)**
- Emphasize benefits of early filing (fast refunds)
- Promote filing appointments and online scheduling
- Highlight security and accuracy of early filing
- Create urgency around IRS processing times
- Target email list with deadline reminders

**Tax Season Deadline Campaigns (February - April)**
- Weekly or bi-weekly deadline reminder content
- "Don't miss the April 15 deadline" messaging
- Last-minute filing availability announcements
- Extension filing information and offers
- Content on common filing mistakes to avoid

**Referral Incentive Campaign (Peak Season)**
- Offer rewards for client referrals
- Incentivize current clients to refer friends/family
- Create simple referral process and tracking
- Recognize and reward top referrers
- Promote to existing client base

**Niche Targeting Campaigns**
- Small business owners (bookkeeping + tax prep)
- Real estate investors (1099 income focus)
- Self-employed (Schedule C optimization)
- Retired individuals (Social Security planning)
- New homeowners (first-time itemization)

### Off-Season Campaigns (May - July)

Promote advisory services and retain clients during slow period:

**Advisory Services Promotion (May - July)**
- Highlight bookkeeping and accounting services
- Promote quarterly estimated tax planning
- Offer payroll service information
- Focus on ongoing advisory relationships
- Reduce tax preparation focus

**Quarterly Tax Planning (May, August, November)**
- Quarterly estimated tax review and planning
- Mid-year tax projection services
- Adjustment of estimated payments
- Business income and expense review
- Proactive quarterly advisory touchpoints

**Summer Planning Campaigns**
- Entity structure review and optimization
- S-Corp election analysis
- Tax-efficient business structure planning
- Retirement planning and contribution review
- Investment income planning

**Client Retention and Relationship Building (Year-Round)**
- Tax tip newsletters (monthly or quarterly)
- Educational webinars and workshops
- Industry-specific tax updates
- Changes in tax law affecting clients
- Value-added content beyond tax prep

### Year-Round Advisory Promotions

Develop campaigns to build advisory client base:

**Bookkeeping Services**
- Monthly reconciliation and financial statements
- Real-time income and expense tracking
- Quarterly estimated tax preparation
- Year-end closing and audit prep
- Integration with tax preparation

**Business Accounting Services**
- Monthly profit and loss statements
- Quarterly financial reviews and analysis
- Payroll processing and compliance
- Sales tax calculation and filing
- Quarterly estimated tax management

**Payroll Services**
- Payroll processing for small businesses
- Quarterly tax return filing
- Employee withholding optimization
- Year-end W-2 and payroll tax compliance
- Reduction of client administrative burden

**Tax Planning Services**
- Quarterly estimated payment planning
- Annual tax planning consultation
- Business structure optimization analysis
- Charitable giving strategy
- Retirement contribution planning

**Retirement Planning Advisory**
- IRA contribution strategy
- Social Security planning coordination
- RMD management (age 73+)
- Qualified charitable distributions
- Estate tax planning (high net worth)
