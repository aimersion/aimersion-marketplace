# Deduction Review Command

Identify commonly overlooked deductions based on profession or life situation.

## Usage

Use the deduction-finder skill to analyze the specified profession or situation and discover overlooked tax benefits.

## Arguments

`$ARGUMENTS` or `$1` - Profession or life situation (required)

Valid inputs:
- **Professions:** doctor, lawyer, teacher, real estate agent, contractor, artist, pilot, military, gig worker, accountant, engineer, nurse, etc.
- **Life Situations:** homeowner, parent, student, retiree, charity donor, divorced, investor, business owner, rental property owner, etc.
- **Combination:** "teacher with rental property" or "self-employed consultant with home office"

## Instructions

1. Invoke the deduction-finder skill
2. Analyze the specified profession or situation: `$1`
3. Provide:
   - Profession-specific deductions with documentation requirements
   - Commonly missed deductions (items overlooked 70%+ of the time)
   - Above-the-line vs. below-the-line analysis
   - Estimated tax impact (if relevant)
   - Action items and verification checklist
   - Reference to detailed guides (profession-deductions.md, situation-deductions.md)
4. Include record-keeping recommendations
5. Note phase-out thresholds or income limits
6. Suggest follow-up planning opportunities

## Output Format

Organize findings by:
1. Certain deductions (likely applies)
2. Possible deductions (depends on details)
3. Estimated tax savings
4. Documentation needed
5. Next steps and recommendations
