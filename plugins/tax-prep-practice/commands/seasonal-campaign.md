# Seasonal Campaign Command

Build a complete seasonal marketing campaign with templates, messaging, and execution plan.

## Usage

Use the marketing-seasonal skill to develop a campaign strategy for the specified tax season period.

## Arguments

`$ARGUMENTS` or `$1` - Season or campaign type (required)

Valid inputs:
- `pre-season` or `july-december` - Early bird and year-end planning
- `peak-season` or `january-april` - Peak tax filing season
- `off-season` or `may-july` - Advisory services promotion
- `early-bird` - Early filer incentive campaign
- `new-year` - New Year resolution marketing
- `year-end-planning` - December tax planning campaign
- `quarterly` - Quarterly estimated tax/planning
- `referral` - Referral incentive program
- `appreciation` - Client appreciation event/campaign
- Specific dates: "march 2024" or "september 2024"

## Instructions

1. Invoke the marketing-seasonal skill
2. Build campaign for specified period: `$1`
3. Generate:
   - Campaign overview (goal, duration, target audience)
   - Email sequence (subject lines and messaging)
   - Social media content calendar (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram)
   - Landing page copy and headlines
   - Video content ideas
   - Blog post or webinar topics
   - Promotional offers and incentives
   - Timeline and execution plan
   - Measurement metrics and success criteria
4. Reference /references/campaign-templates.md
5. Include customization options for firm size/niche
6. Provide copy that can be directly used or adapted
7. Suggest budget allocation if applicable

## Output Format

Provide:
1. Campaign overview and positioning
2. Email templates (ready to send)
3. Social media posts (ready to schedule)
4. Landing page sections (ready to build)
5. Content calendar (with dates and timing)
6. Promotional details (offer, duration, terms)
7. Expected results and measurement approach
