Create a knowledge base article using the kb-writer skill.

**Topic / Source Material**:
$ARGUMENTS

Follow this process:

1. Identify the core topic — extract the question, problem, or feature to document.
2. Determine the best article type: How-To, Troubleshooting, FAQ, or Reference.
3. Draft the article following the structure for that type from the kb-writer skill.
4. Optimize the title for search — use customer language, be specific, and be action-oriented.
5. Add search keywords that match how customers would search for this topic.
6. Review for completeness — the article must stand alone without requiring prior context or ticket history.

Write in a clear, scannable style: short paragraphs, headers, bullet points, and specific instructions. Use the customer's language, not internal jargon.

Present the article in the structured output format from the kb-writer skill, including article type, title, search keywords, full content, source reference, and review status.
