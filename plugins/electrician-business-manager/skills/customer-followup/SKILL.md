# Customer Follow-Up

Maintain and grow customer relationships through systematic follow-up communication.

## Follow-Up Types

### Post-Job Follow-Up (1-3 days after completion)
- Thank the customer for their business
- Confirm work is performing as expected
- Remind of warranty coverage
- Request a review (Google, Yelp, NextDoor, Facebook)
- Mention referral program if applicable

### Service Agreement Renewals (30-60 days before expiration)
Draft renewal reminders for annual service plans:
- Summarize work performed during the agreement period
- Highlight issues caught early through preventive maintenance
- Present renewal pricing (include loyalty discount if applicable)
- Explain what the renewal covers
- Create urgency without being pushy

### Seasonal Maintenance Reminders
Trigger based on season and past service history:
- **Spring**: Outdoor lighting check, GFCI testing, storm prep (surge protectors, generators)
- **Summer**: AC circuit check, pool/spa electrical safety
- **Fall**: Generator service before winter, holiday lighting circuits, heating system electrical
- **Winter**: Space heater safety reminders, ice dam heat cable checks

### Annual Safety Check Reminders
For past customers (12-18 months after last service):
- Panel inspection and thermal scanning
- GFCI/AFCI testing
- Smoke/CO detector battery and replacement schedule
- Surge protection status
- Outlet and switch condition

### Referral Follow-Up
After receiving a referral:
- Thank the referrer
- Update them on the outcome (without sharing private details)
- Deliver referral reward if applicable

## Communication Channels
- **Email** — primary for formal follow-ups, renewals, and detailed information
- **Text/SMS** — appointment reminders, quick check-ins, review requests
- **Phone** — high-value customers, complex renewals, complaint resolution

## Tone Guidelines
- Professional but warm — you are a trusted advisor for their home's electrical system
- Educational — include a useful tip in every communication
- Non-pushy — offer services, do not pressure
- Personalized — reference their specific system and past work

## Output Formats
- **Individual follow-up email/text** — single customer communication
- **Batch campaign** — template for sending to multiple customers
- **Renewal letter** — formal service agreement renewal document
- **Customer touchpoint calendar** — annual plan of when to reach out to each customer segment
