# Marketing Generator

Create targeted marketing campaigns for electrical contracting services.

## Seasonal Campaign Calendar

### Spring (March-May)
- **Storm season prep**: Whole-house surge protectors, generator installs
- **Outdoor living**: Landscape lighting, patio outlets, outdoor kitchen circuits
- **Spring cleaning electrical**: Panel inspections, safety audits
- **New construction season**: Builder partnerships, spec home packages

### Summer (June-August)
- **EV charger season**: Level 2 home charger installations (align with new car buying)
- **Pool/spa electrical**: Safety inspections, new installations
- **Smart home upgrades**: Smart switches, smart panels, home automation
- **AC circuit upgrades**: Dedicated circuits for new units, mini-split installations

### Fall (September-November)
- **Generator season**: Backup power before winter storms
- **Holiday lighting**: Permanent LED installations, dedicated circuits
- **Home safety month**: Smoke detector upgrades, GFCI/AFCI updates
- **Panel upgrades**: Pre-winter system readiness

### Winter (December-February)
- **Space heater safety**: Educational content + panel upgrade leads
- **New year upgrades**: "New year, new electrical" panel modernization
- **Tax season**: Business electrical upgrades before year-end
- **Builder off-season**: Lock in contractor rates for spring projects

## Campaign Components

For each campaign, generate:

1. **Headline and hook** — attention-grabbing lead for the service
2. **Educational content** — why the customer needs this (safety, savings, convenience)
3. **Offer/CTA** — specific promotion or call-to-action
4. **Social media posts** — 3-5 platform-specific posts (Facebook, NextDoor, Instagram)
5. **Email content** — for existing customer database
6. **Google/Facebook ad copy** — paid advertising text
7. **Landing page content** — if web presence exists

## High-Value Service Promotions

### Panel Upgrade Campaign
- Target: Homes 20+ years old, Federal Pacific or Zinsco panels, frequent breaker trips
- Hook: Safety + insurance + home value angles
- Offer: Free panel inspection, financing options

### EV Charger Campaign
- Target: EV owners and prospective buyers, eco-conscious homeowners
- Hook: Convenience of home charging, cost savings vs. public charging
- Offer: Turnkey installation quote, rebate/incentive information

### Generator Campaign
- Target: Homeowners in storm-prone areas, remote workers, medical equipment users
- Hook: Never lose power again, protect your family and work
- Offer: Free site assessment, financing options

### Whole-Home Safety Audit
- Target: New homeowners, homes 10+ years old, families with children
- Hook: Peace of mind, prevention vs. emergency repair costs
- Offer: Discounted comprehensive electrical inspection

## Content Tone
- Expert and trustworthy — position as the knowledgeable local electrician
- Safety-focused — lead with protecting family and home
- Educational — teach customers why electrical work matters
- Community-oriented — local business supporting local families
