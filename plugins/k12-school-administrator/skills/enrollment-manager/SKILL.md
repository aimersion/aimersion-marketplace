# Enrollment Manager

Design enrollment campaigns, open house events, registration materials, and retention strategies for K-12 schools — public, charter, magnet, and private.

## Enrollment Campaign Design

### Campaign Timeline
- **6-8 months before**: Brand positioning, website refresh, social media presence
- **4-6 months before**: Open house events, campus tours, community partnerships
- **2-4 months before**: Registration push, information sessions, application deadlines
- **1-2 months before**: Follow-up calls, waitlist management, new family orientation
- **Post-enrollment**: Welcome packets, summer bridge programs, family engagement events

### Messaging Framework
Build campaign messaging around four pillars:
1. **Academic Excellence** — Test scores, college readiness, program highlights
2. **Whole Child** — Arts, athletics, social-emotional learning, extracurriculars
3. **Community** — School culture, family involvement, diversity, belonging
4. **Outcomes** — Graduation rates, college acceptance, career readiness, alumni stories

### Channel Strategy
- **Digital**: School website, social media (Facebook, Instagram), Google ads, email campaigns
- **Community**: Flyers at libraries/community centers, faith organizations, local businesses
- **Events**: Open houses, shadow days, curriculum nights, community fairs
- **Referral**: Current family ambassador programs, word-of-mouth incentives
- **Media**: Local newspaper features, radio spots, community TV

## Open House Planning

### Event Structure (90-120 minutes)
1. **Welcome & Overview** (15 min) — Principal address, school mission, quick highlights
2. **Campus Tours** (30 min) — Student-led tours hitting classrooms, labs, library, gym
3. **Program Showcases** (30 min) — Stations for academics, arts, athletics, special programs
4. **Q&A Panel** (15 min) — Current parents, teachers, and students answer questions
5. **Registration Support** (ongoing) — Staff available to help with applications on-site

### Logistics Checklist
- Signage and wayfinding throughout campus
- Student greeters and tour guides (trained in advance)
- Childcare room for younger siblings
- Bilingual staff or interpreters available
- Refreshments and school spirit swag
- Registration forms (paper and digital) ready
- Follow-up contact collection (name, email, phone, grade interest)

## Retention Strategies

### Early Warning System
Monitor these indicators for potential attrition:
- Chronic absenteeism (>10% of school days)
- Declining grades or sudden behavior changes
- Low family engagement or missed conferences
- Transportation or financial hardship signals
- New family isolation (no connections after 60 days)

### Retention Actions
- **Personal outreach**: Principal calls to at-risk families within 48 hours
- **Mentorship**: Pair disengaged students with staff mentors
- **Exit interviews**: Conduct with every departing family; track patterns
- **Re-enrollment reminders**: Begin 4 months before deadline with multi-touch campaign
- **Satisfaction surveys**: Bi-annual surveys to surface concerns before they become exits

## Registration Materials

### Enrollment Packet Contents
- Welcome letter from the principal
- School overview brochure (programs, achievements, culture)
- Registration forms (demographics, emergency contacts, health, transportation)
- Academic calendar and daily schedule
- Uniform/dress code information
- Before/after school care options
- Free and reduced lunch application
- Technology acceptable use policy
- Parent/student handbook acknowledgment
