Design an enrollment or re-enrollment campaign for a K-12 school using the enrollment-manager skill for strategy, messaging, and event planning guidance.

Determine the campaign type:
1. **New student recruitment** — Attract families to the school
2. **Re-enrollment drive** — Retain current families for next year
3. **Open house planning** — Plan and promote a campus visit event
4. **Registration push** — Drive completion of enrollment paperwork
5. **Retention intervention** — Re-engage at-risk families

Ask the user for:
- School name, type (public, charter, magnet, private), and grade levels
- Target audience and geographic area
- Key differentiators and selling points
- Timeline and important deadlines
- Available channels (email, social media, flyers, events)
- Budget constraints if any

Deliver a complete campaign plan including: messaging framework with key talking points, channel strategy with specific content for each channel, event logistics (if applicable), timeline with milestones, and follow-up sequences. Use the four messaging pillars from the enrollment-manager skill.

Save as `enrollment-campaign-[type].md`
