# Marketing Planner

Create comprehensive marketing plans for car dealership sales events, social content calendars, and email campaigns.

## Sales Event Planning Framework

### Event Types and Timing

| Event | Best Timing | Duration | Focus |
|-------|------------|----------|-------|
| Presidents' Day Sale | February (3rd Monday weekend) | 4-5 days | New inventory clearance, winter deals |
| Tax Refund Event | February-April | Ongoing | Pre-owned, entry-level pricing |
| Memorial Day Sale | May (last Monday weekend) | 4-5 days | Summer vehicle push, convertibles, trucks |
| 4th of July Blowout | June 28-July 7 | 7-10 days | Mid-year clearance, patriotic theme |
| Labor Day Sale | September (1st Monday weekend) | 4-5 days | Model year-end clearance |
| Black Friday / Cyber Week | November | 7-10 days | Biggest deals of the year positioning |
| Year-End Clearance | December | All month | Remaining current-year inventory |
| New Year / New Car | January | 2-3 weeks | Fresh start messaging, new model push |

### Event Planning Checklist

**6 Weeks Before:**
- Define event theme, dates, and budget
- Identify inventory to feature (aged units, high-margin units)
- Set sales targets and spiff structure
- Begin creative development

**4 Weeks Before:**
- Launch digital advertising (Facebook, Google, Instagram)
- Email campaign to database (save-the-date)
- Update website with event landing page
- Order event materials (balloons, banners, tent if outdoor)

**2 Weeks Before:**
- Intensify social media content (countdown posts, vehicle spotlights)
- Send targeted emails to warm leads and orphan owners
- Confirm staffing and extended hours
- BDC appointment push — target event dates

**Event Week:**
- Daily social posts (live from the event, customer deliveries)
- Real-time inventory updates on social
- BDC follows up on all no-shows and recent leads
- Daily sales huddle to adjust strategy

**Post-Event:**
- Thank-you emails to all attendees
- Follow up unsold appointments within 24 hours
- Social media recap with customer photos (with permission)
- ROI analysis: ad spend vs. units sold, gross profit, cost per sale

## Social Media Content Strategy

### Content Mix (80/20 Rule)
- **80% value content**: Vehicle features, buyer tips, community involvement, behind-the-scenes, customer stories
- **20% promotional**: Sales events, specific deals, inventory spotlights

### Platform-Specific Guidelines

**Facebook**: Longer posts, event creation, community engagement, video walkarounds
**Instagram**: High-quality vehicle photos, Stories for behind-the-scenes, Reels for quick tips
**TikTok**: Fun, authentic content — day-in-the-life, reaction videos, vehicle reveals
**Google Business Profile**: Regular posts, photo updates, respond to every review

### Weekly Content Calendar Template

| Day | Content Type | Example |
|-----|-------------|---------|
| Monday | New arrival spotlight | "Just landed! 2024 Honda CR-V with only 12K miles" |
| Tuesday | Customer delivery / testimonial | "Congratulations to the Smith family on their new Tahoe!" |
| Wednesday | Educational / tips | "5 things to check before your summer road trip" |
| Thursday | Behind-the-scenes / team | "Meet our service team — 85 years combined experience" |
| Friday | Weekend special / inventory | "Weekend deal: This F-150 won't last — come see it Saturday" |
| Saturday | Live content from showroom | Real-time Stories/Reels of activity |

## Email Campaign Framework

### Database Segmentation
- **Recent buyers (0-12 months)**: Service reminders, referral requests, satisfaction check-ins
- **Owners (1-3 years)**: Maintenance upsells, trade-in equity alerts, loyalty offers
- **Mature owners (3+ years)**: Trade-up campaigns, new model announcements, equity mining
- **Unconverted leads**: Inventory updates, price drops, seasonal events
- **Service customers**: Service specials, maintenance due, recall notices

### Email Metrics Benchmarks (Automotive)
- **Open rate**: 18-25% (aim for 22%+)
- **Click-through rate**: 2-4%
- **Unsubscribe rate**: Under 0.5%
- **Conversion (appointment set)**: 1-2% of sends
