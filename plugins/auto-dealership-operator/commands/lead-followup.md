---
description: Create lead follow-up sequences and scripts
argument-hint: [lead type or specific lead details]
---

Create a complete lead follow-up plan based on the lead information provided.

Ask the user for:
- Lead source (website, phone, walk-in, third-party like AutoTrader/CarGurus, social media)
- Vehicle of interest (if known)
- Customer name and any known details
- Current status (new lead, contacted but no response, appointment set, etc.)

Based on the input, generate:

1. **Immediate response** — Draft the first outreach message (email, text, or phone script depending on lead source and channel preference)
2. **Follow-up sequence** — A complete day-by-day follow-up cadence with specific messages for each touchpoint (texts, emails, and call scripts)
3. **Objection handling** — Prepare responses for the 3 most likely objections based on the lead type
4. **Appointment setting script** — Phone script to convert the lead into a confirmed showroom appointment

Use the lead-nurture skill for cadence timing, tone guidelines, and template structures. Tailor all messaging to the specific vehicle and customer situation — never use generic filler.

Format each message as ready-to-send (the user should be able to copy-paste into their CRM or phone).
