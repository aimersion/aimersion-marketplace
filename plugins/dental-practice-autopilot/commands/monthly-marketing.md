Generate a monthly marketing plan for the dental practice. Month or theme: $ARGUMENTS

## Process

1. **Context Gathering** — Ask the user (skip what's already provided):
   - Practice name and location
   - Target demographic (families, cosmetic, implants, pediatric, general)
   - Any special offers or promotions this month
   - Seasonal relevance (back-to-school, holiday season, summer, new year)
   - Channels they use (social media, email, direct mail, website blog, local ads)
   - Budget range (if applicable)
   - Services they want to highlight

2. **Monthly Theme** — Establish a cohesive theme that ties the month's content together:
   - Connect to a seasonal hook, national health observance, or practice milestone
   - Examples: "National Dental Hygiene Month" (October), "Give the Gift of a Smile" (December), "New Year, New Smile" (January), "Sports Safety Month" (April)

3. **Content Calendar** — Generate a week-by-week plan:

   **Week 1:** Educational content (blog post + social posts about a common dental topic)
   **Week 2:** Promotion/offer content (highlight a specific service with special pricing or incentive)
   **Week 3:** Social proof (patient testimonial spotlight, before/after, team highlight)
   **Week 4:** Community/fun content (team spotlight, local event, dental myth-busting, fun facts)

   For each week, provide:
   - 3 social media posts (Facebook/Instagram) with caption text and image suggestions
   - 1 blog post topic with title, outline, and SEO keywords
   - 1 email newsletter section
   - Relevant hashtags

4. **Seasonal Campaign** — Design one featured campaign for the month:
   - Campaign name and tagline
   - Offer details (if applicable)
   - Target audience
   - Channels and creative assets needed
   - Call to action
   - Tracking method (unique phone number, promo code, landing page)

5. **New Patient Acquisition Ideas** — Include at least 2 tactics:
   - Google Business Profile optimization tips
   - Local SEO suggestions
   - Referral program promotion
   - Community partnership ideas (schools, gyms, local businesses)
   - Special offer for new patients

6. **Email Newsletter Draft** — Produce a complete monthly newsletter with:
   - Subject line (3 options)
   - Header/hero section
   - Featured article
   - Monthly special or promotion
   - Team spotlight or fun section
   - Call to action (book appointment)
   - Footer with contact info and social links

Tone: friendly, approachable, community-focused. Dental marketing should make people feel good about their smile, not anxious about their teeth.
