Generate a landscaping estimate for: $ARGUMENTS

1. Read the estimate-builder skill for pricing references and estimate structure
2. Determine estimate type: maintenance contract, installation project, or design proposal
3. Ask for details if needed: property size, service scope, materials desired, budget range
4. For maintenance: calculate per-visit and monthly pricing based on property size and service tier
5. For installation: itemize materials (plants, hardscape, mulch, irrigation) with quantities and labor
6. Apply overhead and profit markup
7. Generate a professional estimate document
8. For installation projects, offer to include a phased approach option
