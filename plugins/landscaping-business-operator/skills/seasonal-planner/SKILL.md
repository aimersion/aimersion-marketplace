# Seasonal Planner

Build year-round service calendars tailored to climate zones with upsell opportunities at every season.

## Climate Zone Framework

### Cool-Season Zones (Northern US — Zones 3-6)
Grass types: Kentucky Bluegrass, Fescue, Perennial Ryegrass

### Transition Zone (Mid-Atlantic, Upper South — Zones 6-7)
Mixed: both cool and warm season grasses

### Warm-Season Zones (Southern US — Zones 7-10)
Grass types: Bermuda, Zoysia, St. Augustine, Centipede, Bahia

## Monthly Service Calendar — Cool-Season (Northern)

### March
- **Core services**: Spring cleanup, debris removal, first assessment
- **Upsell**: Mulch delivery and installation, early pre-emergent
- **Marketing**: "Spring is coming" campaign, renewal outreach

### April
- **Core services**: First mow, edging, pre-emergent herbicide
- **Upsell**: Aeration and overseeding, lawn renovation for thin areas
- **Marketing**: "New customer" spring specials

### May
- **Core services**: Weekly mowing begins, bed maintenance, trimming
- **Upsell**: Planting (annuals, perennials), irrigation startup
- **Marketing**: Mother's Day garden packages

### June
- **Core services**: Mowing, edging, trimming, bed weeding
- **Upsell**: Grub prevention application, landscape lighting
- **Marketing**: Summer maintenance packages

### July
- **Core services**: Mowing (raise height in heat), watering management
- **Upsell**: Mosquito/pest treatment, drought management
- **Marketing**: "Beat the heat" lawn care tips

### August
- **Core services**: Mowing, prepare for fall renovation
- **Upsell**: Fall aeration/overseeding booking (upsell NOW for September)
- **Marketing**: "Book fall services early" campaign

### September
- **Core services**: Aeration, overseeding, fall fertilization
- **Upsell**: Fall planting (trees, shrubs, bulbs), landscape projects
- **Marketing**: Fall color and curb appeal focus

### October
- **Core services**: Final fertilization, leaf removal begins, bed prep for winter
- **Upsell**: Leaf removal packages, fall cleanup bundles
- **Marketing**: Winterization and snow removal contracts

### November
- **Core services**: Final leaf removal, equipment winterization, bed cleanup
- **Upsell**: Holiday lighting installation, snow removal contracts
- **Marketing**: Thank you/referral campaign for the year

### December-February
- **Core services**: Snow and ice management (if offered), equipment maintenance
- **Upsell**: Off-season landscape design, hardscape planning for spring
- **Marketing**: Renewal outreach for next season, early-bird pricing

## Upsell Timing Strategy

| Upsell Service | Best Time to Propose | Why |
|----------------|---------------------|-----|
| Mulch | February-March | Before spring growth |
| Aeration/overseeding | July-August | Book for September execution |
| Fall cleanup | September | Before leaves peak |
| Holiday lighting | October | Before the rush |
| Snow removal | October-November | Before first snow |
| Irrigation install | March-April | Before summer heat |
| Landscape design | December-February | Off-season planning |
| Hardscaping | February-March | Schedule for spring build |
| Fertilization program | February-March | Annual program enrollment |
| Tree/shrub care | Year-round | After diagnosing issues on-site |

## Service Pricing Tiers

### Basic Maintenance
- Weekly mow, edge, blow
- Monthly pricing: base rate per property

### Standard Maintenance
- Weekly mow, edge, blow
- Bi-weekly trimming and bed weeding
- Monthly pricing: 1.5-2x basic

### Premium Maintenance
- Weekly mow, edge, blow
- Weekly trimming, bed maintenance, and weed control
- Seasonal fertilization program
- Seasonal color rotation (annuals)
- Monthly pricing: 2.5-3.5x basic

## Output Formats
- **Annual service calendar** — month-by-month tasks and upsell opportunities
- **Customer proposal** — seasonal maintenance agreement
- **Crew training calendar** — what skills are needed each season
- **Marketing calendar** — promotional campaigns aligned to seasonal services
