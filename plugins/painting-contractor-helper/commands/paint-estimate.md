Generate a painting estimate for: $ARGUMENTS

1. Read the estimate-calculator skill for pricing methods and production rates
2. Ask clarifying questions if needed: interior/exterior, number of rooms or SF, surface condition, paint quality desired
3. Calculate square footage for all surfaces (walls, ceilings, trim)
4. Determine prep requirements based on surface condition
5. Calculate paint quantities (gallons by color/surface, including waste)
6. Calculate labor hours by task (prep, prime, paint, trim, cleanup)
7. Price materials (paint, primer, tape, supplies)
8. Apply labor rates and overhead/profit markup
9. Generate a detailed estimate and a material shopping list
10. Offer to create a professional customer proposal
