Provide color recommendations for: $ARGUMENTS

1. Read the color-consultant skill for consultation methodology and room guidelines
2. Ask the client discovery questions: room function, desired mood, existing elements, light direction, personal preferences
3. Search for current color trends and popular palettes from major paint brands
4. Recommend a coordinated palette of 3-5 colors (primary wall, accent, trim, ceiling)
5. Explain the reasoning behind each recommendation
6. Note how light direction and room size affect color perception
7. Advise on sampling strategy (large swatches on different walls, view at multiple times of day)
8. Provide specific paint brand color names/codes if a preferred brand is indicated
