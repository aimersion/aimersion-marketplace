Plan painting crew schedule.

1. Read the crew-scheduler skill for scheduling methodology and production standards
2. Ask for: number of crews, pending jobs with scope and deadlines, any priority projects
3. Check weather forecast if exterior work is planned
4. Apply weather rules for exterior painting (temperature, humidity, rain)
5. Sequence jobs logically: prep before paint, interior during bad weather, exterior during good weather
6. Assign crews based on job requirements and skill levels
7. Generate a weekly schedule with daily assignments per crew
8. Flag any scheduling conflicts, weather risks, or capacity issues
