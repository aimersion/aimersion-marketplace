# Customer Experience

Deliver exceptional customer experience at every touchpoint of a painting project.

## Customer Journey Touchpoints

### 1. Pre-Job Preparation (2-3 days before start)
Send the client:
- **Start date and time confirmation**
- **Crew lead name and contact**
- **Preparation instructions** — what the homeowner should do:
  - Remove small items from walls (photos, shelves, decorations)
  - Move small furniture away from walls or to center of room
  - Secure pets in a safe area away from work zones
  - Clear pathways for crew access
  - Note any areas of concern (cracks, stains, damage)
  - Provide parking guidance for crew vehicles
- **What to expect** — noise, odors, timeline, daily hours
- **Color confirmation** — final color selections verified

### 2. Day-of Communication
- **Morning text/call** — "Our crew is on the way! [Crew lead] will arrive at [time]."
- **Crew introduction** — crew lead introduces themselves and reviews the plan
- **Daily end-of-day update** — what was completed, what is next

### 3. Post-Job Follow-Up (1-2 days after completion)
- **Thank you message** — appreciation for their business
- **Quality check** — "Is everything looking great? Any touch-ups needed?"
- **Care instructions** — how to maintain their new paint job:
  - Wait 30 days before washing walls
  - Clean with soft cloth and mild soap
  - Touch-up paint will be left on-site (with color names noted)
  - Keep touch-up paint stored properly (cool, sealed, labeled)
- **Warranty information** — what is covered and for how long

### 4. Review Request (5-7 days after completion)
- **Timing** — after confirming they are happy, not before
- **Make it easy** — direct link to Google review page
- **Personalize** — reference their specific project
- **Gratitude** — thank them regardless of whether they leave a review

### 5. Long-Term Follow-Up (6-12 months)
- **Annual check-in** — how is the paint holding up?
- **Referral request** — "Know anyone else who could use our services?"
- **Seasonal offers** — spring exterior prep, holiday interior refresh
- **Maintenance tips** — seasonal paint care advice

## Review Request Template

Subject: How did we do?

Hi [Client Name],

Thank you again for choosing [Company Name] for your [project type]. We loved working on your [specific detail — kitchen, exterior, etc.]!

If you are happy with the results, we would be incredibly grateful if you could share your experience with a quick Google review. It helps other homeowners find quality painting services.

[Direct Google Review Link]

It only takes a minute and means the world to our small business.

Thank you!
[Your Name]

## Handling Callbacks

When a customer calls with a concern:
1. **Listen fully** — let them explain without interrupting
2. **Acknowledge** — "I understand your concern, and I want to make this right."
3. **Schedule quickly** — get someone out within 24-48 hours
4. **Over-deliver** — fix the issue plus check for anything else
5. **Follow up** — confirm satisfaction after the callback visit

## Output Formats
- **Pre-job email/text** — preparation instructions customized to the project
- **Post-job thank you** — with care instructions and warranty info
- **Review request** — personalized with direct review link
- **Follow-up campaign** — series of touchpoint communications
