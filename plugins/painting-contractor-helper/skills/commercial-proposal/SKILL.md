# Commercial Proposal

Create professional commercial painting proposals that win bids.

## Proposal Structure

### Cover Page
- Company name, logo, and contact information
- Project name and address
- Property owner/manager name
- Proposal date and validity period (typically 30-60 days)
- Proposal number

### Executive Summary
- Brief project description (1-2 paragraphs)
- Proposed approach and timeline
- Total investment range or fixed price
- Key differentiators (why choose this company)

### Scope of Work
Detailed description of all included work:
- **Surface preparation** — washing, scraping, sanding, patching, priming
- **Painting specifications** — number of coats, application method, paint brand and product
- **Areas included** — itemized list of surfaces to be painted
- **Areas excluded** — clearly state what is NOT included
- **Color specification** — identified or TBD with client

### Material Specifications
- Paint manufacturer and product line
- Sheen selections by surface (flat, eggshell, satin, semi-gloss)
- Primer specifications
- Quality standards and warranty information for products used

### Pricing
Options for presenting pricing:
- **Lump sum** — single total price for the project
- **Unit pricing** — per SF, per unit, per building
- **Phased pricing** — if project will be done in stages
- **Good/Better/Best** — tiered options with different material grades

### Timeline and Schedule
- Proposed start date (or lead time from contract signing)
- Estimated duration
- Phase schedule for multi-phase projects
- Weather contingency plan for exterior work

### Company Qualifications
- Years in business
- Relevant project experience (similar scope/type)
- Insurance coverage (GL, WC, auto — offer to provide certificates)
- References (3-5 relevant to this project type)
- Licenses and certifications
- Safety record and training programs

### Terms and Conditions
- Payment terms (deposit, progress payments, final payment)
- Change order process
- Warranty (workmanship — typically 2-5 years)
- Insurance certificates available upon request
- Cancellation policy

### Signature Page
- Acceptance lines for both parties
- Date fields
- Payment authorization

## Commercial Market Segments

### Property Management Companies
- Emphasize: reliability, minimal tenant disruption, recurring service capability
- Pricing: per-unit or per-SF for easy comparison
- Include: maintenance painting programs, annual touch-up plans
- References: other property management clients

### HOAs and Condominiums
- Emphasize: color consistency, architectural compliance, phased approach
- Pricing: per-building or per-phase
- Include: color rendering/visualization, HOA board presentation support
- Address: resident communication plan, parking/access logistics

### Retail and Office
- Emphasize: minimal business disruption, after-hours work capability
- Pricing: per-SF or lump sum
- Include: timeline that minimizes impact on operations
- Address: VOC concerns, ventilation, business hour restrictions

### Industrial and Warehouse
- Emphasize: safety compliance, speed, durability of coatings
- Pricing: per-SF with options for coating systems
- Include: surface preparation specifications, SSPC standards if applicable

## Output Formats
- **Full commercial proposal** — professional multi-page document (.docx or .pdf)
- **Bid comparison sheet** — simplified format for bid submission
- **Presentation deck** — for HOA board or property management meetings
- **Maintenance program proposal** — ongoing service agreement document
