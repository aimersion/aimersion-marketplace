Use the competitor-tracker skill to create a competitive intelligence briefing.

1. If $ARGUMENTS specify a competitor, focus on that competitor. Otherwise, ask the user which competitors or product category to analyze.

2. Search for recent competitive activity:
   - Recent FDA clearances/approvals in the product category
   - Published clinical studies in the last 6 months
   - Company press releases and product announcements
   - Industry news and conference presentations

3. Generate a competitive intelligence briefing with:
   - Summary of recent competitive activity
   - Impact assessment for the user's territory
   - Updated battlecard for the primary competitor
   - Recommended response strategies
   - Talking points for surgeon conversations about competitive products

4. Flag any urgent competitive threats that need immediate action.

5. Save the briefing to a document.

Keep all competitive information factual and compliant — compare features and data, never disparage competitors.
