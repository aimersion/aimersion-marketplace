Use the territory-planner skill to create a comprehensive territory plan.

1. Ask the user for:
   - List of accounts in their territory (or read from a provided file)
   - Current quota and revenue targets
   - Key competitive dynamics in the territory
   - Any strategic priorities from management

2. Score and tier every account (A/B/C/D) using the weighted prioritization framework.

3. Generate a territory plan document with:
   - Account tiering table with scores and rationale
   - Recommended visit cadence per tier
   - Time allocation breakdown (% of time per tier)
   - Monthly revenue targets by account that roll up to quota
   - Top 5 "must-win" accounts with specific strategies
   - Quarterly milestones and checkpoints

4. Create a weekly route template that clusters accounts geographically.

5. Save the territory plan to a professional document.

If $ARGUMENTS specify a quarter or focus area, tailor the plan accordingly.
