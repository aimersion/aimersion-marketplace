# Territory Planner

Organize, prioritize, and plan medical device sales territories for maximum revenue impact and efficient coverage.

## Account Mapping

Help reps build a comprehensive territory picture:

### Account Classification (Tiering)
| Tier | Criteria | Visit Cadence | Time Allocation |
|------|----------|---------------|-----------------|
| A | Top revenue, high growth potential, key opinion leaders | Weekly | 40% |
| B | Moderate revenue, growth opportunity, engaged surgeons | Biweekly | 30% |
| C | Low current revenue, conversion potential | Monthly | 20% |
| D | Minimal opportunity, maintain relationship | Quarterly | 10% |

### Account Data Points
For each account, gather:
- Hospital/ASC name, address, and key contacts
- Current device usage (brand, volume, contract status)
- Procedure volumes by type (from public data or rep knowledge)
- Decision-makers: surgeons, OR directors, materials managers, VAC committee members
- Competitive landscape: which competitors are entrenched, relationship strength
- Contract expiration dates and GPO affiliations

## Prioritization Framework

Score accounts using weighted criteria:

1. **Revenue potential** (30%) — Procedure volume × average selling price × realistic capture rate
2. **Win probability** (25%) — Relationship strength, competitive position, contract timing
3. **Strategic value** (20%) — Key opinion leader influence, teaching hospital, reference site potential
4. **Growth trajectory** (15%) — Facility expansion, new surgeons joining, procedure volume trends
5. **Competitive vulnerability** (10%) — Competitor service gaps, expiring contracts, surgeon dissatisfaction

## Route Planning

Optimize weekly/monthly travel:
- Cluster accounts by geography for efficient day routes
- Pair high-priority visits with nearby lower-tier accounts
- Build in OR observation time (morning) and office meetings (afternoon)
- Account for case coverage requirements and case start times
- Track windshield time vs. face time ratio (target: 60%+ face time)

## Quota Planning

Help break annual quota into actionable targets:
- Monthly and quarterly revenue milestones
- Account-level revenue targets that roll up to quota
- New account conversion targets vs. existing account growth
- Product mix targets if applicable
- Identify the "must-win" accounts that make or break the year
